Psalms Chapter 136

1 Oh, give thanks to the LORD, for He is good; His steadfast love endures forever.

2 Give thanks to the supreme God, for his steadfast love endures forever.

3 Offer thanks to the supreme Lord of all lords, for His steadfast love endures forever.

4 To the One who alone performs extraordinary miracles: for His steadfast love endures forever.

5 To the One who, through His wisdom, created the heavens: His steadfast love endures forever.

6 To the One who spread out the earth over the waters, for His steadfast love endures forever.

7 To the One who created the great lights—for His steadfast love endures forever.

8 The sun governs the day, for his steadfast love endures forever.

9 The moon and the stars to govern the night: for his steadfast love endures forever.

10 To the one who struck down Egypt's firstborn, for his steadfast love endures forever.

11 And He led Israel out from among them, for His steadfast love endures forever.

12 With great power and an outstretched arm, for His steadfast love endures forever.

13 To the One who split the Red Sea into separate parts, for His steadfast love endures forever.

14 And He brought Israel safely through the midst of it, for His steadfast love endures forever.

15 But He overthrew Pharaoh and his army in the Red Sea, for His steadfast love endures forever.

16 To the one who guided his people through the desert: for his steadfast love endures forever.

17 To the one who struck down mighty kings—for his steadfast love endures forever.

18 And he struck down mighty kings, for his steadfast love endures forever.

19 Sihon, king of the Amorites—for his steadfast love endures forever.

20 And Og, the king of Bashan, [give thanks to the Lord], for his steadfast love endures forever.

21 And He gave their land as an inheritance, for His steadfast love endures forever.

22 It is an inheritance for Israel, His servant, for His steadfast love endures forever.

23 He took notice of us in our humble condition, for his steadfast love endures forever.

24 He has delivered us from our enemies, for his steadfast love endures forever.

25 He provides food for all living creatures, for his steadfast love endures forever.

26 Oh, give thanks to the God of heaven, for His steadfast love endures forever.
