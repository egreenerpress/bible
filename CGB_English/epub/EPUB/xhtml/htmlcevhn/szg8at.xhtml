Galatians Chapter 6

1 Brothers and sisters, if someone is caught in a wrongdoing, you who are guided by the Spirit should gently restore that person. Do so with humility, keeping watch over yourself, so that you are not also tempted.

2 Carry each other's burdens, and in this way you will fulfill the law of Christ.

3 If someone considers themselves to be important when they are not, they are only deceiving themselves.

4 Let each person examine their own actions, and then they will find reason to take pride in themselves alone, without comparing themselves to others.

5 Each person must carry their own responsibility.

6 Let those who are instructed in the word share all good things with the one who teaches them.

7 Do not be misled; God cannot be mocked or treated with contempt. Whatever a person sows, that is what they will also reap.

8 For whoever invests in satisfying their selfish desires will harvest destruction from those desires; but whoever invests in living by the Spirit will harvest eternal life from the Spirit.

9 Let us not grow tired of doing what is good, for at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up.

10 Therefore, whenever we have the chance, let us do good to everyone, and especially to those who belong to the family of believers.

11 You can see how lengthy this letter is that I have written to you with my own hand.

12 Those who seek to make a good outward impression in the flesh are pressuring you to be circumcisedâ€”but only to avoid being persecuted for the sake of the cross of Christ.

13 For those who are circumcised do not themselves keep the law; yet they want you to be circumcised so that they may boast about your physical condition.

14 Far be it from me to boast in anything except the cross of our Lord Jesus Christ. Through Him, the world has been put to death as far as I am concerned, and I have been put to death as far as the world is concerned.

15 In Christ Jesus, neither being circumcised nor being uncircumcised has any value. What truly matters is becoming a new creation.

16 And to all who live by this principle, may peace and mercy be upon them, and also upon the true Israel of God.

17 From now on, let no one cause me distress, for I carry in my body the scars that testify to my allegiance to the Lord Jesus.

18 Brothers and sisters, may the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit. Amen.
