Psalms Chapter 118

1 Oh, give thanks to the LORD, for He is good; His steadfast love endures forever.

2 Let Israel now declare, "His steadfast love endures forever."

3 Let the house of Aaron now declare, "His steadfast love endures forever."

4 Let all who revere the LORD declare, "His steadfast love endures forever."

5 I cried out to the LORD in my trouble, and the LORD answered me, bringing me into a spacious and secure place.

6 The LORD stands with me; I will not be afraid. What can mere humans do to me?

7 The LORD stands with me, supported by those who come to my aid; therefore, I will witness the fulfillment of my desires against those who hate me.

8 It is more reliable to place your trust in the LORD than to depend on human beings.

9 It is more reliable to place your trust in the LORD than to depend on the promises or power of human leaders.

10 All the nations surrounded me, but in the name of the LORD I will defeat them.

11 They surrounded me on every side; yes, they closed in around me. But in the name of the LORD, I will overcome them.

12 They surrounded me like a swarm of bees, but they were extinguished as quickly as a fire of thorns. For in the name of the LORD, I will utterly defeat them.

13 You have attacked me fiercely, seeking to make me fall, but the LORD has come to my aid.

14 The LORD is my source of strength and my reason for singing; He has become my deliverance.

15 The sound of joy and deliverance fills the dwellings of the righteous; the mighty power of the LORD accomplishes great deeds.

16 The mighty power of the LORD is lifted high; the LORD's strength accomplishes great and courageous deeds.

17 I will not die, but I will live, and I will proclaim the mighty deeds of the LORD.

18 The LORD has disciplined me severely, but He has not handed me over to death.

19 Open for me the gates of righteousness; I will enter through them and give thanks to the LORD.

20 This is the gate of the LORD; the righteous will enter through it.

21 I will give you praise, for you have listened to me and have become my deliverance.

22 The stone that the builders rejected has become the cornerstone.

23 This is the work of the LORD; it is astonishing in our sight.

24 This is the day that the LORD has created; let us rejoice and be glad in it.

25 Please, LORD, save us now! LORD, we earnestly ask you, bring success and blessing in this moment!

26 Blessed is the one who comes in the name of the LORD; we bless you from the house of the LORD.

27 The Lord is God, who has shown us His light. Secure the offering with cords, bringing it to the horns of the altar.

28 You are my God, and I will give you praise; you are my God, and I will honor you.

29 Oh, give thanks to the LORD, for He is good; His steadfast love endures forever.
