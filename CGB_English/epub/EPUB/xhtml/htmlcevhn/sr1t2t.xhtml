Ezekiel Chapter 28

1 The word of the LORD came to me once more, saying,

2 "Son of man, speak to the ruler of Tyre and tell him this: This is what the Lord GOD says: Because your heart has become proud, and you have declared, ‘I am a god; I sit on the throne of a god, in the heart of the seas,’ yet you are only a mortal, not a god, though you imagine yourself to have the mind of a god."

3 Look, you are wiser than Daniel; no mystery is hidden from you.

4 By your wisdom and understanding, you have acquired wealth for yourself, gathering gold and silver into your treasuries.

5 Through your vast wisdom and your trading, you have greatly increased your wealth, and your heart has become proud because of your riches.

6 Therefore, this is what the Lord GOD says: Because you have made your heart like the heart of God—

7 Therefore, I will bring foreigners against you, the most ruthless of nations, and they will draw their swords against the splendor of your wisdom. They will defile your radiance.

8 They will bring you down to the grave, and you will perish like those who are killed in the heart of the sea.

9 Will you still declare before the one who kills you, "I am a god"? Yet you will be nothing more than a mortal, not a god, in the hands of the one who takes your life.

10 You will die the death of the uncircumcised, slain by the hand of foreigners, for I have declared it, says the Lord GOD.

11 Furthermore, the message of the LORD came to me, saying,

12 "Son of man, compose a lament for the king of Tyre and say to him, 'This is what the Lord GOD declares: You were the embodiment of perfection, filled with wisdom and flawless in beauty.'"

13 You were in Eden, the garden of God, adorned with every precious stone: carnelian, topaz, and diamond, beryl, onyx, and jasper, sapphire, emerald, and garnet, all set in gold. The craftsmanship of your tambourines and pipes was prepared within you on the day you were created.

14 You are the anointed guardian cherub, appointed to protect; I placed you in that role. You were on God’s holy mountain, moving freely among the fiery stones.

15 You were flawless in your ways from the day you were created until wrongdoing was discovered in you.

16 Because of the abundance of your trade, they have filled your midst with violence, and you have sinned. Therefore, I will cast you out as defiled from the mountain of God, and I will destroy you, O guardian cherub, from among the fiery stones.

17 Your heart became proud because of your beauty, and you corrupted your wisdom due to your splendor. I will cast you down to the ground; I will make you a spectacle before kings, so they may see you.

18 You have defiled your sanctuaries through the abundance of your sins, through the wrongdoing of your dishonest dealings. Therefore, I will bring forth a fire from within you, and it will consume you. I will reduce you to ashes on the earth in the sight of all who witness your downfall.

19 All those among the nations who know you will be appalled at your fate. You will become an object of horror, and you will cease to exist forever.

20 Once more, the word of the LORD came to me, saying,

21 "Son of man, direct your attention toward Sidon and deliver a prophecy against it."

22 And say, "This is what the Lord GOD declares: Behold, I am against you, O Sidon, and I will display my glory in your midst. Then they will know that I am the LORD when I carry out judgments against you and demonstrate my holiness through you."

23 For I will bring plague upon her, and bloodshed will fill her streets. The wounded will fall within her, judged by the sword that surrounds her on every side. Then they will know that I am the LORD.

24 No longer will there be a painful thorn or a hurtful brier for the house of Israel, nor for any of those around them who have treated them with contempt. Then they will know that I am the Sovereign LORD.

25 This is what the Lord GOD says: When I gather the house of Israel from the nations where they have been scattered and demonstrate my holiness through them in the sight of the nations, then they will live in the land I gave to my servant Jacob.

26 They will live securely in the land, building houses and planting vineyards. Indeed, they will dwell in safety and confidence once I have carried out judgments against all those around them who have treated them with contempt. Then they will know that I am the LORD their God.
