II Samuel Chapter 22

1 David spoke these words to the LORD in a song on the day when the LORD had rescued him from the power of all his enemies and from the hand of Saul:

2 And he declared, "The LORD is my solid foundation, my stronghold, and my rescuer."

3 The God who is my solid foundation—in Him I place my trust. He is my protector, the mighty source of my deliverance, my secure stronghold, and my safe haven. He is my Savior; You rescue me from violence.

4 I will call upon the LORD, who is deserving of all praise, and in doing so, I will be delivered from my enemies.

5 When the torrents of death surrounded me, the overwhelming waters of wicked men filled me with terror.

6 The anguish of the grave surrounded me; the traps of death confronted me.

7 In my time of trouble, I called out to the LORD and cried to my God for help. He heard my voice from his holy temple, and my plea reached his ears.

8 Then the earth quaked and trembled; the very foundations of the heavens shook and swayed, for He was filled with wrath.

9 Smoke rose from his nostrils, and flames burst from his mouth, consuming all before them; burning coals were ignited by its heat.

10 He bent the heavens and descended; thick darkness was beneath His feet.

11 He mounted a cherub and soared; he appeared swiftly on the wings of the wind.

12 He surrounded himself with darkness as if it were a canopy, with deep, shadowy waters and dense clouds filling the skies.

13 Through the radiance that emanated from Him, burning coals were set ablaze.

14 The LORD thundered from the heavens, and the Most High proclaimed His voice.

15 He shot his arrows and scattered them; he hurled lightning bolts and threw them into confusion.

16 The depths of the sea were laid bare, and the very foundations of the earth were exposed by the rebuke of the LORD, by the fierce blast of breath from his nostrils.

17 He reached down from on high and took hold of me; he rescued me from deep, overwhelming waters.

18 He rescued me from my powerful adversary and from those who hated me, for they were too mighty for me to overcome.

19 They confronted me in the day of my disaster, but the LORD was my support.

20 He led me out into a spacious place; he rescued me because he took pleasure in me.

21 The LORD has repaid me according to my uprightness; according to the purity of my actions, He has given me what I deserve.

22 For I have faithfully followed the ways of the LORD and have not turned away from my God in wickedness.

23 For all His decrees were always before me, and I did not turn away from His commands.

24 I have also walked in integrity before Him, and I have guarded myself from my own wrongdoing.

25 Therefore, the LORD has rewarded me according to my righteousness; according to the purity of my actions in His sight.

26 With those who show mercy, you will demonstrate your own mercy, and with those who live with integrity, you will reveal your own uprightness.

27 With the pure, you will show yourself pure; but with the deceitful, you will show yourself contrary.

28 You will rescue the oppressed, but your gaze is fixed on the proud, to bring them low.

29 For you, O LORD, are my source of light, and the LORD will dispel my darkness.

30 With your strength, I have charged through an army; with my God’s help, I have scaled a fortress wall.

31 As for God, His way is flawless; the word of the LORD is proven true. He is a shield for all who take refuge in Him.

32 Who is God except the LORD? And who is a rock except our God?

33 God is my source of strength and might; He makes my path complete and secure.

34 He makes my feet as swift and sure as those of a deer, and he places me securely on the heights.

35 He trains my hands for battle, so that my arms can bend a bow of bronze.

36 You have also given me the shield of your deliverance, and your steadfast care has made me prosper.

37 You have made my path wide and secure beneath me, so that my steps have not faltered.

38 I have pursued my enemies and defeated them; I did not turn back until I had completely destroyed them.

39 I have utterly destroyed them and struck them down, so that they cannot rise again. Indeed, they have fallen beneath my feet.

40 For you have equipped me with strength for the fight; you have brought down those who rose against me, placing them beneath my power.

41 You have also granted me victory over my enemies, enabling me to defeat those who oppose me.

42 They searched for help, but there was no one to rescue them; they even cried out to the LORD, but he did not answer them.

43 Then I crushed them into fine powder like the dust of the earth; I trampled them like mud in the streets and scattered them far and wide.

44 You have also rescued me from the conflicts of my own people; you have preserved me to be a leader over the nations. A people I did not know will now serve me.

45 Foreigners will yield to me; when they hear of me, they will obey me.

46 Foreigners will wither away, and they will tremble with fear in their strongholds.

47 The LORD lives; blessed be my rock; and may the God of my saving refuge be exalted.

48 It is God who executes justice for me and subdues the nations beneath me.

49 You have rescued me from my enemies; you have also raised me up, placing me above those who rose against me. You have delivered me from the violent one.

50 Therefore, I will give thanks to you, O LORD, among the nations, and I will sing praises to your name.

51 He is the stronghold of salvation for His king; He shows steadfast love to His anointed, to David, and to his descendants forever.
