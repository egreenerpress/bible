Proverbs Chapter 8

1 Does not wisdom call out? Does not understanding raise her voice?

2 She takes her position at the highest points of the city, at the crossroads where the paths meet.

3 She calls out at the city gates, at the entrance to the town, and at the doorways as people come and go.

4 I call out to you, O humanity; my voice is directed to all people.

5 O you who are naive, grasp wisdom; and you who are foolish, develop a discerning heart.

6 Listen carefully, for I will speak of noble and excellent matters; the words that come from my lips will be truthful and righteous.

7 For my mouth will speak truth, and wickedness is utterly detestable to my lips.

8 All the words I speak are rooted in righteousness; there is nothing crooked or twisted in them.

9 To those who have insight, all these things are clear, and to those who seek understanding, they are straightforward.

10 Accept my teaching rather than silver, and choose knowledge over the finest gold.

11 For wisdom is more valuable than precious jewels, and no desirable thing can compare to it.

12 I, Wisdom, reside alongside prudence, and I discover knowledge through insightful and ingenious plans.

13 To truly revere the LORD is to detest what is evil. I despise pride, arrogance, corrupt behavior, and deceitful speech.

14 Wisdom and sound judgment belong to me; I am insight, and I possess strength.

15 Through me, kings exercise their rule, and rulers establish what is just.

16 Through me, rulers govern, and nobles, along with all the judges of the earth, exercise their authority.

17 I love those who love me, and those who earnestly seek me will find me.

18 Wealth and honor accompany me; indeed, enduring riches and righteousness are mine.

19 The fruit I produce is more valuable than gold, even the purest gold; and the benefits I bring surpass the finest silver.

20 I guide along the path of righteousness, walking in the center of the ways of justice.

21 So that I may grant those who love me true wealth, and I will abundantly fill their storehouses.

22 The LORD established me at the start of His purpose, before His ancient works began.

23 I was established from eternity, from the very beginning, before the earth even existed.

24 Before the oceans existed, I was born; before the springs and their abundant waters flowed.

25 Before the mountains were firmly established, before the hills came into being, I was brought forth.

26 Before He had formed the earth, or the open lands, or even the first particles of the worldâ€™s soil.

27 When He established the heavens, I was present; when He marked out the horizon over the surface of the deep.

28 When He set the clouds in their place above, and secured the springs of the deep below.

29 When He established the boundaries of the sea, decreeing that its waters must not cross His command, and when He set the foundations of the earth in place.

30 At that time, I was beside Him, like a cherished companion; I was His constant delight, rejoicing in His presence every day.

31 I take joy in the inhabited world, the place where people dwell, and my greatest delight is found among humanity.

32 Now then, listen to me, you who are my children: for those who follow my ways will be truly blessed.

33 Listen to instruction and gain wisdom; do not reject it.

34 Blessed is the person who listens to me, diligently keeping watch at my gates each day, standing ready at the entrance to my doors.

35 Whoever finds me finds life and will receive favor from the LORD.

36 However, whoever sins against me harms their own soul; all who hate me are in love with death.
