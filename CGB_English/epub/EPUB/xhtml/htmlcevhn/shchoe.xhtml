Genesis Chapter 5

1 This is the record of Adamâ€™s descendants. On the day God created humanity, He made them in His own likeness.

2 God created them male and female, and He blessed them. On the day they were created, He named them "Adam," signifying humanity as a whole.

3 Adam lived for one hundred and thirty years and fathered a son in his own likeness, reflecting his image. He named him Seth.

4 After Adam became the father of Seth, he lived for eight hundred more years. During that time, he had other sons and daughters.

5 Adam lived a total of nine hundred and thirty years, and then he died.

6 Seth lived 105 years and became the father of Enosh.

7 After Seth became the father of Enosh, he lived 807 more years, and during that time, he had other sons and daughters.

8 Seth lived a total of nine hundred and twelve years, and then he died.

9 And Enos lived to be ninety years old, and then he became the father of Cainan.

10 After Enos became the father of Cainan, he lived for eight hundred and fifteen years, during which he had more sons and daughters.

11 Enoch lived a total of nine hundred and five years, and then he died.

12 Cainan was seventy years old when he became the father of Mahalaleel.

13 After Cainan became the father of Mahalaleel, he lived 840 more years and had other sons and daughters.

14 Cainan lived a total of nine hundred and ten years, and then he died.

15 Mahalaleel was sixty-five years old when he became the father of Jared.

16 After Mahalaleel became the father of Jared, he lived for eight hundred and thirty more years, during which he had other sons and daughters.

17 Mahalaleel lived a total of eight hundred and ninety-five years, and then he died.

18 Jared lived for 162 years, and then he became the father of Enoch.

19 Jared lived for eight hundred years after he became the father of Enoch, and during that time, he had more sons and daughters.

20 Jared lived a total of nine hundred and sixty-two years, and then he died.

21 Enoch was sixty-five years old when he became the father of Methuselah.

22 Enoch lived in close fellowship with God for three hundred years after he became the father of Methuselah, and during that time, he also had other sons and daughters.

23 Enoch lived a total of three hundred and sixty-five years.

24 Enoch lived in close fellowship with God; then he was no more, because God took him away.

25 Methuselah lived for one hundred and eighty-seven years and became the father of Lamech.

26 After Methuselah became the father of Lamech, he lived 782 more years and had other sons and daughters.

27 Methuselah lived a total of nine hundred and sixty-nine years, and then he died.

28 Lamech lived for one hundred and eighty-two years, and then he fathered a son.

29 And he named him Noah, saying, "This one will bring us relief from our labor and the painful toil of our hands, caused by the ground that the LORD has cursed."

30 After Lamech became the father of Noah, he lived for five hundred and ninety-five more years, during which he had other sons and daughters.

31 Lamech lived a total of seven hundred and seventy-seven years, and then he died.

32 When Noah was five hundred years old, he became the father of Shem, Ham, and Japheth.
