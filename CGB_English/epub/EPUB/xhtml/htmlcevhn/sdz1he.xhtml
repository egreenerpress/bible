Joel Chapter 3

1 Listen carefully, for in those days and at that time, when I restore the fortunes of Judah and Jerusalem,

2 I will also assemble all the nations and bring them down to the Valley of Jehoshaphat. There, I will confront them concerning my people and my inheritance, Israel, whom they have dispersed among the nations and whose land they have divided.

3 They have gambled for my people, trading a young boy for the services of a prostitute and selling a young girl for wine to indulge in their drinking.

4 What concern do you have with me, O Tyre and Sidon, and all the regions of Philistia? Are you planning to repay me for something? If you attempt to repay me, I will swiftly and surely turn your repayment back upon your own heads.

5 Because you have taken my silver and my gold, and have brought my precious treasures into your temples.

6 You have sold the people of Judah and Jerusalem to the Greeks, intending to send them far away from their own land.

7 Listen carefully: I will bring them back from the place where you sold them, and I will turn the consequences of your actions back upon you.

8 I will hand over your sons and daughters to the people of Judah, and they will sell them to the Sabeans, a distant nation. This will happen because the LORD has declared it.

9 Announce this among the nations: Prepare for war! Rouse the mighty warriors; let all the soldiers advance and assemble. Let them rise up and march forward!

10 Transform your farming tools into weapons of war—turn your plows into swords and your pruning hooks into spears. Even those who are weak should declare, "I am strong!"

11 Gather together and come, all you nations; assemble yourselves from every direction. Summon your mighty warriors to descend there, O LORD.

12 Let the nations be roused and come up to the Valley of Jehoshaphat, for there I will sit to judge all the surrounding nations.

13 Take up the sickle, for the harvest is ready. Come, go down, for the winepress is full and the vats overflow—their wickedness is immense.

14 Great crowds, vast multitudes are gathered in the valley of judgment, for the day of the LORD is close at hand in that valley of decision.

15 The sun and the moon will grow dark, and the stars will cease to shine.

16 The LORD will roar from Zion and raise His voice from Jerusalem; the heavens and the earth will tremble. Yet the LORD will be a refuge for His people and a stronghold for the people of Israel.

17 In this way, you will come to understand that I am the LORD your God, who dwells in Zion, my holy mountain. Then Jerusalem will be holy, and no foreigners will pass through her again.

18 In that day, the mountains will drip with sweet wine, and the hills will overflow with milk. All the streams of Judah will run with water, and a spring will rise from the house of the LORD, watering the valley of Shittim.

19 Egypt will become a wasteland, and Edom will turn into a barren desert, because of the violence they committed against the people of Judah and the innocent blood they shed in their land.

20 But Judah will endure forever, and Jerusalem will remain from one generation to the next.

21 For I will purify their guilt that I have not yet purified, for the LORD resides in Zion.
