Psalms Chapter 72

1 (A Psalm dedicated to Solomon.) Grant the king Your justice, O God, and Your righteousness to the kingâ€™s son.

2 He will govern your people with fairness and administer justice to your needy with equity.

3 The mountains will bring peace to the people, and the hills will do so through righteousness.

4 He will bring justice to the poor among the people; he will deliver the children of the destitute and crush the oppressor.

5 They will revere you as long as the sun and moon remain, enduring through every generation.

6 He will descend like rain upon freshly cut grass, like gentle showers that nourish the earth.

7 During his reign, the righteous will thrive, and there will be an abundance of peace as long as the moon remains in the sky.

8 He will rule from one sea to the other, and from the great river to the farthest reaches of the earth.

9 Those who live in the wilderness will bow down before him, and his enemies will be humbled, lying face down in the dust.

10 The rulers of Tarshish and the distant coastlands will bring tribute; the kings of Sheba and Seba will present their offerings.

11 Indeed, all kings will bow down before him; every nation will serve him.

12 For he will rescue the needy when they cry out for help, the poor as well, and those who have no one to assist them.

13 He will show compassion to the poor and those in need, and he will rescue the lives of the afflicted.

14 He will rescue their lives from deceit and violence, and their blood will be of great value in his eyes.

15 He will live, and the gold of Sheba will be given to him. Prayers will be offered on his behalf without ceasing, and he will be praised every day.

16 There will be an abundance of grain in the land, even on the highest mountain peaks; its harvest will flourish like the forests of Lebanon. And the people of the city will thrive and multiply like the grass of the earth.

17 His name will last forever; his reputation will endure as long as the sun shines. People will find blessing through him, and all nations will proclaim him blessed.

18 Praise be to the LORD God, the God of Israel, who alone accomplishes marvelous deeds.

19 May His glorious name be praised forever, and may the entire earth be filled with His majesty. Amen, and Amen.

20 The prayers of David, son of Jesse, have come to an end.
