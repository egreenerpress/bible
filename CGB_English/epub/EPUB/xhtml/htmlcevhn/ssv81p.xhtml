Isaiah Chapter 4

1 On that day, seven women will cling to one man, saying, "We will provide our own food and make our own clothing; only let us be called by your name. Take away our disgrace."

2 On that day, the branch of the LORD will be splendid and glorious, and the produce of the land will be magnificent and beautiful for the survivors of Israel.

3 And it will happen that whoever remains in Zion and whoever stays in Jerusalem will be called holy—everyone who is recorded among the living in Jerusalem.

4 When the Lord has cleansed the impurity of the daughters of Zion and has purged the bloodshed from Jerusalem’s midst through the spirit of justice and the spirit of refining fire.

5 The LORD will create over every dwelling place on Mount Zion and over all its gatherings a cloud by day and smoke, and the brightness of a blazing fire by night. For over all the glory there will be a protective covering.

6 There will be a shelter to provide shade during the day, protecting from the heat, and a place of safety, a refuge from storms and rain.
