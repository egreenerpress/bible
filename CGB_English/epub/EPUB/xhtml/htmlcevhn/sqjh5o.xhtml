Psalms Chapter 82

1 (A Psalm of Asaph.) God takes His place in the assembly of the divine beings; He presides in judgment among the rulers.

2 How long will you make unjust decisions and show favoritism to the wicked? Pause and reflect on this.

3 Stand up for the poor and the fatherless; ensure that justice is done for the oppressed and those in need.

4 Rescue the poor and those in need; save them from the power of the wicked.

5 They lack knowledge and refuse to comprehend; they continue to walk in ignorance and moral darkness. As a result, the very foundations of the world are shaken and thrown into disorder.

6 I have declared, "You are divine beings, and all of you are children of the Most High."

7 But you will perish like mere mortals, and fall like any other ruler.

8 Rise up, O God, and bring justice to the earth, for You will claim all nations as Your own.
