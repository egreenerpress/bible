John Chapter 15

1 I am the genuine vine, and my Father is the gardener.

2 Every branch connected to me that does not produce fruit, he removes; and every branch that does bear fruit, he prunes and cleanses, so that it may yield even more fruit.

3 You are now made clean because of the message I have spoken to you.

4 Remain united with me, and I will remain united with you. Just as a branch cannot produce fruit on its own unless it stays connected to the vine, neither can you bear fruit unless you remain united with me.

5 I am the true vine, and you are the branches. Whoever remains united with me, and I with them, will bear abundant fruit. Apart from me, you can accomplish nothing.

6 If someone does not remain connected to me, they are like a branch that is thrown away and withers. People gather such branches, throw them into the fire, and they are burned.

7 If you remain in me, and my teachings remain in you, you may ask for whatever you desire, and it will be granted to you.

8 In this way, my Father is honored—when you produce abundant fruit. By doing so, you will prove yourselves to be my true followers.

9 Just as the Father has loved me, I have also loved you. Remain steadfast in my love.

10 If you obey my commands, you will remain in my love, just as I have obeyed my Father’s commands and remain in his love.

11 I have told you these things so that my joy may be in you, and your joy may be complete.

12 This is my command: Love one another just as I have loved you.

13 No one demonstrates greater love than this: when someone willingly sacrifices their own life for the sake of their friends.

14 You are my friends if you do what I command you.

15 From now on, I no longer call you servants, because a servant does not know what his master is doing. Instead, I have called you friends, for everything I have learned from my Father I have made known to you.

16 You did not choose me, but I have chosen you and appointed you so that you may go and produce lasting fruit. And whatever you ask of the Father in my name, he will give to you.

17 I am giving you these instructions so that you may love one another.

18 If the world hates you, understand that it hated me before it hated you.

19 If you belonged to the world, the world would love you as its own. But because you do not belong to the world—for I have chosen you out of the world—the world hates you.

20 Remember the words I spoke to you: A servant is not greater than his master. If they persecuted me, they will also persecute you. If they held to my teachings, they will hold to yours as well.

21 However, they will treat you in all these ways because of your association with me, for they do not know the one who sent me.

22 If I had not come and spoken to them, they would not be guilty of sin; but now they have no excuse for their wrongdoing.

23 Whoever hates me also hates my Father.

24 If I had not performed among them the works that no one else has done, they would not be guilty of sin. But now they have seen these works and have hated both me and my Father.

25 This happened so that the word written in their law might be fulfilled: "They hated me without any reason."

26 But when the Advocate comes, whom I will send to you from the Father—the Spirit of truth, who originates from the Father—he will bear witness about me.

27 You will also testify, because you have been with me from the very start.
