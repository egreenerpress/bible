Psalms Chapter 116

1 I love the LORD because he has listened to my voice and my pleas for help.

2 Because he has listened attentively to me, I will call upon him as long as I live.

3 The anguish of death surrounded me, and the torment of the grave seized me; I encountered distress and sorrow.

4 Then I called out to the LORD, saying, "O LORD, I beg you, save my life!"

5 The LORD is gracious and just; indeed, our God is compassionate.

6 The LORD protects the vulnerable; when I was in distress, He came to my aid.

7 Return to your rest, O my soul, for the Lord has been abundantly good to you.

8 For you have rescued my life from death, my eyes from weeping, and my feet from stumbling.

9 I will live my life in the presence of the LORD, walking faithfully before Him in this world of the living.

10 I trusted in God, and so I spoke; I was deeply troubled and in distress.

11 In my moment of distress, I declared, "Every person is deceitful."

12 What can I give back to the LORD in return for all the good He has done for me?

13 I will lift up the cup of deliverance and proclaim the name of the LORD.

14 I will fulfill my promises to the LORD now, in the presence of all His people.

15 The death of those who are faithful to the LORD is deeply valued and significant in His eyes.

16 O LORD, I am indeed your servant; I am your servant, the child of your maidservant. You have set me free from my chains.

17 I will present to you the offering of gratitude and proclaim the name of the LORD.

18 I will fulfill the promises I made to the LORD, doing so now in the presence of all His people.

19 In the courtyards of the house of the LORD, within you, O Jerusalem. Praise the LORD.
