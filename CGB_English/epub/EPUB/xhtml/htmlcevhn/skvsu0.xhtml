Ephesians Chapter 6

1 Children, obey your parents as followers of the Lord, for this is what is right and proper.

2 Respect and honor your father and mother—this is the first commandment that comes with a promise.

3 So that it may go well with you, and you may live a long life on the earth.

4 And fathers, do not provoke your children to anger, but raise them with loving discipline and instruction that reflects the ways of the Lord.

5 Servants, submit yourselves to your earthly masters with respect and humility, serving them sincerely and wholeheartedly, as you would serve Christ.

6 Do not work merely to be seen by others or to win their approval, but as servants of Christ, wholeheartedly carrying out the will of God.

7 Serve wholeheartedly, as if you were working for the Lord and not merely for people.

8 Be assured that whatever good deed anyone does, they will receive the same from the Lord, whether they are enslaved or free.

9 And you, masters, act in the same way toward your servants, refraining from threats, knowing that your own Master is in heaven, and there is no partiality with Him.

10 Finally, my brothers and sisters, find your strength in the Lord and in the might of His power.

11 Clothe yourselves fully in the armor of God, so that you may be equipped to stand firm against the schemes and deceitful tactics of the devil.

12 For our struggle is not against human opponents of flesh and blood, but against the spiritual forces of evil—against the rulers, authorities, and cosmic powers of this present darkness, and against the malevolent spiritual forces operating in the heavenly realms.

13 Therefore, take up the full armor of God, so that you may be able to resist in the day of evil, and after you have done everything, to stand firm.

14 Stand firm, then, with truth fastened securely around you like a belt, and wearing righteousness as your protective armor.

15 And let your feet be equipped with the readiness that comes from the good news of peace.

16 Above all, take up the shield of faith, with which you will be able to extinguish all the flaming arrows of the evil one.

17 Put on the helmet of salvation, and take up the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God.

18 Pray continually, offering every kind of prayer and petition in the power of the Spirit. Stay alert and persistent in your prayers, interceding earnestly for all God’s people.

19 And for me, may the ability to speak be granted, so that I may proclaim with boldness and clarity the hidden truth of the gospel.

20 For this reason, I am an ambassador in chains, so that through it I may speak with boldness, as it is my duty to speak.

21 So that you may also be informed about my circumstances and how I am doing, Tychicus, a dear brother and trustworthy servant in the Lord, will share with you all the details.

22 I have sent him to you for this very reason, so that you may be informed about our circumstances and that he may encourage and strengthen your hearts.

23 May peace be with the brothers and sisters, and may love accompanied by faith be yours, from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

24 May grace be with all who genuinely love our Lord Jesus Christ. Amen.
