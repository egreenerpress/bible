Jeremiah Chapter 16

1 The message of the LORD also came to me, saying,

2 You must not take a wife for yourself, nor shall you have sons or daughters in this place.

3 For this is what the LORD says concerning the sons and daughters born in this land, and concerning their mothers who gave birth to them, and concerning their fathers who fathered them in this place:

4 They will perish by terrible deaths; no one will mourn for them, nor will they be buried. Instead, they will become like refuse scattered on the ground. They will be destroyed by the sword and by famine, and their bodies will serve as food for the birds of the sky and the wild animals of the earth.

5 For this is what the LORD says: Do not enter a house of mourning, nor go to grieve or mourn for them, for I have withdrawn my peace from this people, declares the LORD—my steadfast love and compassion.

6 Both the prominent and the lowly will die in this land. They will not be buried, nor will anyone mourn for them, gash themselves, or shave their heads in grief.

7 People will no longer make cuts on their bodies or shave their heads in mourning to show grief for the dead. Nor will they offer them the cup of consolation to drink, even when they mourn the loss of a father or mother.

8 You must not enter the house of celebration to sit with them, eating and drinking.

9 For this is what the LORD of hosts, the God of Israel, declares: Listen carefully, I will bring an end to this place before your very eyes, and in your lifetime—the sounds of joy and celebration, the voices of the bridegroom and the bride will no longer be heard here.

10 And when you present all these words to the people, they will ask you, 'Why has the LORD declared such severe judgment against us? What wrongdoing have we committed? What sin have we committed against the LORD our God?'

11 Then you shall say to them, "Because your ancestors have abandoned me, declares the LORD, and have followed other gods, serving and worshiping them. They have forsaken me and have not obeyed my law."

12 You have acted even more wickedly than your ancestors, for each of you follows the stubborn desires of your own evil heart, refusing to listen to me.

13 Therefore, I will drive you out of this land into a land that neither you nor your ancestors have known. There you will serve other gods day and night, for I will show you no mercy.

14 Therefore, listen carefully, the time is coming, declares the LORD, when people will no longer say, "As surely as the LORD lives, who brought the Israelites out of the land of Egypt."

15 But as surely as the LORD lives—the one who brought the Israelites out of the land of the north and from all the countries where he had scattered them—I will bring them back to their own land, the land I gave to their ancestors.

16 Listen carefully, for this is what the LORD declares: I will summon many fishermen, and they will gather them in like fish from the sea. Afterward, I will call for many hunters, and they will pursue them from every mountain, every hill, and even from the crevices of the rocks.

17 For my eyes are upon all their actions: nothing is concealed from my sight, nor is their wrongdoing hidden from my eyes.

18 First, I will repay them double for their iniquity and their sin, because they have defiled My land and filled My inheritance with the lifeless bodies of their detestable idols and abominable practices.

19 O LORD, my strength, my fortress, and my safe haven in times of trouble, the nations will come to you from the farthest corners of the earth and declare, "Truly, our ancestors passed down to us nothing but falsehoods, empty idols, and things that bring no benefit."

20 Can a person create gods for themselves, even though they are not truly gods?

21 Therefore, take notice: this one time I will make them understand; I will show them my power and my strength. Then they will come to know that my name is the LORD.
