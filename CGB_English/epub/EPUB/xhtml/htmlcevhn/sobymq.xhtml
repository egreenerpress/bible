Psalms Chapter 8

1 (For the music director, to be played with the Gittith instrument. A psalm of David.) O LORD, our Sovereign, how majestic is your name throughout all the earth! You have placed your splendor above the heavens.

2 From the lips of children and infants, you have established strength to confront your adversaries, silencing the enemy and the avenger.

3 When I look at the heavens, the work of your hands, the moon and the stars, which you have set in their placeâ€”

4 What is humanity, that you take notice of them? And what is a mortal being, that you care for them?

5 For you have made him slightly less than divine beings, and you have adorned him with splendor and dignity.

6 You appointed him to rule over the works of your hands; you placed everything under his authority.

7 All sheep and cattle, and even the wild animals of the field.

8 The birds of the sky, the fish of the sea, and all creatures that move through the waters of the oceans.

9 O LORD, our Sovereign, how majestic is your name throughout all the earth!
