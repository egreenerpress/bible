I Chronicles Chapter 8

1 Benjamin was the father of Bela, his firstborn; Ashbel was his second son, and Aharah his third.

2 Nohah was the fourth, and Rapha was the fifth.

3 The sons of Bela were Addar, Gera, and Abihud.

4 And Abishua, Naaman, and Ahoah,

5 And Gera, Shephuphan, and Huram.

6 These are the descendants of Ehud: they were the leaders of the ancestral families of the people living in Geba, and they were relocated to Manahath.

7 Naaman, Ahijah, and Gera were taken away, and he became the father of Uzza and Ahihud.

8 Shaharaim became the father of children in the land of Moab after he had sent away his wives. His wives were Hushim and Baara.

9 And he became the father of Jobab, Zibia, Mesha, and Malcham through his wife Hodesh.

10 Jeuz, Shachia, and Mirma. These were his sons, who became leaders of their ancestral families.

11 And from Hushim, he fathered Abitub and Elpaal.

12 The descendants of Elpaal were Eber, Misham, and Shamed. Shamed built the cities of Ono and Lod, along with their surrounding towns.

13 Beriah and Shema, who were leaders of the ancestral families of the people living in Aijalon, were the ones who drove out the inhabitants of Gath.

14 And Ahio, Shashak, and Jeremoth.

15 And Zebadiah, and Arad, and Ader,

16 And Michael, Ispah, and Joha, the sons of Beriah.

17 And Zebadiah, Meshullam, Hezeki, and Heber.

18 Ishmerai, Jezliah, and Jobab, the sons of Elpaal.

19 And Jakim, Zichri, and Zabdi,

20 And Elienai, Zilthai, and Eliel.

21 And Adaiah, Beraiah, and Shimrath were the sons of Shimhi.

22 And Ishpan, Heber, and Eliel.

23 And Abdon, Zichri, and Hanan...

24 And Hananiah, Elam, and Antothijah...

25 And Iphedeiah and Penuel, the sons of Shashak.

26 And Shamsherai, Shehariah, and Athaliah.

27 And Jaresiah, Eliah, and Zichri, the sons of Jeroham.

28 These were the leaders of the ancestral families, distinguished by their generations, prominent individuals. They resided in Jerusalem.

29 At Gibeon lived the father of Gibeon, whose wife was named Maachah.

30 His firstborn son was Abdon, followed by Zur, Kish, Baal, and Nadab.

31 And Gedor, Ahio, and Zechariah.

32 Mikloth was the father of Shimeah. These individuals also lived alongside their relatives in Jerusalem, residing near them.

33 Ner was the father of Kish, Kish was the father of Saul, and Saul was the father of Jonathan, Malchi-shua, Abinadab, and Esh-baal.

34 Jonathan's son was Merib-baal, and Merib-baal became the father of Micah.

35 The sons of Micah were Pithon, Melech, Tarea, and Ahaz.

36 Ahaz was the father of Jehoadah; Jehoadah was the father of Alemeth, Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri was the father of Moza.

37 Moza was the father of Binea; Rapha was Binea's son, Eleasah was Rapha's son, and Azel was Eleasah's son.

38 Azel had six sons, and their names were Azrikam, Bocheru, Ishmael, Sheariah, Obadiah, and Hanan. All of these were the sons of Azel.

39 The sons of Eshek, his brother, were Ulam, his firstborn; Jehush, the second; and Eliphelet, the third.

40 The descendants of Ulam were courageous and powerful warriors, skilled archers. They had many children and grandchildren, totaling one hundred and fifty. All these were part of the lineage of Benjamin.
