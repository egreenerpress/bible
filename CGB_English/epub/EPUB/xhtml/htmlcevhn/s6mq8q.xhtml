Psalms Chapter 29

1 (A Psalm of David.) Acknowledge the LORD, you powerful ones; ascribe to the LORD the honor and might that He deserves.

2 Ascribe to the LORD the honor that is fitting for his name; worship the LORD in the splendor of his sacred majesty.

3 The voice of the LORD resounds over the waters; the God of glory thunders. The LORD is present over the mighty waters.

4 The voice of the LORD is mighty; the voice of the LORD radiates splendor and grandeur.

5 The voice of the LORD shatters the mighty cedars; indeed, the LORD splinters the cedars of Lebanon.

6 He causes them to leap like a young calf; Lebanon and Sirion like a vigorous young ox.

7 The voice of the LORD splits apart the flames of fire.

8 The voice of the LORD shakes the wilderness; the LORD causes the wilderness of Kadesh to tremble.

9 The voice of the LORD causes the deer to give birth and strips the forests bare; in His temple, all proclaim His glory.

10 The LORD reigns over the great waters; indeed, the LORD is enthroned as King forever.

11 The LORD will grant power to His people; the LORD will bestow upon His people the blessing of peace.
