Psalms Chapter 119

1 (Aleph) Blessed are those whose lives are blameless, who walk in accordance with the law of the LORD.

2 Blessed are those who faithfully uphold His decrees and earnestly seek Him with all their heart.

3 They do not commit wrongdoing; they live according to His ways.

4 You have commanded us to faithfully observe your instructions with great care and diligence.

5 Oh, how I wish my life were consistently guided to obey your commands!

6 Then I will not be put to shame, when I consider all your commands.

7 I will praise you with sincerity and integrity of heart, once I have come to understand your righteous decrees.

8 I will faithfully follow your decrees; do not abandon me completely.

9 (BETH.) How can a young person keep their life pure? By living in accordance with your word.

10 With all my heart I have sought you; do not let me stray from your commands.

11 I have treasured your word in my heart, so that I may not sin against you.

12 Blessed are You, O LORD; instruct me in Your decrees.

13 With my words, I have proclaimed all the decrees that come from your mouth.

14 I have found great joy in following your instructions, as much as I would delight in possessing all kinds of wealth.

15 I will deeply reflect on your instructions and give careful attention to your ways.

16 I will find joy in your decrees; I will not neglect your word.

17 (GIMEL.) Show abundant kindness to your servant, so that I may continue to live and remain faithful to your word.

18 Open my eyes, so that I may see the marvelous truths contained in your law.

19 I am a foreigner in this land; do not conceal your instructions from me.

20 My soul is overwhelmed with an intense longing for your righteous decrees at all times.

21 You have rebuked the arrogant, who are under a curse, for they stray from your commands.

22 Take away from me disgrace and scorn, for I have faithfully followed your instructions.

23 Even rulers sat and spoke against me, but I, your servant, focused deeply on your decrees.

24 Your testimonies are my joy and my guides.

25 (DALETH.) My soul clings to the ground; revive me according to your promise.

26 I have made my ways known to you, and you have listened to me; instruct me in your decrees.

27 Help me comprehend the path of your instructions, so that I may speak of your marvelous deeds.

28 My soul is overwhelmed with sorrow; strengthen me according to your promise.

29 Turn me away from the path of deceit, and graciously give me the guidance of your instruction.

30 I have chosen the path of truth; I have set your judgments before me as my guide.

31 I have remained faithful to your instructions; O LORD, do not let me be disgraced.

32 I will eagerly follow the path of your commands when you give me a heart that is open and willing.

33 O LORD, instruct me in the path of your decrees, and I will faithfully follow them to the very end.

34 Grant me understanding, and I will faithfully follow your law; indeed, I will wholeheartedly obey it.

35 Guide me to walk in the way of your commands, for in them I find my joy.

36 Turn my heart toward your instructions, and not toward selfish gain.

37 Turn my eyes away from looking at worthless things, and give me life according to your ways.

38 "Fulfill your promise to your servant, who is dedicated to revering you."

39 Remove the disgrace that I dread, for your rulings are just and righteous.

40 See, I have earnestly desired your instructions; revive me according to your justice.

41 (Vav.) May your mercies come to me, O LORD, and your salvation as well, according to your promise.

42 In this way, I will have a response for those who taunt me, because I place my confidence in your word.

43 Do not completely remove the word of truth from my mouth, for I have placed my hope in your judgments.

44 Therefore, I will faithfully observe your law always, for all time to come.

45 I will walk in freedom, for I diligently follow your instructions.

46 I will speak of your decrees even in the presence of kings, and I will not be ashamed.

47 And I will find joy in your commandments, which I have cherished.

48 I will also raise my hands in reverence to your commandments, which I deeply cherish; and I will reflect deeply on your statutes.

49 (ZAYIN.) Remember the promise you made to your servant, the word on which you have given me hope.

50 This is my solace in my suffering: for your word has given me life.

51 The arrogant have mocked me with great scorn, yet I have not turned aside from your law.

52 I have recalled your ancient decrees, O LORD, and they have brought me comfort.

53 Terror has gripped me because of the wicked who abandon your law.

54 Your laws have been my source of joy and comfort throughout my journey in this life.

55 I have called to mind your name, O LORD, during the night, and I have faithfully followed your instructions.

56 I have gained this because I have faithfully followed your instructions.

57 You, O LORD, are my portion and my inheritance; I have promised to obey your words.

58 I earnestly sought your favor with all my heart; show me mercy according to your promise.

59 I reflected on my actions and redirected my steps to align with your instructions.

60 I hurried and did not hesitate to follow your commands.

61 The cords of the wicked have ensnared me, yet I have not forsaken your instruction.

62 At midnight, I will rise to give you thanks because of your just and righteous decrees.

63 I associate closely with all who revere you and with those who faithfully follow your instructions.

64 O LORD, the earth is filled with your steadfast love; instruct me in your decrees.

65 (TETH.) You have treated your servant well, O LORD, in accordance with your promise.

66 Instruct me in sound discernment and understanding, for I have placed my trust in your commands.

67 Before I experienced hardship, I wandered from the right path; but now I have obeyed your word.

68 You are good, and you do good; instruct me in your laws.

69 The arrogant have fabricated falsehoods against me, but I will wholeheartedly uphold your commandments.

70 Their hearts are calloused and unfeeling, like fat that has grown thick; yet I find joy and delight in your law.

71 It is beneficial for me that I have endured hardship, for through it I have come to understand and follow your laws.

72 Your instruction is more precious to me than vast treasures of gold and silver.

73 Your hands have created me and shaped me; grant me understanding so that I may learn your commandments.

74 Those who hold you in reverence will rejoice when they see me, for I have placed my trust in your word.

75 I acknowledge, O LORD, that your judgments are just, and that in your faithfulness you have brought hardship upon me.

76 I ask that your steadfast love and mercy bring me comfort, as you have promised to your servant.

77 Let your compassionate mercies reach me, so that I may live; for your law is my joy and delight.

78 Let those who are arrogant be put to shame, for they have wronged me without justification. Yet I will continue to reflect deeply on your instructions.

79 Let those who revere you turn to me, and those who understand your decrees.

80 Grant that my heart may be steadfast in following your decrees, so that I will not be put to shame.

81 My soul grows weary as I long for your deliverance, yet I place my hope in your word.

82 My eyes grow weary as I wait for Your promise, asking, "When will You bring me comfort?"

83 For I have become like a wineskin dried in the smoke, yet I have not forgotten your decrees.

84 How long will my life last? When will you bring justice against those who persecute me?

85 Arrogant individuals have set traps for me, acting in ways that do not align with your instructions.

86 All your commands are trustworthy and true. Though people persecute me without cause, come to my aid.

87 They nearly destroyed me completely on earth, but I did not abandon your instructions.

88 Revive me according to your steadfast love, so that I may obey the decrees of your word.

89 (LAMED.) O LORD, your word stands firm forever, established in the heavens.

90 Your faithfulness extends to every generation; you established the earth, and it remains steadfast.

91 They persist to this day in accordance with your decrees, for all things are subject to your command.

92 If your law had not been my source of joy, I would have been destroyed in my suffering.

93 I will never forget your instructions, for through them you have given me life.

94 I belong to you; save me, for I have diligently sought your instructions.

95 The wicked lie in wait to destroy me, but I will diligently reflect on your teachings.

96 I have observed that all human endeavors have their limits, but your commandment is immeasurably vast and boundless.

97 (MEM.) Oh, how deeply I love your instruction! It is my constant meditation throughout the day.

98 Through your commandments, you have given me greater wisdom than my enemies, for your teachings are always with me.

99 I possess greater understanding than all my teachers, for I continually reflect on your testimonies.

100 I possess greater understanding than those of old, because I diligently follow your instructions.

101 I have kept my feet from every path of wrongdoing, so that I may remain faithful to your word.

102 I have not strayed from your decrees, for you yourself have instructed me.

103 How delightful are your words to my taste! Truly, they are sweeter than honey to my mouth!

104 By following your instructions, I gain insight; therefore, I detest every path of deceit.

105 Your word is a guiding light for my steps and a radiant beacon for my journey.

106 I have made a solemn promise, and I will fulfill it: I will faithfully uphold your just and righteous decrees.

107 I am deeply troubled and in great distress; revive me, O LORD, in accordance with your promise.

108 Please accept, I earnestly ask, the voluntary offerings of my words, O LORD, and instruct me in your laws.

109 My life is constantly at risk, yet I have not forgotten your law.

110 The wicked have set a trap for me, yet I have not strayed from your instructions.

111 I have embraced your testimonies as my eternal inheritance, for they bring joy and delight to my heart.

112 I have set my heart to follow your decrees faithfully, now and forevermore.

113 I despise worthless and empty thoughts, but I deeply love your law.

114 You are my refuge and my protection; I place my trust in your word.

115 Leave me, you who do evil, for I am determined to obey the commands of my God.

116 Support me according to your promise, so that I may continue to live; do not let my hope bring me shame.

117 Support me, and I will be secure; I will continually give careful attention to your decrees.

118 You have subdued all who stray from your decrees, for their deceit is nothing but falsehood.

119 You remove all the wicked of the earth like impurities; therefore, I deeply cherish your decrees.

120 My body trembles in awe of you, and I am filled with fear at the thought of your judgments.

121 I have acted with justice and righteousness; do not abandon me to those who oppress me.

122 Stand as a guarantee for your servant, ensuring my well-being; do not allow the arrogant to oppress me.

123 My eyes grow weary as I wait for your deliverance, longing for the fulfillment of your promise of righteousness.

124 Treat me, your servant, with the kindness and compassion that come from you, and instruct me in your laws and decrees.

125 I am your servant; grant me understanding so that I may fully grasp your teachings.

126 It is time for you, LORD, to act, for they have disregarded your law.

127 Therefore, I cherish your commandments more than gold, even more than the purest gold.

128 Therefore, I consider all your instructions concerning every matter to be just and true, and I detest every path of falsehood.

129 Your decrees are marvelous; therefore, my soul faithfully observes them.

130 The unfolding of your words brings illumination; it grants insight to those who lack understanding.

131 I opened my mouth and gasped with longing, for I deeply desired your commandments.

132 Look upon me and show me mercy, as you are accustomed to doing for those who love your name.

133 Guide my actions according to your word, and do not let any sin rule over me.

134 Rescue me from the oppression of others, and I will faithfully obey your commands.

135 Let your favor shine upon your servant, and instruct me in your laws.

136 Tears stream down my face like flowing rivers because people do not follow your law.

137 (TZADDI.) You are righteous, O LORD, and your judgments are just.

138 The decrees you have established are just and completely trustworthy.

139 My passion has overwhelmed me, because my enemies have disregarded your words.

140 Your word is exceedingly pure; therefore, your servant deeply cherishes it.

141 I am insignificant and looked down upon, yet I do not forget your instructions.

142 Your righteousness is eternal and unchanging, and your law is the ultimate truth.

143 Distress and deep anxiety have gripped me, yet your commandments remain my source of joy and delight.

144 The justice and truth of your decrees endure forever; grant me insight, and I will truly live.

145 (KOPH) I have called out to you with all my heart; answer me, O LORD. I will faithfully obey your decrees.

146 I called out to you; rescue me, and I will faithfully obey your instructions.

147 I rose before the break of dawn and called out to you; I placed my hope in your word.

148 My eyes awaken before the night watches, so that I may meditate on your word.

149 Listen to my voice according to your steadfast love, O LORD; revive me according to your justice.

150 Those who pursue wickedness draw near; they are distant from your law.

151 You are close, O LORD, and all your commands are true.

152 Regarding your testimonies, I have long understood that you have established them to endure forever.

153 (Resh.) Look upon my suffering and rescue me, for I have not forgotten your law.

154 Defend my case and rescue me; revive me according to your promise.

155 Deliverance remains distant from the wicked, for they do not pursue your decrees.

156 Your compassion is vast, O LORD; revive me according to your righteous decrees.

157 Many are those who pursue and oppose me, yet I do not turn away from your instructions.

158 I observed those who were breaking your laws, and it deeply troubled me, for they did not follow your commands.

159 Reflect on how deeply I cherish your instructions; revive me, O LORD, according to your steadfast love.

160 Your word is true from the very start, and all your righteous decrees stand firm forever.

161 Rulers have pursued me without justification, yet my heart reveres your word.

162 I delight in your word like someone who discovers immense treasure.

163 I deeply despise and detest falsehood, but I hold your law in the highest regard and love it deeply.

164 Throughout the day, seven times, I offer you praise because of your just and righteous decrees.

165 Those who love your law have great peace, and nothing will cause them to stumble.

166 LORD, I have placed my hope in your deliverance and have faithfully followed your commands.

167 I have faithfully followed your instructions, and I deeply cherish them.

168 I have faithfully followed your instructions and your decrees, for all my actions are laid bare before you.

169 (Tav.) May my plea come before you, O LORD; grant me insight according to your word.

170 Allow my earnest plea to come before you; rescue me in accordance with your promise.

171 My lips will speak words of praise when you have instructed me in your laws.

172 My tongue will declare your word, for all your commandments are just and right.

173 Please extend your hand to assist me, for I have deliberately chosen to follow your instructions and commands.

174 I have earnestly desired your deliverance, O LORD; and your instruction brings me great joy.

175 May my life be preserved, and I will praise you; may your righteous decrees sustain and guide me.

176 I have wandered away like a lost sheep; come and find your servant, for I have not forgotten your commands.
