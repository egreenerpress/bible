Proverbs Chapter 2

1 My child, if you are willing to accept my teachings and treasure my instructions within you,

2 Therefore, listen carefully to wisdom, and focus your heart on gaining understanding.

3 Indeed, if you earnestly seek knowledge and call out for understanding;

4 If you pursue wisdom as eagerly as you would search for silver, and seek it as diligently as you would hunt for hidden treasures,

5 Then you will come to understand what it truly means to revere the LORD, and you will gain the knowledge of God.

6 The LORD grants wisdom; from His mouth come knowledge and understanding.

7 He stores up true wisdom for the righteous; He is a shield for those who live with integrity.

8 He maintains the ways of justice and protects the path of those who are faithful to him.

9 Then you will comprehend what is right, justice, and fairnessâ€”indeed, every virtuous way.

10 When wisdom takes root in your heart, and knowledge becomes a delight to your soul;

11 Good judgment will protect you, and discernment will guard you.

12 To rescue you from the path of the wicked, from those who speak deceitful and perverse words.

13 Those who abandon the paths of righteousness to follow the ways of darkness.

14 They take pleasure in doing wrong and find joy in the perverse ways of the wicked.

15 "Those whose ways are twisted and who are devious in their paths."

16 To rescue you from the immoral woman, from the outsider who seduces with her smooth words;

17 She abandons the guidance she received in her youth and disregards the sacred covenant of her God.

18 Her house leads down to death, and her paths descend to the realm of the dead.

19 No one who goes to her ever returns; they never regain the path of life.

20 So that you may follow the way of those who do good and remain steadfast on the paths of the upright.

21 For those who live with integrity will inhabit the land, and those who are blameless will endure in it.

22 However, the wicked will be removed from the land, and those who act treacherously will be uprooted from it.
