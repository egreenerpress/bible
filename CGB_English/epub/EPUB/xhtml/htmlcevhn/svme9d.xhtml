Psalms Chapter 45

1 (To the director of music, set to the melody of "Lilies," for the sons of Korah, a contemplative song, a love poem.) My heart overflows with noble words; I speak of the works I have composed concerning the king. My tongue is like the pen of a skilled writer, swiftly and eloquently expressing my thoughts.

2 You are more majestic than all others; your speech is filled with grace. Because of this, God has blessed you eternally.

3 Fasten your sword to your side, O mighty one, in your splendor and majesty.

4 Ride forth in your majesty and triumph, upheld by truth, humility, and justice. Let your right hand guide you to awe-inspiring deeds.

5 Your arrows are sharp, piercing the hearts of the king’s foes; the nations fall beneath your power.

6 Your throne, O God, endures forever and ever; the scepter of your kingdom is a scepter of justice.

7 You love righteousness and detest wickedness; therefore, God, your God, has anointed you with the oil of joy more than any of your companions.

8 Your robes are fragrant with the scent of myrrh, aloes, and cassia, emanating from ivory palaces, bringing you joy and delight.

9 The daughters of kings are among your honored ladies; at your right hand stands the queen, adorned in the finest gold from Ophir.

10 Listen carefully, daughter, and pay close attention; turn your ear to my words. Leave behind your own people and your father’s household.

11 Therefore, the king will greatly admire your beauty, for he is your sovereign; and you are to honor and revere him.

12 The people of Tyre will come with gifts; even the wealthiest among the nations will seek your favor.

13 The king's daughter is radiant in her inner beauty; her garments are intricately woven with gold.

14 She will be presented to the king in garments adorned with intricate embroidery; the young women, her companions who accompany her, will also be brought before you.

15 They will be led with joy and celebration; they will come into the king’s palace.

16 Rather than your ancestors, your descendants will rise to prominence, and you will establish them as leaders throughout the earth.

17 I will ensure that your name is remembered throughout all generations; therefore, the people will praise you endlessly, for all time.
