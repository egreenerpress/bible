Psalms Chapter 128

1 (A song of ascents.) Blessed are all who revere the LORD, who walk in his ways.

2 For you will enjoy the fruit of your labor; you will be blessed, and all will go well for you.

3 Your wife will be like a flourishing vine, thriving within your home; your children will be like young olive trees, growing strong and vibrant around your table.

4 Look, this is how the person who reveres the LORD will be blessed.

5 The LORD will bless you from Zion, and you will witness the prosperity of Jerusalem throughout all the days of your life.

6 Indeed, you will live to see your grandchildren, and there will be peace throughout Israel.
