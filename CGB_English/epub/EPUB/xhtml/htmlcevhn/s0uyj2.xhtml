Joshua Chapter 12

1 These are the kings of the land whom the Israelites defeated and whose territory they took possession of, on the eastern side of the Jordan River, toward the sunrise. Their land extended from the Arnon River to Mount Hermon, including the entire eastern plain.

2 Sihon, the king of the Amorites, lived in Heshbon and ruled over a territory that extended from Aroer, located on the edge of the Arnon River, including the middle of the river itself, and from half of Gilead all the way to the Jabbok River, which marked the boundary of the Ammonites.

3 And from the flatlands to the Sea of Chinnereth on the east, and to the Sea of the Arabah, which is the Salt Sea, on the east, toward Beth-jeshimoth; and from the south, below the slopes of Pisgah:

4 The territory of Og, king of Bashan, who was one of the last of the Rephaim (a race of giants), and who ruled in Ashtaroth and Edrei.

5 He ruled over the region of Mount Hermon, Salcah, and all of Bashan, extending as far as the territory of the Geshurites and the Maachathites, and over half of Gilead, up to the border of Sihon, the king of Heshbon.

6 Moses, the servant of the LORD, and the Israelites defeated them. Then Moses, the servant of the LORD, gave their land as an inheritance to the Reubenites, the Gadites, and the half-tribe of Manasseh.

7 These are the kings of the land whom Joshua and the Israelites defeated on the western side of the Jordan, from Baal-gad in the Valley of Lebanon to Mount Halak, which rises toward Seir. Joshua gave their land to the tribes of Israel as their possession, divided according to their allotted portions.

8 In the mountains, in the valleys, on the plains, by the springs, in the wilderness, and in the southern region—there dwell the Hittites, the Amorites, the Canaanites, the Perizzites, the Hivites, and the Jebusites.

9 The king of Jericho—one; the king of Ai, which is near Bethel—one.

10 The king of Jerusalem—one; the king of Hebron—one.

11 The king of Jarmuth, one; the king of Lachish, one.

12 The king of Eglon, one; the king of Gezer, one.

13 The king of Debir, one; the king of Geder, one.

14 The king of Hormah, one; the king of Arad, one.

15 The king of Libnah, one; the king of Adullam, one.

16 The king of Makkedah, one; the king of Bethel, one.

17 The king of Tappuah, one; the king of Hepher, one.

18 The king of Aphek, one; the king of Lasharon, one.

19 The king of Madon, one; the king of Hazor, one.

20 The king of Shimron-meron—one; the king of Achshaph—one.

21 The king of Taanach, one; the king of Megiddo, one.

22 The king of Kedesh—one; the king of Jokneam in Carmel—one.

23 The king of Dor, located in the region of Dor—one ruler; the king of the nations of Gilgal—another ruler.

24 The king of Tirzah, one: in total, there were thirty-one kings.
