Proverbs Chapter 11

1 Using dishonest scales is detestable to the LORD, but an accurate measure brings Him joy.

2 When pride arises, disgrace follows; but wisdom resides with the humble.

3 The moral integrity of the upright will lead them forward, but the twisted ways of the unfaithful will bring about their ruin.

4 Wealth provides no advantage on the day of judgment, but living a righteous life rescues from death.

5 The integrity of the upright will guide their path, but the wicked will be brought down by their own wrongdoing.

6 The integrity of the righteous will rescue them, but the treacherous will be ensnared by their own wickedness.

7 When a wicked person dies, their expectations come to nothing, and the hope of the unjust is utterly destroyed.

8 The one who lives rightly is rescued from distress, while the wicked person takes their place.

9 A deceitful person uses their words to harm their neighbor, but those who are righteous will be rescued through understanding and wisdom.

10 When the righteous prosper, the city celebrates; and when the wicked meet their end, there is joyful exultation.

11 Through the blessings brought by the righteous, a city is lifted up and flourishes; but it is brought to ruin by the words of the wicked.

12 A person who lacks good sense shows contempt for their neighbor, but someone with understanding remains silent.

13 A gossip betrays confidences, but a person of trustworthy character keeps a matter private.

14 When there is no guidance, the people stumble and fall; but where there are many wise advisors, there is security and stability.

15 The one who guarantees a debt for a stranger will suffer for it, but whoever avoids such obligations will remain secure.

16 A woman of grace holds onto her dignity, and mighty men hold onto their wealth.

17 A compassionate person brings benefit to their own being, but one who acts cruelly harms their own self.

18 The wicked person engages in deceitful actions, but for the one who cultivates righteousness, there will be a lasting and certain reward.

19 Righteousness leads to life, but whoever pursues evil is pursuing it to their own destruction.

20 Those who have a deceitful and perverse heart are detestable to the LORD, but those who walk in integrity and righteousness bring Him great joy.

21 Even if the wicked band together and support one another, they will not escape punishment. However, the descendants of the righteous will be rescued and kept safe.

23 The desires of those who are upright lead only to what is good, but the hopes of the wicked result in nothing but anger and judgment.

24 Some people give generously and still grow richer, while others hold back more than they should and end up in poverty.

25 A generous person will prosper, and whoever refreshes others will themselves be refreshed.

26 Whoever hoards grain will be cursed by the people, but blessings will rest on the one who sells it generously.

27 The one who earnestly pursues what is good secures favor, but the one who seeks harm will have it return to him.

28 Whoever places their confidence in their wealth will stumble and fall, but those who live righteously will thrive like a flourishing tree.

29 Whoever brings trouble upon their own household will gain nothing of value, and the foolish will end up serving those who are wise in heart.

30 The actions and influence of a righteous person are like a tree that brings life; and whoever leads others to wisdom and understanding shows true insight.

31 Indeed, the righteous will receive their due reward on earth; how much more will the wicked and the sinner face their just consequences.
