Psalms Chapter 16

1 (A Psalm of David, inscribed as a precious treasure.) Protect me, O God, for I take refuge in you.

2 O my soul, you have declared to the LORD, "You are my Lord; my well-being does not reach up to You."

3 But as for the faithful ones who dwell on the earth, and the honorable, in whom I find all my joy.

4 Those who eagerly pursue other gods will find their troubles greatly increased. I will not present their drink offerings of blood, nor will I even speak the names of their gods.

5 The LORD is my allotted portion and my cup; you sustain my destiny.

6 The boundaries of my life have been set in delightful places; indeed, I possess a beautiful inheritance.

7 I will praise the LORD, who has given me guidance; even in the quiet hours of the night, my innermost thoughts teach me.

8 I continually keep the Lord in my presence; because He stands at my right hand, I will not be shaken.

9 Therefore, my heart is filled with joy, and my soul rejoices; my body too will rest securely in hope.

10 For you will not abandon my soul to the realm of the dead, nor will you allow your faithful one to undergo decay.

11 You will guide me on the path of life; in your presence there is complete joy; at your right hand are eternal delights.
