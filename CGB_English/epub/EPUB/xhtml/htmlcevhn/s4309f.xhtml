Psalms Chapter 25

1 (A Psalm of David.) To You, O LORD, I offer my whole being.

2 O my God, I place my trust in you; do not let me be disgraced, and do not allow my enemies to triumph over me.

3 Indeed, may none who wait for you be put to shame; let those who act treacherously without reason be the ones who are disgraced.

4 Show me your ways, O LORD; instruct me in your paths.

5 Guide me in your truth and instruct me, for you are the God who saves me; all day long I place my hope in you.

6 O LORD, remember your compassionate mercies and your steadfast love, for they have existed from ancient times.

7 Do not hold against me the sins I committed in my youth or the wrongs I have done. Instead, in keeping with Your steadfast love and compassion, remember me, O LORD, for the sake of Your goodness.

8 The LORD is good and righteous; therefore, He instructs sinners in the right path.

9 God will lead the humble in what is right and just, and He will instruct the meek in His ways.

10 All the ways of the LORD are characterized by steadfast love and faithfulness toward those who uphold His covenant and obey His decrees.

11 For the sake of your name, O LORD, forgive my wrongdoing, for it is immense.

12 Who is the person who reveres the LORD? God will instruct them in the path they should follow.

13 His life will be filled with peace and contentment, and his descendants will possess the land.

14 The intimate counsel of the LORD is with those who revere Him, and He will reveal His covenant to them.

15 My eyes are always fixed on the LORD, for he will rescue my feet from the snare.

16 Turn to me and show me your mercy, for I am alone and in distress.

17 The burdens weighing on my heart have grown heavy; please deliver me from my deep distress.

18 Consider my suffering and my distress, and pardon all my transgressions.

19 Pay attention to my enemies, for they are numerous, and they despise me with a fierce and merciless hatred.

20 Guard my life and rescue me; do not let me be put to shame, for I have placed my trust in you.

21 May integrity and uprightness protect me, for I place my hope in you.

22 Rescue Israel, O God, from all its distresses.
