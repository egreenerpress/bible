Psalms Chapter 23

1 (A Psalm of David.) The LORD is my shepherd; I lack nothing.

2 He causes me to rest in lush meadows; he guides me alongside tranquil streams.

3 He renews my spirit; he guides me along the right paths for the sake of his reputation.

4 Even when I walk through the darkest valley, where deathâ€™s shadow looms, I will not fear any harm, for you are with me. Your guiding rod and your protecting staff bring me comfort and assurance.

5 You set a feast before me, even as my enemies look on; you honor me by anointing my head with oil, and my cup overflows with abundance.

6 Certainly, goodness and steadfast love will pursue me every day of my life, and I will live in the presence of the LORD forever.
