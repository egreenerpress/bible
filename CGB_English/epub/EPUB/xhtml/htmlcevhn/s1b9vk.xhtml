Psalms Chapter 101

1 (A Psalm of David.) I will sing of your steadfast love and justice; to you, O LORD, I will sing praises.

2 I will conduct myself with wisdom in a blameless manner. O Lord, when will you come to me? I will walk in my home with integrity and a sincere heart.

3 I will not allow anything evil to come before my eyes; I detest the actions of those who turn away from what is right. Such deeds will have no hold on me.

4 A deceitful heart will be far from me; I will have no association with a wicked person.

5 Whoever secretly spreads slander against their neighbor, I will destroy. As for the one who has a haughty gaze and an arrogant heart, I will not tolerate them.

6 My attention will be focused on the faithful people of the land, so that they may live in my presence. Whoever walks in integrity and follows a blameless path will be the one who serves me.

7 The one who practices deceit will not live in my house; the one who speaks lies will not remain in my presence.

8 I will swiftly eliminate all the wicked from the land, so that I may remove all evildoers from the city of the LORD.
