Psalms Chapter 104

1 Praise the LORD, O my soul. O LORD my God, you are exceedingly great; you are adorned with splendor and majesty.

2 You who clothe yourself in light as if it were a robe, who stretch out the skies like a canopy.

3 Who sets the beams of His heavenly chambers in the waters; Who makes the clouds His chariot; Who moves upon the wings of the wind.

4 He makes his angels like the wind, and his servants like flames of fire.

5 Who established the earth's foundations, so that it would never be shaken or moved from its place?

6 You covered it with the deep as if with a robe; the waters rose above the mountains.

7 At your rebuke, they fled; at the sound of your thunder, they quickly scattered.

8 They ascend the mountains and descend into the valleys, making their way to the place you have established for them.

9 You have established a boundary that they cannot cross; they will not return to cover the earth again.

10 He directs the springs to flow into the valleys, where they wind their way between the mountains.

11 They provide water for all the animals of the field; even the wild donkeys satisfy their thirst.

12 In them, the birds of the sky find their dwelling; they sing from among the branches.

13 He waters the hills from His heavenly dwelling; the earth is abundantly filled with the produce of Your works.

14 He makes the grass grow for the livestock and plants for people to cultivate, so that the earth may produce food.

15 And wine that brings joy to the human heart, and oil that makes the face radiant, and bread that sustains and strengthens the heart.

16 The trees of the LORD are abundantly nourished; the cedars of Lebanon, which He Himself has planted.

18 The towering mountains provide a safe haven for the wild goats, and the rocky cliffs offer shelter for the rock hyraxes.

19 He established the moon to mark the appointed times; the sun knows the time of its setting.

20 You create darkness, and it becomes night, when all the creatures of the forest come out and move about.

21 The young lions roar as they hunt their prey, seeking their food from God.

22 As the sun rises, they gather together and return to their dens to rest.

23 A person goes out to their work and to their labor until the evening.

24 O LORD, how vast and varied are your works! With wisdom you have created them all; the earth overflows with your abundant blessings.

25 This vast and expansive sea is filled with countless creatures, both small and great, that move and swarm within its depths.

26 Here, the ships sail, and there is the great sea creature, Leviathan, which you created to frolic in the waters.

27 All of them look to you, that you may provide them with food at the proper time.

28 What you provide, they gather; when you open your hand, they are satisfied with abundance.

29 When you hide your face, they are dismayed; when you take away their breath, they perish and return to the dust.

30 You send out your Spirit, and they are brought into being; you restore and renew the face of the earth.

31 The splendor of the LORD will last forever; the LORD will take delight in His creations.

32 He gazes upon the earth, and it quakes; he touches the mountains, and they emit smoke.

33 I will sing to the LORD all my life; I will praise my God as long as I exist.

34 My thoughts of Him will bring me joy; I will find delight in the LORD.

35 May the sinners be removed from the earth, and may the wicked cease to exist. Bless the LORD, O my soul. Praise the LORD.
