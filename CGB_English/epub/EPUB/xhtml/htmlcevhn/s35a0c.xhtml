Numbers Chapter 33

1 This is the account of the journeys of the Israelites, who departed from the land of Egypt in organized groups under the leadership of Moses and Aaron.

2 Moses recorded the stages of their travels as they departed from place to place, following the command of the LORD. These are the stages of their journeys as they traveled onward.

3 They set out from Rameses in the first month, on the fifteenth day of that month, the day after the Passover. The Israelites marched out boldly and triumphantly, in full view of all the Egyptians.

4 For the Egyptians buried all their firstborn, whom the LORD had struck down among them. The LORD also carried out judgments against their gods.

5 The Israelites departed from Rameses and set up camp in Succoth.

6 They set out from Succoth and camped at Etham, located on the border of the wilderness.

7 They departed from Etham and turned back toward Pi-hahiroth, which lies east of Baal-zephon, and they camped near Migdol.

8 They left Pi-hahiroth and traveled through the middle of the sea into the wilderness. After a three-day journey in the wilderness of Etham, they camped at Marah.

9 They departed from Marah and arrived at Elim, where there were twelve springs of water and seventy palm trees. They camped there.

10 They departed from Elim and set up camp by the Red Sea.

11 They departed from the Red Sea and set up camp in the wilderness of Sin.

12 They set out from the wilderness of Sin and made camp at Dophkah.

13 They left Dophkah and set up camp in Alush.

14 They departed from Alush and set up camp at Rephidim, but there was no water there for the people to drink.

15 They left Rephidim and set up camp in the wilderness of Sinai.

16 They departed from the wilderness of Sinai and set up camp at Kibroth-hattaavah.

17 They left Kibroth-hattaavah and set up camp at Hazeroth.

18 They left Hazeroth and set up camp at Rithmah.

19 They left Rithmah and set up camp at Rimmon-parez.

20 They left Rimmon-parez and set up camp in Libnah.

21 They departed from Libnah and set up camp at Rissah.

22 They traveled from Rissah and set up camp at Kehelathah.

23 They departed from Kehelathah and set up camp at Mount Shapher.

24 They departed from Mount Shapher and set up camp in Haradah.

25 They departed from Haradah and camped at Makheloth.

26 They departed from Makheloth and set up camp at Tahath.

27 They left Tahath and set up camp at Tarah.

28 They departed from Tarah and camped at Mithcah.

29 They departed from Mithcah and camped at Hashmonah.

30 They left Hashmonah and set up camp at Moseroth.

31 They left Moseroth and set up camp at Bene-jaakan.

32 They departed from Bene-jaakan and set up camp at Hor-hagidgad.

33 They departed from Hor-hagidgad and set up camp in Jotbathah.

34 They departed from Jotbathah and set up camp at Ebronah.

35 They left Ebronah and set up camp at Ezion-geber.

36 They departed from Ezion-geber and camped in the wilderness of Zin, that is, Kadesh.

37 They departed from Kadesh and camped at Mount Hor, on the border of the land of Edom.

38 Aaron the priest ascended Mount Hor as the LORD had commanded, and there he died. This occurred in the fortieth year after the Israelites had left the land of Egypt, on the first day of the fifth month.

39 Aaron was one hundred and twenty-three years old when he died on Mount Hor.

40 King Arad, the Canaanite ruler who lived in the southern region of the land of Canaan, heard about the approach of the Israelites.

41 They left Mount Hor and camped at Zalmonah.

42 They left Zalmonah and set up camp at Punon.

43 They left Punon and set up camp in Oboth.

44 They left Oboth and set up camp at Iye-abarim, on the border of Moab.

45 They left Iim and set up camp at Dibon-gad.

46 They departed from Dibon-gad and set up camp at Almon-diblathaim.

47 They departed from Almon-diblathaim and camped in the mountains of Abarim, near Nebo.

48 They left the mountains of Abarim and camped in the plains of Moab, by the Jordan River near Jericho.

49 They set up camp by the Jordan River, from Beth-jesimoth all the way to Abel-shittim, in the plains of Moab.

50 The LORD spoke to Moses in the plains of Moab, by the Jordan River near Jericho, saying,

51 Speak to the Israelites and tell them: When you have crossed the Jordan River and entered the land of Canaan,

52 You must expel all the inhabitants of the land before you, demolish all their carved images, destroy all their cast idols, and completely tear down all their sacred high places.

53 You will drive out the inhabitants of the land and settle there, for I have given you the land to take possession of it.

54 You shall distribute the land by lot as an inheritance among your families. You shall inherit according to the tribes of your ancestors.

55 But if you do not drive out the inhabitants of the land from before you, then those whom you allow to remain will become like sharp thorns in your eyes and painful barbs in your sides. They will cause you trouble and distress in the land where you live.

56 Furthermore, it will happen that I will deal with you just as I intended to deal with them.
