Psalms Chapter 135

1 Praise the LORD! Praise the name of the LORD; give praise, you who serve the LORD.

2 You who stand in the house of the LORD, in the courtyards of the house of our God,

3 Praise the LORD, for the LORD is good; sing praises to His name, for it is delightful and fitting.

4 For the LORD has chosen Jacob to be His own, and Israel as His special possession.

5 I am fully aware that the LORD is supreme, and that our Lord surpasses all other gods in power and authority.

6 Whatever the LORD desires, that He accomplishes in heaven, on earth, in the seas, and in all the depths.

7 He causes the mists to rise from the farthest reaches of the earth; he creates lightning to accompany the rain; he brings forth the wind from his storehouses.

8 Who struck down the firstborn of Egypt, both human and animal.

9 Who performed signs and miracles in your midst, O Egypt, against Pharaoh and all his officials.

10 Who struck down great nations and killed powerful kings;

11 Sihon, the king of the Amorites, and Og, the king of Bashan, along with all the kingdoms of Canaan.

12 And He gave their land as an inheritance, an inheritance to Israel, His people.

13 Your name, O LORD, remains forever; your renown, O LORD, endures through all generations.

14 For the LORD will bring judgment upon His people, and He will relent concerning His servants.

15 The idols worshipped by the nations are made of silver and gold, crafted by human hands.

16 They have mouths, but they cannot speak; they have eyes, but they cannot see.

17 They have ears, but they cannot hear; there is no breath at all in their mouths.

18 Those who create idols become like them; the same is true for all who place their trust in them.

19 Praise the LORD, all you of the house of Israel; praise the LORD, all you of the house of Aaron.

20 Praise the LORD, O house of Levi; all you who revere the LORD, give Him praise.

21 Praise be to the LORD, who reigns from Zion and dwells in Jerusalem. Let all people praise the LORD!
