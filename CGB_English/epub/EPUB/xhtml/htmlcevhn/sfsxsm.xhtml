Jeremiah Chapter 47

1 This is the message from the LORD that came to Jeremiah the prophet concerning the Philistines, before Pharaoh attacked Gaza.

2 This is what the LORD says: "Look, waters will rise up from the north, becoming a raging flood. They will overwhelm the land and everything in itâ€”the cities and those who live in them. Then the people will cry out, and all who dwell in the land will wail in despair."

3 At the thunderous sound of his mighty horses' hooves pounding the ground, the roaring of his chariots charging forward, and the deep rumble of their wheels, fathers will not even turn back to look for their children, for their hands will be too weak with fear.

4 For the day is coming when the Lord will bring devastation upon all the Philistines and will cut off from Tyre and Sidon every remaining ally. The Lord will destroy the Philistines, the remnant from the land of Caphtor.

5 Gaza has been left desolate; Ashkelon is destroyed, along with the remnants of their valley. How long will you continue to harm yourself in mourning?

6 O sword of the LORD, how long will you continue to strike? Return to your sheath; cease your work, rest, and be still.

7 How can it remain calm when the LORD has commanded it to act against Ashkelon and the coastal regions? There He has determined its purpose.
