Psalms Chapter 19

1 (For the music director, a psalm of David.) The heavens proclaim the glory of God, and the sky above reveals the work of his hands.

2 Day after day pours forth speech, and night after night reveals understanding.

3 There is no tongue or dialect in which their voice is not understood.

4 Their message has gone out to all the earth, and their words to the farthest reaches of the world. In them, He has established a dwelling place for the sun.

5 It is like a bridegroom emerging from his wedding chamber, full of joy, or like a champion exulting as he prepares to run his race.

6 His movement begins at one end of the heavens and completes its course to the other end; nothing escapes the reach of its warmth.

7 The instruction of the LORD is flawless, restoring the inner being; the declaration of the LORD is trustworthy, giving wisdom to the inexperienced.

8 The decrees of the LORD are just, bringing joy to the heart; the instruction of the LORD is flawless, giving clarity to the mind.

9 Reverence for the LORD is pure, lasting forever. The decrees of the LORD are trustworthy and completely just.

10 They are more desirable than gold, even the purest gold; they are sweeter than honey, even the finest honey from the comb.

11 Furthermore, your servant is instructed and guided by them; and in following them, there is abundant blessing and benefit.

12 Who can fully grasp their own mistakes? Purify me from the hidden faults I cannot see.

13 Restrain your servant from willful sins; do not let them rule over me. Then I will be blameless and free from the guilt of great rebellion.

14 May the words I speak and the thoughts of my heart be pleasing to you, O LORD, my source of strength and my redeemer.
