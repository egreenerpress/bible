Revelation Chapter 22

1 Then he showed me a pure river of the water of life, shining like crystal, flowing from the throne of God and of the Lamb.

2 In the middle of the city’s main street, and on both sides of the river, stood the tree of life. It produced twelve kinds of fruit, yielding its fruit each month. The leaves of the tree were for the healing and restoration of the nations.

3 And there will no longer be any curse. The throne of God and of the Lamb will be present there, and his servants will worship and serve him.

4 And they will see his face, and his name will be marked on their foreheads.

5 There will be no night there, and they will not need a lamp or the light of the sun, for the Lord God will give them light. And they will reign forever and ever.

6 He said to me, "These words are trustworthy and true. The Lord God, who inspires the prophets, has sent His angel to reveal to His servants what must soon take place."

7 Listen carefully, I am coming soon. Blessed is the one who faithfully obeys the words of the prophecy written in this book.

8 I, John, witnessed these things and heard them. After I had both heard and seen them, I fell down to worship at the feet of the angel who had shown me these things.

9 Then he said to me, "Do not do that! For I am your fellow servant, and a brother among the prophets, and among those who keep the words of this book. Worship God."

10 And he said to me, "Do not keep the words of this prophetic book hidden, for the time of their fulfillment is near."

11 Let the one who is unjust continue in their injustice, and the one who is morally defiled remain in their defilement. Likewise, let the one who is righteous persist in doing what is right, and the one who is holy continue in their holiness.

12 Listen carefully, I am coming soon, and I bring my reward with me. I will repay each person according to what they have done.

13 I am the Alpha and the Omega, the origin and the conclusion, the first and the ultimate.

14 Blessed are those who obey His commands, for they will have the right to partake of the tree of life and may enter through the gates into the city.

15 Outside are the dogs, the practitioners of sorcery, the sexually immoral, the murderers, the idolaters, and everyone who loves and practices falsehood.

16 I, Jesus, have sent my angel to declare these things to you among the churches. I am both the source and the descendant of David, and I am the radiant morning star.

17 The Spirit and the bride say, “Come.” And let the one who hears also say, “Come.” Let the one who is thirsty come, and whoever desires, let them take the water of life without cost.

18 I solemnly declare to everyone who hears the words of this prophetic book: If anyone adds anything to these words, God will add to that person the plagues described in this book.

19 If anyone removes or alters any of the words contained in this prophetic book, God will remove that person’s share from the book of life, from the holy city, and from all the blessings described in this book.

20 The one who testifies to these things declares, "Surely, I am coming soon." Amen. Come, Lord Jesus!

21 May the gracious favor of our Lord Jesus Christ be with all of you. Amen.
