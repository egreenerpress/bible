Psalms Chapter 31

1 (For the director of music. A psalm of David.) Lord, I place my complete trust in you; may I never be disgraced. Rescue me in your justice and righteousness.

2 Listen closely to me; rescue me quickly. Be my solid refuge, a secure fortress to save me.

3 For you are my solid foundation and my stronghold; therefore, for the sake of your reputation, lead me and direct my path.

4 Rescue me from the hidden trap they have set for me, for you are my source of strength.

5 Into your hands I entrust my spirit; you have delivered me, O LORD, God of faithfulness.

6 I despise those who put their trust in worthless falsehoods, but I place my confidence in the LORD.

7 I will be joyful and celebrate your steadfast love, for you have taken notice of my distress; you have understood the depths of my soul in times of hardship.

8 You have not handed me over to the power of the enemy; instead, you have placed me in a spacious and secure place.

9 Show me mercy, O LORD, for I am in distress; my eyes are worn out from sorrow, and my soul and body are deeply afflicted.

10 My life is filled with sorrow, and my years are marked by constant sighing. My strength fails because of my wrongdoing, and my body is wasting away.

11 I was an object of disgrace among all my enemies, but especially among my neighbors, and a source of dread to those who knew me. Those who saw me outside fled from me.

12 I am forgotten, like someone who has died and faded from memory; I am like a shattered piece of pottery, useless and discarded.

13 I have heard the accusations of many; terror surrounds me. While they conspire together against me, they plot to take my life.

14 I placed my trust in you, O LORD; I declared, "You are my God."

15 "My life and circumstances are in your control; rescue me from the power of my enemies and from those who pursue me."

16 Let your favor shine upon your servant; deliver me because of your steadfast love.

17 O LORD, do not let me be put to shame, for I have called out to you. May the wicked be brought to shame instead, and may they be silenced in the grave.

18 Silence the deceitful lips that arrogantly and scornfully speak harsh words against those who are upright and just.

19 Oh, how immense is your goodness, which you have stored up for those who revere you! You have accomplished this for those who place their trust in you, even in the presence of all people.

20 You will conceal them in the shelter of your presence, safe from the arrogance of humanity. You will protect them in a secure refuge, shielding them from the conflict of malicious words.

21 Praise the LORD, for He has demonstrated His extraordinary kindness to me within a fortified city.

22 In my moment of panic, I thought, "I am completely cut off from your presence." Yet you listened to the sound of my desperate pleas when I cried out to you.

23 Love the LORD, all you who are his faithful ones, for the LORD protects those who are loyal to him, but he fully repays those who act with arrogance.

24 Take courage, and He will strengthen your heart, all of you who place your hope in the LORD.
