Psalms Chapter 70

1 (For the director of music. A psalm of David, intended for remembrance.) Hurry, O God, to rescue me; come quickly, O LORD, to help me.

2 May those who seek to take my life be disgraced and dismayed; may they be driven back and brought to shame, those who plot my harm.

3 Let them be turned back in disgrace, those who mockingly say, "Aha, aha!"

4 May all who seek you be filled with joy and gladness because of you; and may those who cherish your deliverance always declare, "Let God be exalted!"

5 I am poor and in desperate need; hurry to me, O God! You are my help and my rescuer; O LORD, do not delay!
