Proverbs Chapter 4

1 Listen, my children, to a father’s teaching, and pay attention so that you may gain understanding.

2 For I am offering you sound and beneficial instruction; do not abandon or neglect my teachings.

3 For I was my father’s son, cherished and dearly loved, the only one in my mother’s eyes.

4 He instructed me and said, "Hold firmly to my words in your heart; follow my commands, and you will live."

5 Acquire wisdom, gain understanding; do not forget it, nor turn away from the words I speak.

6 Do not abandon wisdom, and she will protect you; cherish her, and she will guard you.

7 Wisdom is of utmost importance; therefore, pursue wisdom above all else. And in all your pursuits, make sure to also seek understanding.

8 Honor wisdom, and it will elevate you; it will bring you distinction when you wholeheartedly embrace it.

9 She will bestow upon your head a graceful adornment; she will present you with a glorious crown.

10 Listen, my child, and accept my teachings; then the years of your life will be many.

11 I have instructed you in the ways of wisdom; I have guided you along the paths of righteousness.

12 When you walk, your path will not be hindered; and when you run, you will not stumble.

13 Firmly grasp instruction and do not let it go; hold tightly to it, for it is your very life.

14 Do not set foot on the path of the wicked, nor walk in the way of those who do evil.

15 Stay away from it; do not go near it. Turn aside from it and keep moving forward.

16 For they cannot rest unless they have done harm; their sleep is disturbed unless they have caused someone to stumble.

17 For they consume the fruit of their wrongdoing and indulge in the drink of their cruelty.

18 The way of the righteous is like the first light of dawn, growing brighter and brighter until it reaches the fullness of day.

19 The path of the wicked is like deep darkness; they do not recognize what causes them to stumble.

20 My child, pay close attention to my words; listen carefully to what I say.

21 Do not let them fade from your sight; hold them firmly within the core of your heart.

22 For they bring life to those who discover them, and healing to their entire being.

23 Guard your heart with utmost care, for from it flow the very springs of life.

24 Remove from yourself a deceitful tongue, and keep dishonest speech far from you.

25 Keep your gaze fixed directly ahead, and let your eyes focus straight before you.

26 Consider carefully the direction of your life, and ensure that all your actions are guided by steadfast principles.

27 Do not turn aside to the right or to the left; keep your steps away from wrongdoing.
