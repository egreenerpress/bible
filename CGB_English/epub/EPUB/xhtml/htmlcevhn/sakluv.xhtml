Song of Songs Chapter 5

1 I have entered my garden, my sister, my bride; I have gathered my myrrh along with my fragrant spices. I have eaten my honeycomb with its honey; I have drunk my wine mixed with milk. Eat, my friends; drink deeply, yes, drink your fill, my beloved.

2 I am asleep, but my heart is awake. It is the voice of my beloved, who is knocking and saying, "Open to me, my sister, my love, my dove, my pure one. For my head is drenched with dew, and my hair is wet with the moisture of the night."

3 I have taken off my robe; how can I put it back on? I have washed my feet; how can I dirty them again?

4 My beloved reached his hand through the opening of the door, and my heart was deeply stirred within me for him.

5 I got up to open the door for my beloved; my hands were dripping with myrrh, and my fingers were coated with fragrant myrrh, flowing over the handles of the lock.

6 I opened the door for my beloved, but my beloved had already withdrawn and left. My heart sank when he spoke; I searched for him, but I could not find him. I called out to him, but he did not answer me.

7 The guards who patrol the city found me; they struck me, they injured me; the sentinels of the walls took my veil from me.

8 I urge you, daughters of Jerusalem, if you find my beloved, tell him this: I am faint with love.

9 What makes your beloved stand out above all others, O most beautiful among women? What sets your beloved apart from any other, that you urge us so earnestly?

10 My beloved is radiant and ruddy, distinguished above ten thousand.

11 His head is like the purest gold, his hair is thick and dark as a raven's feathers.

12 His eyes are like doves beside streams of water, gleaming as if washed with milk, perfectly placed and radiant.

13 His cheeks are like a garden of fragrant spices, a bed of aromatic blossoms; his lips are like lilies, gently releasing the sweet scent of myrrh.

14 His hands are like golden rings adorned with beryl; his abdomen gleams like polished ivory, embellished with sapphires.

15 His legs are like pillars of marble, standing firm on bases of pure gold. His appearance is majestic, like the towering cedars of Lebanon, splendid and enduring.

16 His words are utterly delightful, and he is completely beautiful in every way. This is the one I love, my dearest companion. O daughters of Jerusalem, this is my beloved and my closest friend.
