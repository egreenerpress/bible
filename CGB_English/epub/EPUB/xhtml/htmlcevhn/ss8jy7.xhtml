Psalms Chapter 83

1 (A song or psalm by Asaph.) O God, do not remain silent; do not hold back your voice or stay inactive, O God.

2 Behold, your enemies are in uproar, and those who despise you raise their heads in defiance.

3 They have devised cunning plans against your people and plotted against those you protect in secret.

4 They have declared, "Come, let us destroy them as a nation, so that the name of Israel will no longer be remembered."

5 They have made plans together in complete agreement; they have formed an alliance against you.

6 The tents of Edom and the Ishmaelites, the people of Moab and the Hagrites...

7 Gebal, Ammon, and Amalek; the Philistines along with the people of Tyre;

8 Assyria has also allied with them; they have aided the descendants of Lot. Pause and reflect.

9 Deal with them as you dealt with the Midianites; as you did to Sisera and Jabin at the brook of Kishon.

10 They perished at En-dor; they became like manure for the ground.

11 Cause their leaders to suffer the same fate as Oreb and Zeeb, and let all their rulers meet the end of Zebah and Zalmunna.

12 Who said, "Let us seize the dwellings of God for ourselves."

13 O my God, cause them to be like a whirling wheel, like chaff scattered before the wind.

14 As fire consumes a forest, and as flames set the mountains ablaze;

15 Pursue them with your tempest, and terrify them with your storm.

16 Cause them to be overwhelmed with disgrace, so that they may turn and seek your name, O LORD.

17 Let them be utterly dismayed and thrown into confusion forever; indeed, let them be disgraced and brought to ruin.

18 So that all people may recognize that you, whose name alone is the LORD, are the Most High, sovereign over all the earth.
