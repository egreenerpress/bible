Psalms Chapter 120

1 (A song of ascents.) In my time of trouble, I called out to the LORD, and He answered me.

2 Rescue me, O LORD, from the words of falsehood and from the speech of deception.

3 What will be given to you? Or what will be done to you, you deceitful tongue?

4 The sharp arrows of a warrior, accompanied by burning coals from the juniper tree.

5 How sorrowful I am, that I must live among the people of Mesech, that I must reside in the tents of Kedar!

6 My soul has endured a prolonged stay with those who despise peace.

7 I seek peace, but when I speak, they are eager for war.
