Titus Chapter 3

1 Remind them to submit to governing authorities and rulers, to obey those in positions of authority, and to be prepared to engage in every kind of good deed.

2 Do not speak ill of anyone, avoid quarrels, and be gentle, demonstrating humility and kindness toward all people.

3 For we too were once foolish, disobedient, and misled, enslaved by various desires and pleasures. We lived in malice and envy, being hateful and hating one another.

4 But when the kindness and love of God our Savior toward humanity was revealed,

5 He saved us not because of any righteous deeds we had done, but because of His mercy. He saved us through the cleansing of rebirth and the renewing work of the Holy Spirit.

6 This grace, which he poured out on us richly through Jesus Christ our Savior.

7 Since we have been declared righteous by His grace, we are made heirs in accordance with the hope of eternal life.

8 This is a trustworthy statement, and I want you to confidently affirm these truths, so that those who have put their faith in God may devote themselves to doing good works. Such things are excellent and beneficial for everyone.

9 But steer clear of pointless debates, endless genealogies, arguments, and quarrels about the law, for they are useless and empty.

10 A person who promotes divisive teachings, after being warned once and then a second time, should be avoided.

11 Understand that such a person is corrupt and continues to sin, having already condemned themselves by their own actions.

12 When I send Artemas to you, or Tychicus, make every effort to come to me in Nicopolis, for I have decided to spend the winter there.

13 Ensure that Zenas the lawyer and Apollos are well provided for as they continue their journey, so that they lack nothing they may need.

14 And let our people also learn to devote themselves to good works, meeting pressing needs, so that they may not be unproductive.

15 All those who are with me send their greetings to you. Extend my greetings to those who love us in the faith. May grace be with all of you. Amen.
