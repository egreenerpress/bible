Psalms Chapter 14

1 (For the music director, a psalm of David.) The fool declares in his heart, "There is no God." They are corrupt, their actions are detestable; there is no one who does what is good.

2 The LORD gazed down from heaven upon humanity, to observe whether there were any who truly comprehended and earnestly sought after God.

3 Everyone has turned away; they have all become corrupt. There is no one who does good, not even one.

4 Do all those who do evil lack understanding? They devour my people as if they were consuming bread, yet they do not call upon the LORD.

5 They were overwhelmed with terror, for God is present among the righteous generation.

6 You have disgraced the plans of the poor, for the LORD is their shelter.

7 If only deliverance for Israel would come from Zion! When the LORD restores the fortunes of His people, Jacob will celebrate, and Israel will be filled with joy.
