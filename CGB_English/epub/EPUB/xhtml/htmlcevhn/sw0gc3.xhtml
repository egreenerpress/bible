Psalms Chapter 28

1 (A Psalm of David.) I cry out to you, O LORD, my rock; do not remain silent before me. For if you do not answer me, I will be like those who descend into the grave.

2 Listen to the sound of my pleas when I call out to you, when I raise my hands in prayer toward your sacred sanctuary.

3 Do not let me be swept away with the wicked or counted among those who do evilâ€”those who speak kindly to their neighbors while harboring malice in their hearts.

4 Repay them according to their actions and the evil of their deeds. Reward them according to what their hands have done; give them what their conduct deserves.

5 Because they pay no attention to the works of the LORD, nor consider the deeds of his hands, he will tear them down and not rebuild them.

6 Praise be to the LORD, for He has listened to the sound of my prayers.

7 The LORD is my source of strength and my protection; my heart has placed its trust in Him, and I have been delivered. Therefore, my heart overflows with joy, and I will praise Him with my song.

8 The LORD is their source of power, and He is the mighty deliverer of His chosen one.

9 Rescue your people and bless those who belong to you. Sustain them and uphold them forever.
