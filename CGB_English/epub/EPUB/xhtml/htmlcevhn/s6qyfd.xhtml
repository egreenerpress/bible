Psalms Chapter 26

1 (A Psalm of David.) Lord, defend my cause, for I have lived with integrity; I have placed my trust in you, and I will not falter.

2 Test me, O LORD, and examine me; refine my innermost thoughts and my heart.

3 For your steadfast love is ever before me, and I have lived according to your truth.

4 I have not associated with worthless people, nor will I join in with deceitful individuals.

5 I despise the gathering of those who do evil, and I refuse to associate with the wicked.

6 I will cleanse my hands in purity, and in this state, I will approach your altar, O LORD.

7 So that I may proclaim with a voice full of gratitude and declare all your marvelous deeds.

8 LORD, I have cherished the dwelling place of your house, and the sanctuary where your glorious presence resides.

9 Do not take my life along with the wicked, nor let me be counted among those who shed blood.

10 In whose hands are schemes of wrongdoing, and their right hand is filled with bribes.

11 As for me, I will live with integrity. Rescue me and show me your mercy.

12 My feet stand on level ground; in the assembly of the people, I will praise the LORD.
