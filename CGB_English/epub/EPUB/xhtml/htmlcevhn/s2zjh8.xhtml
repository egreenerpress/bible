Hosea Chapter 14

1 O Israel, turn back to the LORD your God, for you have stumbled because of your sin.

2 Take your words with you and return to the LORD. Say to him, "Forgive all our wrongdoing, and accept us with favor. Then we will offer the praise of our lips as a sacrifice."

3 Assyria will not save us; we will not rely on horses for deliverance. Nor will we ever again say to the works of our hands, "You are our gods," for in you, the fatherless find compassion.

4 I will restore them from their waywardness and love them with complete generosity, for my anger has turned away from them.

5 I will be like the dew to Israel: they will flourish like the lily and send down their roots deeply, as sturdy as the cedars of Lebanon.

6 His branches will extend far and wide, and his splendor will be like that of the olive tree, with its enduring beauty and vitality. His fragrance will be as rich and refreshing as the forests of Lebanon, renowned for their aromatic cedars.

7 Those who live under his protection will flourish; they will revive like grain and grow like the vine. Their fragrance will be like the fine wine of Lebanon.

8 Ephraim will declare, "What do I have to do with idols anymore? I have listened to God and paid attention to Him. I am like a flourishing evergreen tree; your fruit comes from me."

9 Who is wise and will understand these things? Who is discerning and will grasp their meaning? For the ways of the LORD are just, and the righteous will walk in them, but the rebellious will stumble and fall.
