Psalms Chapter 150

1 Praise the LORD. Praise God in his holy dwelling; praise him in the vast expanse of his mighty heavens.

2 Praise Him for His powerful deeds; praise Him in accordance with His surpassing greatness.

3 Honor him with the blast of the trumpet; honor him with the music of the lyre and harp.

4 Praise Him with the tambourine and dancing; praise Him with stringed instruments and flutes.

5 Praise Him with the resounding clash of cymbals; praise Him with the loud, triumphant clang of cymbals.

6 Let everything that has breath praise the LORD. Praise the LORD!
