Psalms Chapter 95

1 Come, let us sing to the LORD; let us shout joyfully to the rock who saves us.

2 Let us approach his presence with gratitude, and offer him joyful praise through songs of worship.

3 For the LORD is a mighty God, a supreme King who reigns over all other gods.

4 In His hand lie the vast depths of the earth; the might of the mountains belongs to Him as well.

5 The sea belongs to him, for he created it, and his hands shaped the dry land.

6 Come, let us worship and humble ourselves; let us kneel in reverence before the LORD, our Creator.

7 For He is our God, and we are the people He shepherds, the flock under His care. Today, if you will listen to His voice,

8 Do not harden your heart, as you did in the rebellion, during the time of testing in the wilderness.

9 When your ancestors tested me, challenged me, and witnessed my deeds.

10 For forty years I was deeply troubled by this generation, and I declared, "They are a people whose hearts go astray; they have not understood my ways."

11 To whom I swore in my anger that they would not enter into my place of rest.
