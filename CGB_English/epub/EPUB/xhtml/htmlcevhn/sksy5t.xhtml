Psalms Chapter 80

1 (For the director of music, to be played on the "Lilies of the Covenant," a psalm of Asaph.) Listen, O Shepherd of Israel, You who guide Joseph like a flock; You who are enthroned above the cherubim, reveal Your glory.

2 Before the tribes of Ephraim, Benjamin, and Manasseh, awaken your mighty power and come to rescue us.

3 Restore us, O God, and let your presence shine upon us; then we will be delivered.

4 O LORD, God of armies, how long will you remain angry and turn away from the prayers of your people?

5 You provide them with sorrow as their food, and you give them abundant tears to drink.

6 You have made us a source of conflict for our neighbors, and our enemies mock us among themselves.

7 Restore us, O God of armies, and let your face shine upon us; then we will be delivered.

8 You brought a vine out of Egypt; you drove out the nations and planted it.

9 You prepared the ground for it, allowing it to take deep root, and it spread to cover the land.

10 The hills were cloaked in its shade, and its branches were as majestic as the finest cedars.

11 She extended her branches toward the sea and her shoots toward the river.

12 Why have you torn down her protective walls, so that everyone who passes by can plunder her?

13 The wild boar from the forest ravages it, and the beasts of the field consume it.

14 Turn back to us, we earnestly pray, O God of armies. Gaze down from heaven, observe us, and care for this vine.

15 The vineyard that your right hand has planted, and the shoot that you have nurtured and strengthened for yourself.

16 It is consumed by fire, it is cut down; they are destroyed by the power of your presence.

17 May your hand rest upon the one you have chosen, the one you have strengthened for your own purposeâ€”the son of humanity whom you have empowered.

18 We will not turn away from you; revive us, and we will call on your name.

19 Restore us, O LORD God of hosts; let your face shine upon us, and we will be saved.
