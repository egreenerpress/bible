I Chronicles Chapter 4

1 The descendants of Judah were Perez, Hezron, Carmi, Hur, and Shobal.

2 Reaiah, the son of Shobal, became the father of Jahath; and Jahath became the father of Ahumai and Lahad. These are the clans of the Zorathites.

3 These were the descendants of Etam: Jezreel, Ishma, and Idbash. Their sister’s name was Hazelelponi.

4 Penuel was the father of Gedor, and Ezer was the father of Hushah. These were the descendants of Hur, the firstborn of Ephrathah, who was the ancestor of Bethlehem.

5 Ashur, the father of Tekoa, had two wives: Helah and Naarah.

6 Naarah gave birth to Ahuzam, Hepher, Temeni, and Haahashtari. These were the sons of Naarah.

7 The children of Helah were Zereth, Jezoar, and Ethnan.

8 Coz was the father of Anub, Zobebah, and the families of Aharhel, the son of Harum.

9 Jabez was more distinguished than his brothers. His mother named him Jabez, saying, “I gave birth to him in pain.”

10 Jabez prayed to the God of Israel, saying, "Please bless me abundantly and expand my territory. May your hand be with me, and protect me from harm so that I may not suffer pain." And God granted him what he asked for.

11 Chelub, the brother of Shuah, became the father of Mehir, who was the father of Eshton.

12 Eshton was the father of Beth-rapha, Paseah, and Tehinnah, who was the father of Ir-nahash. These were the descendants of Rechah.

13 The descendants of Kenaz were Othniel and Seraiah. Othniel’s son was Hathath.

14 Meonothai was the father of Ophrah, and Seraiah was the father of Joab, who founded the community in the Valley of Charashim, for they were skilled craftsmen.

15 The descendants of Caleb, the son of Jephunneh, were Iru, Elah, and Naam. The sons of Elah included Kenaz.

16 The sons of Jehaleleel were Ziph, Ziphah, Tiria, and Asareel.

17 The sons of Ezra were Jether, Mered, Epher, and Jalon. His wife also gave birth to Miriam, Shammai, and Ishbah, who became the father of Eshtemoa.

18 And his wife Jehudijah bore Jered, who became the father of Gedor, and Heber, who became the father of Socho, and Jekuthiel, who became the father of Zanoah. These were the sons of Bithiah, the daughter of Pharaoh, whom Mered married.

19 The sons of his wife Hodiah, who was the sister of Naham (the father of Keilah the Garmite), were Eshtemoa the Maachathite.

20 The sons of Shimon were Amnon, Rinnah, Ben-hanan, and Tilon. The sons of Ishi were Zoheth and Ben-zoheth.

21 The descendants of Shelah, the son of Judah, were Er, the ancestor of Lecah; Laadah, the ancestor of Mareshah; and the families of the house of those who crafted fine linen, belonging to the house of Ashbea.

22 Jokim, the men of Chozeba, Joash, and Saraph, who held authority in Moab, along with Jashubi-lehem. These are matters from ancient times.

23 These were the potters, and those who lived in the fields and among the hedges; they resided there with the king to carry out his work.

24 The descendants of Simeon were Nemuel, Jamin, Jarib, Zerah, and Shaul.

25 Shallum was his son, Mibsam was his son, and Mishma was his son.

26 The descendants of Mishma were: his son Hamuel, Hamuel's son Zacchur, and Zacchur's son Shimei.

27 Shimei had sixteen sons and six daughters, but his brothers did not have many children, nor did their entire family increase in number as the descendants of Judah did.

28 They settled in Beersheba, Moladah, and Hazar-shual.

29 And at Bilhah, and at Ezem, and at Tolad,

30 And in Bethuel, and in Hormah, and in Ziklag,

31 And in Beth-marcaboth, Hazar-susim, Beth-birei, and Shaaraim. These were their cities until the time of David's reign.

32 Their villages included Etam, Ain, Rimmon, Tochen, and Ashan—five towns in total.

33 All the surrounding villages near these cities extended as far as Baal. These were their settlements, and this is their family lineage.

34 And Meshobab, Jamlech, and Joshah, the son of Amaziah,

35 And Joel, along with Jehu the son of Josibiah, who was the son of Seraiah, who was the son of Asiel.

36 And Elioenai, Jaakobah, Jeshohaiah, Asaiah, Adiel, Jesimiel, and Benaiah.

37 And Ziza, the son of Shiphi, who was the son of Allon, who was the son of Jedaiah, who was the son of Shimri, who was the son of Shemaiah.

38 The individuals listed by name were leaders among their families, and their ancestral households grew significantly in number.

39 They traveled to the entrance of Gedor, reaching the eastern side of the valley, in search of grazing land for their flocks.

40 They discovered rich and fertile grazing land, a spacious territory that was tranquil and peaceful. This was because the descendants of Ham had settled there long ago.

41 These men, whose names are recorded, came during the reign of Hezekiah, king of Judah. They attacked the tents and settlements they found there, completely destroying them to this very day, and settled in their place because there was good pastureland for their flocks.

42 And some of them, specifically from the descendants of Simeon, five hundred men, journeyed to Mount Seir. They were led by their commanders: Pelatiah, Neariah, Rephaiah, and Uzziel, who were the sons of Ishi.

43 They struck down the remaining Amalekites who had survived, and they settled there, remaining in that place up to the present day.
