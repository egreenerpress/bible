Jonah Chapter 2

1 Then Jonah prayed to the LORD his God from inside the belly of the fish.

2 And I said, "In my distress, I called out to the LORD, and He answered me. From the depths of the grave, I cried for help, and You heard my voice."

3 For you had thrown me into the depths, into the heart of the seas; the waters surrounded me, and your currents swept over me. All your breakers and waves rolled over me.

4 Then I said, "I have been banished from your presence; yet I will once again turn my gaze toward your holy temple."

5 The waters surrounded me, reaching even to my very life; the depths engulfed me completely, and seaweed entangled my head.

6 I descended to the depths of the mountains; the earthâ€™s bars closed around me forever. Yet you, O LORD my God, have raised my life from the pit of destruction.

7 When my spirit grew weak within me, I remembered the LORD, and my prayer reached out to you, entering your holy temple.

8 Those who cling to worthless idols abandon the mercy that could be theirs.

9 But I will offer sacrifices to you with a voice filled with thanksgiving; I will fulfill the vows I have made. Deliverance comes from the LORD.

10 The LORD commanded the fish, and it expelled Jonah onto the dry land.
