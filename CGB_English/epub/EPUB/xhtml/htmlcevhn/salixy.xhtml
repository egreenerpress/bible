Psalms Chapter 1

1 Blessed is the one who does not follow the advice of the wicked, nor take a stand in the path of sinners, nor settle in the company of mockers.

2 Instead, his joy is found in the instruction of the LORD, and he reflects deeply on His teaching both day and night.

3 The one who follows God's ways will be like a tree firmly rooted beside streams of water, yielding its fruit at the proper time. Its leaves will never fade, and everything they do will flourish and succeed.

4 The wicked are not like this; instead, they are like chaff that the wind blows away.

5 Therefore, the wicked will not be able to endure in the time of judgment, nor will sinners have a place among the assembly of the righteous.

6 The LORD is intimately aware of the path taken by the righteous, but the way of the wicked will ultimately come to ruin.
