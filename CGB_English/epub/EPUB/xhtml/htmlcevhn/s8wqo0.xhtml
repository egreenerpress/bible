Jeremiah Chapter 10

1 Listen to the word that the LORD speaks to you, O house of Israel:

2 This is what the LORD says: Do not adopt the ways of the nations, and do not be terrified by the signs in the sky, for the nations are terrified by them.

3 The practices of the people are meaningless, for they cut down a tree from the forest, shaping it with an axe by the skill of a craftsman.

4 They adorn it with silver and gold; they secure it with nails and hammers so that it does not wobble or fall.

5 They stand tall like a palm tree, but they cannot speak; they must be carried, for they cannot walk. Do not fear them, for they can do no harm, nor is it within their power to do any good.

6 Because there is no one like you, O LORD; you are great, and your name is mighty in power.

7 Who would not fear You, O King of the nations? For this honor belongs to You alone. Indeed, among all the wise people of the nations and in all their kingdoms, there is no one like You.

8 But they are completely senseless and foolish; the wooden idol is nothing more than a teaching of emptiness.

9 Silver beaten into plates is brought from Tarshish, and gold from Uphaz, crafted by skilled artisans and shaped by the hands of metalworkers. Their garments are blue and purple, all of them the work of highly skilled craftsmen.

10 However, the LORD is the true God; He is the living God and the eternal King. When He expresses His anger, the earth quakes, and no nation can withstand His fury.

11 This is what you shall say to them: "The gods who did not create the heavens and the earth will perish from the earth and vanish from beneath these heavens."

12 He created the earth by His mighty power, established the world through His wisdom, and spread out the heavens with His understanding.

13 When He speaks, the heavens are filled with an abundance of waters, and He causes the mists to rise from the farthest reaches of the earth. He creates lightning to accompany the rain and brings forth the wind from His storehouses.

14 Every person is ignorant and without understanding; every craftsman is put to shame by the idols he makes, for his molded images are nothing but lies, and there is no life or spirit in them.

15 They are worthless, the product of deception; at the time of their judgment, they will be destroyed.

16 The portion belonging to Jacob is not like these idols, for he is the Creator of all things, and Israel is the tribe of his inheritance. The LORD of armies is his name.

17 Pack up your belongings and prepare to leave the land, you who dwell in the fortified city.

18 For this is what the LORD says: "Listen, I am about to hurl out the inhabitants of this land all at once, and I will bring such distress upon them that they will feel the full weight of it."

19 How terrible is my suffering! My injury is severe. Yet I said to myself, "This is indeed my affliction, and I must endure it."

20 My dwelling place is ruined, and all its ropes are torn apart. My children have left me and are no more. There is no one left to raise my tent again or to set up its curtains.

21 The shepherds have become senseless and have not sought the LORD; therefore, they will not succeed, and all their flocks will be scattered.

22 Listen! The sound of a report has arrived, and a great uproar is coming from the northern lands, destined to turn the cities of Judah into ruins, making them a haunt of jackals.

23 O LORD, I understand that a person's path is not within their own control; it is not for anyone who walks to determine their own steps.

24 O LORD, discipline me, but with justice; not in your anger, lest you reduce me to nothing.

25 Direct your wrath upon the nations that do not acknowledge you, and upon the peoples who do not call on your name. For they have utterly destroyed Jacob, devoured him, and laid waste to his dwelling place.
