2 My help comes from the LORD, the one who created heaven and earth.

3 He will not allow your foot to slip; the one who watches over you will never grow weary or sleep.

4 Indeed, the one who watches over Israel will neither doze off nor fall asleep.

5 The LORD is your protector; the LORD is your shelter at your right hand.

6 The sun will not harm you during the day, nor will the moon cause you harm at night.

7 The LORD will protect you from all harm; He will safeguard your life.

8 The LORD will protect your every departure and arrival, from this moment onward and forevermore.
