John Chapter 17

1 After saying these things, Jesus looked up toward heaven and said, "Father, the time has come. Bring glory to your Son, so that your Son may bring glory to you."

2 You have granted him authority over all humanity, so that he may give eternal life to all those you have entrusted to him.

3 And this is eternal life: that they may know you, the one true God, and Jesus Christ, whom you have sent.

4 I have brought glory to you on the earth: I have completed the work you gave me to accomplish.

5 And now, Father, honor me in your presence with the same glory I shared with you before the world existed.

6 I have revealed your name to the people you gave me from the world. They were yours, and you gave them to me; they have held firmly to your word.

7 Now they have come to understand that everything you have given me comes from you.

8 For I have given them the words that you gave to me, and they have accepted them. They have truly understood that I came from you, and they have believed that you sent me.

9 I am praying for them; I am not praying for the world, but for those you have given to me, because they belong to you.

10 Everything that is mine belongs to you, and everything that is yours belongs to me; and through them, I am honored and glorified.

11 And now I am no longer in the world, but these remain in the world, and I am coming to you. Holy Father, protect by the power of your name those you have given me, so that they may be united as one, just as we are.

12 While I was with them in the world, I protected them by the power of your name. Those you entrusted to me I have guarded, and not one of them has been lost, except the one destined for destruction, so that the Scripture might be fulfilled.

13 And now I am coming to you; I speak these things while I am still in the world, so that they may have the fullness of my joy within them.

14 I have given them your word, and the world has hated them because they do not belong to the world, just as I do not belong to the world.

15 I do not ask that you remove them from the world, but that you protect them from the evil one.

16 They do not belong to the world, just as I do not belong to the world.

17 Set them apart for your purposes by means of your truth; your word is the embodiment of truth.

18 Just as you sent me into the world, I have also sent them into the world.

19 For their sake, I set myself apart as holy, so that they too may be made holy through the truth.

20 I am not praying only for these, but also for those who will come to believe in me through their message.

21 May they all be united as one, just as you, Father, are in me and I am in you. May they also be united in us, so that the world may believe that you sent me.

22 The honor and splendor you have given me, I have granted to them, so that they may be united as one, just as we are one.

23 I am in them, and you are in me, so that they may be brought to complete unity. Then the world will know that you sent me and that you have loved them just as you have loved me.

24 Father, I desire that those whom you have given me may also be with me where I am, so that they may see the glory you have given me, for you loved me before the creation of the world.

25 O righteous Father, the world has not recognized you, but I have known you, and these [disciples] have come to understand that you sent me.

26 I have made known to them your name, and I will continue to make it known, so that the love with which you have loved me may be in them, and I myself may be in them.
