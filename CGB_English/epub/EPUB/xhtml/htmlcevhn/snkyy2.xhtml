Psalms Chapter 66

1 (For the director of music. A song or psalm.) Shout joyfully to God, all the earth!

2 Proclaim the greatness of his name; let his praise be magnificent and renowned.

3 Tell God, "How awe-inspiring are Your deeds! By the greatness of Your power, Your enemies will bow in submission before You."

4 All the earth will worship you and sing praises to you; they will sing praises to your name. Selah.

5 Come and witness the mighty acts of God: He is awe-inspiring in His dealings with humanity.

6 He transformed the sea into dry ground; they passed through the waters on foot. There we rejoiced in Him.

7 He reigns forever by His mighty power; His eyes watch over all the nations. Let the rebellious not rise up in pride. Selah.

8 Praise our God, all you people, and let the sound of his praise be heard far and wide.

9 He sustains our life and keeps us alive; He does not allow our steps to falter.

10 For You, O God, have tested us; You have refined us as silver is refined.

11 You led us into a trap; you placed a heavy burden upon us.

12 You have allowed men to trample over us; we endured trials as severe as passing through fire and water. Yet, you have brought us out into a place of abundance and prosperity.

13 I will enter your house with burnt offerings; I will fulfill the vows I have made to you.

14 These are the words that my lips have spoken and my mouth has declared in my time of distress.

15 I will present to you burnt offerings of the finest animals, along with the smoke of rams; I will offer bulls together with goats. Selah.

16 Come and listen, all who revere God, and I will tell you what he has done for my soul.

17 I called out to him with my voice, and he was praised through my words.

18 If I hold on to wickedness in my heart, the Lord will not listen to me.

19 Truly, God has heard me; He has paid attention to the sound of my prayer.

20 Praise be to God, who has not rejected my prayer nor withheld His steadfast love from me.
