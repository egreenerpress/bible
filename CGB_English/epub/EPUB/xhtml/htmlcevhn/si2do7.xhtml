Psalms Chapter 103

1 (A Psalm of David.) Praise the LORD, O my soul, and with all that I am, honor his sacred name.

2 Praise the LORD, O my soul, and do not forget all the good He has done for you:

3 He forgives all your sins and heals all your illnesses.

4 He is the one who rescues your life from ruin; he adorns you with steadfast love and compassionate mercy.

5 He fills your life with good things, so that your strength is renewed like the eagle's.

6 The LORD carries out justice and fairness for all who are oppressed.

7 He revealed His ways to Moses, His deeds to the people of Israel.

8 The LORD is compassionate and gracious, patient in anger, and abundant in steadfast love.

9 He will not constantly rebuke, nor will He hold onto His anger forever.

10 He has not treated us as our sins deserve, nor repaid us according to our wrongdoing.

11 For as the heavens are high above the earth, so immense is His steadfast love toward those who revere Him.

12 As distant as the east is from the west, so completely has He taken away our sins from us.

13 Just as a father shows compassion to his children, so the LORD shows compassion to those who honor Him.

14 For He understands our nature; He is mindful that we are but dust.

15 As for humanity, their days are like grass: they flourish like a flower in the field.

16 For the wind sweeps over it, and it vanishes; its place will no longer recognize it.

17 The steadfast love of the LORD endures forever and ever for those who honor Him, and His justice extends to their children and their childrenâ€™s children.

18 To those who faithfully uphold His covenant and diligently remember His commandments to carry them out.

19 The LORD has established his throne in the heavens, and his sovereign authority governs over all.

20 Praise the LORD, all you His angels, mighty in power, who carry out His commands, obedient to the sound of His word.

21 Praise the LORD, all you heavenly armies; you servants of His who carry out His will.

22 Praise the LORD, all His creations in every place where He reigns; praise the LORD, O my soul.
