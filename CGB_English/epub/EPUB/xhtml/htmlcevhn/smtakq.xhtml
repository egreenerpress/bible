Psalms Chapter 105

1 Give thanks to the LORD; call on his name. Proclaim his mighty works among the nations.

2 Sing to Him; offer songs of praise to Him. Speak of all His marvelous deeds and wondrous works.

3 Take pride and find joy in His sacred name; may the hearts of those who earnestly seek the LORD be filled with gladness.

4 Seek the LORD and rely on His power; continually pursue His presence.

5 Recall the extraordinary deeds he has performed, his miraculous acts, and the decrees he has spoken.

6 O descendants of Abraham, his faithful servant, O children of Jacob, his chosen ones.

7 He is the Lord our God; His decrees extend throughout the entire earth.

8 He has faithfully remembered his covenant forever, the promise he established for a thousand generations.

9 This is the covenant He established with Abraham, and the solemn promise He made to Isaac.

10 He established this as a decree for Jacob, as an eternal covenant for Israel.

11 He declared, "To you I will give the land of Canaan as your allotted portion and inheritance."

12 When they were only a small group of people—indeed, very few—and they lived as foreigners in the land.

13 When they journeyed from one nation to another, from one kingdom to a people of a different land;

14 He did not allow anyone to mistreat them; indeed, he rebuked kings on their behalf.

15 He said, "Do not lay a hand on those I have chosen, and do not harm my prophets."

16 Furthermore, He summoned a famine upon the land; He shattered the entire supply of bread.

17 He sent a man ahead of them—Joseph, who was sold into slavery.

18 They bound his feet with chains; he was confined with iron shackles.

19 Until the moment when his promise was fulfilled, the word of the LORD tested him.

20 The king sent orders to release him; the ruler of the nation set him free.

21 He appointed him as master over his household and entrusted him with authority over all his possessions.

22 To direct his leaders according to his will and to instruct his counselors in wisdom.

23 Israel also entered Egypt, and Jacob lived as a foreigner in the land of Ham.

24 He caused his people to grow greatly in number and made them more powerful than their adversaries.

25 He caused their hearts to turn against His people, leading them to act deceitfully toward His servants.

26 God sent Moses, His servant, and Aaron, whom He had chosen.

27 They displayed His miraculous signs among them and performed wonders in the land of Egypt.

28 He brought darkness, and it became dark; yet they did not defy His command.

29 He transformed their waters into blood, causing their fish to perish.

30 Their land teemed with frogs, even in the private rooms of their rulers.

31 He spoke, and there came swarms of various kinds of flies and gnats throughout all their territory.

32 He sent hail instead of rain, and blazing fire throughout their land.

33 He struck down their vineyards and their fig trees, and shattered the trees throughout their territory.

34 He spoke, and the locusts came, along with countless caterpillars, in numbers beyond measure.

35 They consumed all the vegetation in their land and devoured the produce of their soil.

36 He struck down all the firstborn in their land, the most vital and honored of all their strength.

37 He led them out with an abundance of silver and gold, and not a single person among their tribes was weak or feeble.

38 Egypt rejoiced when they left, for dread of them had overwhelmed them.

39 He spread out a cloud to serve as a covering, and fire to provide light during the night.

40 The people made their request, and God provided quails for them, filling them with the bread from heaven to satisfy their hunger.

41 He split open the rock, and water poured out; it flowed through the arid land like a mighty stream.

42 For He called to mind His sacred pledge and His servant Abraham.

43 He led his people out with joy, and his chosen ones with gladness.

44 He granted them the territories of the nations, and they took possession of the wealth produced by the peoples.

45 So that they may follow His decrees and obey His laws. Praise the LORD.
