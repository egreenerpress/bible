Leviticus Chapter 12

1 The LORD spoke to Moses, saying,

2 Tell the Israelites this: If a woman becomes pregnant and gives birth to a male child, she will be ceremonially unclean for seven days, just as she is during the time of her monthly period.

3 On the eighth day, the flesh of his foreskin must be circumcised.

4 After giving birth, she must remain in a state of purification for thirty-three days due to her bleeding. During this time, she must not touch anything sacred or enter the sanctuary until the days of her purification are completed.

5 But if she gives birth to a female child, she will be ceremonially unclean for two weeks, as during her menstrual period, and she must remain in a state of purification for sixty-six days.

6 When the days of her purification are completed, whether for a son or a daughter, she is to bring a one-year-old lamb as a burnt offering and a young pigeon or a turtledove as a sin offering to the entrance of the tent of meeting, to the priest.

7 The one who presents it before the LORD shall make atonement for her, and she will be purified from her flow of blood. This is the regulation for a woman who has given birth, whether to a male or a female child.
