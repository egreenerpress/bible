Psalms Chapter 126

1 (A song of ascents.) When the LORD restored the fortunes of Zion, we were like those who dream.

2 Then our mouths were filled with laughter, and our tongues with joyful songs. Then the nations said, "The LORD has done great things for them."

3 The LORD has accomplished remarkable deeds on our behalf, and we are filled with joy because of them.

4 Restore our fortunes, O LORD, like the flowing streams in the arid south.

5 Those who plant their seeds with weeping will harvest with shouts of joy.

6 The one who goes out weeping, carrying seeds to sow, will surely return with joy, bringing in the harvest with him.
