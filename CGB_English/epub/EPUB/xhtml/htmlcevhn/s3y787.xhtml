Hosea Chapter 3

1 Then the LORD said to me, "Go again and show love to a woman who is loved by her companion, yet is unfaithful, just as the LORD loves the people of Israel, though they turn to other gods and delight in offerings of wine."

2 So I purchased her for myself for fifteen shekels of silver and about a homer and a half of barley.

3 And I said to her, "You must remain faithful to me for many days. You must not engage in promiscuity, nor shall you belong to another man. In the same way, I will also remain devoted to you."

4 For a long time, the people of Israel will live without a king, without a leader, without sacrifices, without sacred pillars, without a priestly garment, and without household idols.

5 Afterward, the people of Israel will turn back and seek the LORD their God, and David their king. In the days to come, they will revere the LORD and acknowledge His goodness.
