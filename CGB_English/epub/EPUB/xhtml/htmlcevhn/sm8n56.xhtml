Micah Chapter 5

1 Assemble yourselves in battle formation, O city of warriors, for a siege has been set against us. They will strike the ruler of Israel with a rod upon the cheek.

2 But you, Bethlehem Ephrathah, though you are small among the clans of Judah, out of you will come forth for me one who is to be ruler over Israel, whose origins are from ancient times, from eternity.

3 Therefore, He will allow them to be abandoned until the time when she who is in labor gives birth. Then the remaining portion of His brothers will return to join the people of Israel.

4 He will stand and shepherd his flock with the strength of the LORD, in the majestic authority of the name of the LORD his God. And they will dwell securely, for now his greatness will extend to the ends of the earth.

5 This man will bring peace when the Assyrian invades our land and marches into our palaces. Then we will raise up against him seven shepherds and eight leaders of the people.

6 They will devastate the land of Assyria with the sword, and the land of Nimrod at its gateways. In this way, he will rescue us from the Assyrian when he invades our land and marches across our borders.

7 The remaining portion of Jacob will dwell among many nations, like dew sent by the LORD, like gentle rain falling on the grassâ€”dew and rain that do not depend on human effort or wait for the actions of people.

8 The remaining descendants of Jacob will dwell among the nations, surrounded by many peoples. They will be like a lion among the wild animals of the forest, like a young lion among flocks of sheep. When it passes through, it tramples and tears apart, and no one can rescue what it seizes.

9 Your power will rise against those who oppose you, and all your enemies will be utterly destroyed.

10 And it will happen on that day, declares the LORD, that I will eliminate your horses from among you and bring an end to your chariots.

11 I will destroy the cities within your land and demolish all your fortresses.

12 I will remove all forms of witchcraft from your grasp, and you will no longer have any fortune-tellers among you.

13 I will destroy your carved idols and your sacred pillars from among you, and you will no longer bow down to the works of your own hands.

14 I will uproot your sacred groves from among you and demolish your cities.

15 I will carry out vengeance with intense anger and wrath upon the nations, a punishment unlike anything they have ever experienced.
