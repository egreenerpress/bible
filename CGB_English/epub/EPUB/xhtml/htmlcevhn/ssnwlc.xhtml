Psalms Chapter 146

1 Praise the Lord! Praise the Lord, O my soul!

2 As long as I live, I will praise the LORD; I will sing praises to my God throughout my entire existence.

3 Do not place your confidence in rulers or in any human being, for they cannot provide true deliverance.

4 When a person's breath departs, they return to the dust; on that very day, their plans and thoughts come to an end.

5 Blessed is the one who has the God of Jacob as their helper, whose trust is placed in the LORD, their God.

6 The one who created the heavens, the earth, the sea, and everything within them; who remains faithful to his promises forever.

7 The Lord upholds justice for the oppressed and provides food for the hungry. He sets the prisoners free.

8 The LORD opens the eyes of the blind; the LORD lifts up those who are weighed down; the LORD loves those who live with integrity.

9 The LORD protects the foreigner; He sustains the fatherless and the widow, but He overturns the path of the wicked.

10 The LORD will reign forever; your God, O Zion, will rule throughout all generations. Praise the LORD!
