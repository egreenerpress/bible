Psalms Chapter 124

1 (A song of ascents, by David.) If the Lord had not been on our side—let Israel now declare—

2 If the LORD had not been on our side when people rose up against us,

3 Then they would have swiftly devoured us when their anger burned against us.

4 Then the waters would have swept us away, the current would have engulfed our very lives.

5 Then the raging waters would have swept over us.

6 Praise be to the LORD, who has not allowed us to become prey for their teeth.

7 Our lives have been set free like a bird escaping from the hunter’s trap; the trap has been broken, and we have been delivered.

8 Our assistance comes from the name of the LORD, the Creator of heaven and earth.
