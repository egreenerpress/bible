Psalms Chapter 62

1 (For the music director, according to the style of Jeduthun. A psalm of David.) Indeed, my soul waits in silence for God alone; from Him comes my deliverance.

2 He alone is my rock and my deliverance; he is my stronghold; I will not be greatly shaken.

3 How long will you plot evil against someone? All of you will be destroyed; you will crumble like a leaning wall, like a fence on the verge of collapse.

4 They conspire only to bring him down from his lofty position; they take pleasure in deceit. With their mouths, they speak blessings, but inwardly, they harbor curses. Selah.

5 My soul, wait in stillness for God alone, for my hope comes from him.

6 He alone is my rock and my salvation; he is my stronghold; I will not be shaken.

7 My salvation and my honor depend on God; he is my mighty rock and my place of safety.

8 Place your trust in Him at all times, O people; pour out your hearts before Him, for God is our secure refuge. Selah.

9 Certainly, people of low status are fleeting and insignificant, and those of high status are deceptive and unreliable. When weighed on the scales, they are all lighter than a breathâ€”utterly insubstantial.

10 Do not place your trust in oppression, nor rely on the emptiness of theft. Even if wealth grows, do not let your heart become attached to it.

11 God has spoken once; I have heard this twice: that all power belongs to God.

12 To you, O Lord, belongs steadfast love, for you repay each person according to their deeds.
