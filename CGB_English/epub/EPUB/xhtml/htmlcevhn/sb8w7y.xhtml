Numbers Chapter 29

1 On the first day of the seventh month, you are to hold a sacred assembly. You must not do any ordinary work, for it is a day of trumpet blasts, set apart for you.

2 You shall present a burnt offering as a pleasing aroma to the LORD: one young bull, one ram, and seven one-year-old lambs, all without defect.

3 Their grain offering shall consist of fine flour mixed with oil: three-tenths of an ephah for a bull, and two-tenths of an ephah for a ram.

4 And for each lamb, one-tenth of an ephah of fine flour shall be offered, for all seven lambs.

5 And one male goat as a sin offering, to provide atonement for you.

6 In addition to the monthly burnt offering, along with its grain offering, and the regular daily burnt offering with its grain offering and drink offerings, all presented according to the prescribed manner, as a pleasing aromaâ€”a fire offering to the LORD.

7 On the tenth day of this seventh month, you are to hold a sacred assembly. You must humble yourselves and refrain from doing any work on that day.

8 You are to present a burnt offering to the LORD as a pleasing aroma: one young bull, one ram, and seven male lambs in their first year. They must be without defect.

9 The grain offering accompanying their sacrifice must consist of fine flour mixed with olive oil: three-tenths of an ephah for each bull, and two-tenths of an ephah for each ram.

10 For each lamb, a separate tenth of an ephah of flour is to be offered, and this applies to all seven lambs.

11 One male goat as a sin offering, in addition to the sin offering for atonement, the regular burnt offering, its grain offering, and their accompanying drink offerings.

12 On the fifteenth day of the seventh month, you shall hold a sacred assembly; you must not do any ordinary work, and you shall celebrate a festival to the LORD for seven days.

13 You shall present a burnt offering, a sacrifice made by fire, as a pleasing aroma to the LORD: thirteen young bulls, two rams, and fourteen one-year-old lambs, all without any defect.

14 Their grain offering shall consist of fine flour mixed with oil: three-tenths of an ephah for each of the thirteen bulls, and two-tenths of an ephah for each of the two rams.

15 And for each of the fourteen lambs, a separate tenth portion of flour shall be offered.

16 And one male goat as a sin offering, in addition to the regular burnt offering, along with its grain offering and drink offering.

17 On the second day, you shall present twelve young bulls, two rams, and fourteen one-year-old lambs without any defect.

18 And the grain offering and the drink offerings for the bulls, the rams, and the lambs shall correspond to their number, following the prescribed standard.

19 And one male goat as a sin offering, in addition to the regular burnt offering, along with its grain offering and their drink offerings.

20 On the third day, present eleven bulls, two rams, and fourteen one-year-old lambs, all without defect.

21 And the grain offering and the drink offerings for the bulls, the rams, and the lambs shall correspond to their number, following the prescribed standard.

22 And one goat is to be offered as a sin offering, in addition to the regular burnt offering, along with its grain offering and its drink offering.

23 On the fourth day, they offered ten young bulls, two rams, and fourteen one-year-old lambs, all without any defect.

24 The grain offering and the drink offerings for the bulls, the rams, and the lambs shall be provided in proportion to their number, following the prescribed procedure.

25 And one male goat as a sin offering, in addition to the regular burnt offering, along with its grain offering and drink offering.

26 On the fifth day, present nine young bulls, two rams, and fourteen one-year-old lambs, all without defect.

27 And the grain offering and the drink offerings for the bulls, the rams, and the lambs shall correspond to their number, following the prescribed standard.

28 And one goat is to be offered as a sin offering, in addition to the regular burnt offering, along with its grain offering and drink offering.

29 On the sixth day, they were to offer eight bulls, two rams, and fourteen one-year-old lambs, all without any defect.

30 The grain offering and drink offerings for the bulls, the rams, and the lambs shall be provided in proportion to their number, following the prescribed standard.

31 And one goat is to be offered as a sin offering, in addition to the regular burnt offering, along with its grain offering and drink offering.

32 On the seventh day, offer seven bulls, two rams, and fourteen one-year-old lambs, all without any defect.

33 The grain offering and drink offerings for the bulls, the rams, and the lambs shall be prepared in proportion to their number, following the prescribed procedure.

34 And one goat is to be offered as a sin offering, in addition to the regular burnt offering, along with its grain offering and drink offering.

35 On the eighth day, you shall hold a sacred gathering. You must not engage in any ordinary labor on that day.

36 You shall present a burnt offering, a sacrifice made by fire, as a pleasing aroma to the LORD: one young bull, one ram, and seven male lambs in their first year, all without defect.

37 Their grain offering and their drink offerings for the bull, the ram, and the lambs shall correspond to their number, following the prescribed procedure.

38 And one goat is to be offered as a sin offering, in addition to the regular burnt offering, along with its grain offering and its drink offering.

39 You are to present these offerings to the LORD during your appointed festivals, in addition to your vows and your voluntary gifts, as well as your burnt offerings, grain offerings, drink offerings, and fellowship offerings.

40 Moses conveyed to the Israelites everything that the LORD had commanded him to say.
