Psalms Chapter 20

1 (For the music director. A Psalm of David.) May the LORD answer you in your time of distress; may the name of the God of Jacob protect you.

2 May he send you help from his holy place and grant you strength from Zion.

3 Remember all the gifts you have presented, and may your burnt offerings be pleasing to you; pause and reflect on this.

4 May He grant you the desires of your heart and bring all your plans to fulfillment.

5 We will celebrate your deliverance, and in the name of our God, we will raise our banners. May the LORD grant all your requests.

6 Now I understand that the LORD delivers His chosen one; He will answer him from His holy heaven with the mighty power of His right hand to bring salvation.

7 Some place their confidence in chariots, and others in horses, but we will call upon and honor the name of the LORD our God.

8 They have been brought low and have fallen, but we have risen and stand firm.

9 O LORD, deliver us; may the king answer us when we call upon you.
