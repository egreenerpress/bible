Psalms Chapter 148

1 Praise the LORD. Praise the LORD from the heavens; praise Him in the highest places.

2 Praise Him, all His angels; praise Him, all His heavenly armies.

3 Praise the Lord, sun and moon; praise him, all you shining stars.

4 Praise Him, all you highest heavens, and you waters above the heavens.

5 Let them praise the name of the LORD, for He gave the command, and they came into existence.

6 He has established them forever and ever; He has issued a decree that will never be revoked.

7 Praise the LORD from the earth, you great sea creatures, and all the depths of the ocean.

8 Fire and hail, snow and mist, and the tempestuous wind that carries out His command.

9 Mountains and every hill; fruit-bearing trees and all cedars:

10 Animals, both wild and domesticated; creatures that crawl on the ground, and birds that fly in the air:

11 Rulers of the earth and all its inhabitants; leaders and all who govern with authority:

12 Both young men and young women, the elderly and children alike:

13 Let them praise the name of the LORD, for His name alone is majestic; His glory surpasses the earth and the heavens.
