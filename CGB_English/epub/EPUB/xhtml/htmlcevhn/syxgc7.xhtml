I Chronicles Chapter 6

1 The descendants of Levi were Gershon, Kohath, and Merari.

2 The descendants of Kohath were Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel.

3 The descendants of Amram were Aaron, Moses, and Miriam. Aaron’s sons were Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar.

4 Eleazar became the father of Phinehas, and Phinehas became the father of Abishua.

5 Abishua became the father of Bukki, and Bukki became the father of Uzzi.

6 Uzzi became the father of Zerahiah, and Zerahiah became the father of Meraioth.

7 Meraioth became the father of Amariah, and Amariah became the father of Ahitub.

8 Ahitub was the father of Zadok, and Zadok was the father of Ahimaaz.

9 Ahimaaz became the father of Azariah, and Azariah became the father of Johanan.

10 Johanan was the father of Azariah, who served as a priest in the temple that Solomon built in Jerusalem.

11 Azariah became the father of Amariah, and Amariah became the father of Ahitub.

12 Ahitub was the father of Zadok, and Zadok was the father of Shallum.

13 Shallum was the father of Hilkiah, and Hilkiah was the father of Azariah.

14 Azariah became the father of Seraiah, and Seraiah became the father of Jehozadak.

15 Jehozadak was taken into exile when the LORD allowed Judah and Jerusalem to be carried away by the power of Nebuchadnezzar.

16 The descendants of Levi were Gershom, Kohath, and Merari.

17 These are the names of Gershom's sons: Libni and Shimei.

18 The descendants of Kohath were Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel.

19 The descendants of Merari were Mahli and Mushi. These are the family lines of the Levites, organized according to their ancestral fathers.

20 From Gershom: his son Libni, his son Jahath, and his son Zimmah.

21 Joah was his son, Iddo was Joah's son, Zerah was Iddo's son, and Jeaterai was Zerah's son.

22 The descendants of Kohath were Amminadab, his son; Korah, his son; and Assir, his son.

23 Elkanah his son, Ebiasaph his son, and Assir his son.

24 Tahath's son was Uriel, Uriel's son was Uzziah, and Uzziah's son was Shaul.

25 The sons of Elkanah were Amasai and Ahimoth.

26 Concerning Elkanah: the sons of Elkanah were Zophai his son, and Nahath his son.

27 Eliab was his son, Jeroham was Eliab’s son, and Elkanah was Jeroham’s son.

28 The sons of Samuel were his firstborn, Joel, and the second, Abijah.

29 The descendants of Merari were Mahli, his son Libni, his son Shimei, and his son Uzza.

30 Shimea his son, Haggiah his son, Asaiah his son.

31 These are the individuals whom David appointed to oversee the ministry of music in the house of the LORD, after the ark had come to rest.

32 They served before the tabernacle, the dwelling place of the meeting tent, with songs of worship, until Solomon constructed the house of the LORD in Jerusalem. Afterward, they carried out their duties in their appointed roles according to their established order.

33 These are the individuals who served alongside their descendants. Among the sons of the Kohathites was Heman, a singer, the son of Joel, the son of Shemuel.

34 The son of Elkanah, the son of Jeroham, the son of Eliel, the son of Toah.

35 The son of Zuph, the son of Elkanah, the son of Mahath, the son of Amasai.

36 The son of Elkanah, the son of Joel, the son of Azariah, the son of Zephaniah.

37 The son of Tahath, the son of Assir, the son of Ebiasaph, the son of Korah.

38 The son of Izhar, who was the son of Kohath, who was the son of Levi, who was the son of Israel.

39 And his brother Asaph, who stood at his right side—Asaph, the son of Berachiah, the son of Shimea.

40 The son of Michael, the son of Baaseiah, the son of Malchiah.

41 The son of Ethni, the son of Zerah, the son of Adaiah.

42 The son of Ethan, the son of Zimmah, the son of Shimei.

43 The son of Jahath, the son of Gershom, the son of Levi.

44 And their relatives, the descendants of Merari, were positioned on the left side: Ethan, the son of Kishi, who was the son of Abdi, who was the son of Malluch.

45 The son of Hashabiah, the son of Amaziah, the son of Hilkiah.

46 The son of Amzi, the son of Bani, the son of Shamer.

47 The son of Mahli, the son of Mushi, the son of Merari, the son of Levi.

48 Their fellow Levites were also assigned to every kind of service in the tabernacle, the house of God.

49 Aaron and his sons performed their duties at the altar of burnt offerings and the altar of incense. They were assigned to carry out all the tasks related to the Most Holy Place and to provide atonement for Israel, following everything that Moses, the servant of God, had commanded.

50 These are the descendants of Aaron: his son Eleazar, Eleazar’s son Phinehas, and Phinehas’s son Abishua.

51 Bukki was his son, Uzzi was his son, and Zerahiah was his son.

52 Meraioth was his son, Amariah was his son, and Ahitub was his son.

53 Zadok's son was Ahimaaz.

54 Here are the settlements and territories assigned to the descendants of Aaron from the family of the Kohathites, as determined by lot. These were their dwelling places within their designated regions, located near their fortified towns.

55 They assigned Hebron to them in the land of Judah, along with the surrounding pasturelands.

56 However, the fields surrounding the city and its neighboring villages were given to Caleb, the son of Jephunneh.

57 To the descendants of Aaron, they assigned cities within the territory of Judah, including Hebron, which was designated as a city of refuge, along with its surrounding pasturelands. They also gave them Libnah with its pasturelands, Jattir with its pasturelands, and Eshtemoa with its pasturelands.

58 And Hilen with its surrounding pasturelands, Debir with its surrounding pasturelands,

59 Ashan, along with its surrounding pasturelands, and Beth-shemesh, along with its surrounding pasturelands.

60 From the tribe of Benjamin, they received Geba with its surrounding pasturelands, Alemeth with its surrounding pasturelands, and Anathoth with its surrounding pasturelands. In total, there were thirteen cities, each with their surrounding lands, distributed among their families.

61 To the remaining descendants of Kohath from that family of the tribe, ten cities were allotted from the half-tribe of Manasseh, distributed by lot.

62 To the descendants of Gershon, according to their clans, were allotted thirteen cities from the tribes of Issachar, Asher, Naphtali, and from the half-tribe of Manasseh in Bashan.

63 To the descendants of Merari, according to their families, were allotted twelve cities by lot from the tribes of Reuben, Gad, and Zebulun.

64 The Israelites assigned these cities, along with their surrounding pasturelands, to the Levites.

65 They distributed by lot cities from the territories of the tribes of Judah, Simeon, and Benjamin, assigning these cities, which are identified by their names.

66 The remaining families of the descendants of Kohath received towns within their assigned territory from the tribe of Ephraim.

67 They assigned to them the cities of refuge, including Shechem in the hill country of Ephraim along with its surrounding pasturelands, and also Gezer with its surrounding pasturelands.

68 And Jokmeam with its surrounding pasturelands, and Beth-horon with its surrounding pasturelands.

69 And Aijalon with its surrounding pasturelands, and Gath-rimmon with its surrounding pasturelands.

70 From the half-tribe of Manasseh, they received Aner and its surrounding pasturelands, and Bileam and its surrounding pasturelands. These were allotted to the families of the remaining descendants of Kohath.

71 To the descendants of Gershom, from the family of the half-tribe of Manasseh, were assigned Golan in Bashan, along with its surrounding pasturelands, and Ashtaroth, also with its surrounding pasturelands.

72 From the tribe of Issachar, they received Kedesh with its surrounding pasturelands, Daberath with its surrounding pasturelands,

73 Ramoth, along with its surrounding pasturelands, and Anem, along with its surrounding pasturelands.

74 From the tribe of Asher: Mashal with its surrounding pasturelands, and Abdon with its surrounding pasturelands.

75 And Hukok with its surrounding pasturelands, and Rehob with its surrounding pasturelands.

76 From the tribe of Naphtali: Kedesh in Galilee, along with its surrounding pasturelands, Hammon with its surrounding pasturelands, and Kirjathaim with its surrounding pasturelands.

77 To the remaining descendants of Merari, from the tribe of Zebulun, were allotted the town of Rimmon with its surrounding pasturelands and Tabor with its surrounding pasturelands.

78 On the eastern side of the Jordan River, near Jericho, the following cities were allotted to them from the tribe of Reuben: Bezer in the wilderness, along with its surrounding pasturelands, and Jahzah, also with its surrounding pasturelands.

79 Kedemoth, along with its surrounding pasturelands, and Mephaath, along with its surrounding pasturelands.

80 From the tribe of Gad, they received Ramoth in Gilead with its surrounding pasturelands, and Mahanaim with its surrounding pasturelands.

81 And Heshbon with its surrounding pasturelands, and Jazer with its surrounding pasturelands.
