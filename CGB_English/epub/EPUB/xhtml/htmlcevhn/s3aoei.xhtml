Ezekiel Chapter 15

1 The LORD's message came to me, saying,

2 "Son of man, how is the vine any different from other trees, or how is a branch of the vine distinct from the branches of the trees in the forest?"

3 Can wood from it be used to make anything useful? Or can someone take a peg from it to hang a vessel or tool upon?

4 Look, it is thrown into the fire as fuel; the fire consumes both its ends, and the middle of it is scorched. Is it still useful for any purpose?

5 Consider this: when the object was intact, it was not suitable for any purpose. How much less useful will it be after the fire has consumed it and it has been burned?

6 Therefore, this is what the Lord GOD says: Just as the vine tree among the trees of the forest, which I have handed over to the fire to be burned as fuel, so will I hand over the inhabitants of Jerusalem.

7 I will turn my attention against them; they will escape one disaster only to fall into another. You will know that I am the LORD when I direct my judgment against them.

8 I will cause the land to become desolate because they have acted unfaithfully, declares the Lord GOD.
