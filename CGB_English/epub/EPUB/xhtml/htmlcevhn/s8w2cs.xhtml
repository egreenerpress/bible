Psalms Chapter 138

1 (A Psalm of David.) I will give you thanks with all my heart; in the presence of divine beings, I will sing praises to you.

2 I will bow in reverence toward your holy temple and give praise to your name for your steadfast love and faithfulness. For you have exalted your word above all that your name represents.

3 On the day I called out to you, you answered me; you gave me strength and fortified my inner being.

4 All the rulers of the world will honor You, O LORD, when they hear the words You have spoken.

5 Indeed, they will sing joyfully as they walk in the ways of the LORD, for the glory of the LORD is magnificent and boundless.

6 Though the LORD is exalted, He shows concern for the humble, but He keeps His distance from the proud.

7 Even as I walk through the midst of trouble, you will restore my life. You will reach out your hand against the anger of my enemies, and your mighty hand will deliver me.

8 The LORD will bring to completion what concerns me; your steadfast love, O LORD, endures forever. Do not abandon the work of your hands.
