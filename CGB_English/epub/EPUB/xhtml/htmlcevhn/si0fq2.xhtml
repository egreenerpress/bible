Psalms Chapter 67

1 (To the director of music, for stringed instruments. A psalm, a song.) May God show us mercy and bless us; may He let His favor rest upon us. Selah.

2 May your ways be made known throughout the earth, and your saving power among all nations.

3 Let all the people honor and worship you, O God; may every nation and community give you praise.

4 May all the nations rejoice and sing with joy, for you will judge the people with fairness and guide the nations of the earth with justice. Selah.

5 Let all the people honor and worship you, O God; may every nation and community give you praise.

6 Then the land will produce its harvest, and God, our very own God, will bless us.

7 God will bless us, and all the farthest reaches of the earth will revere Him.
