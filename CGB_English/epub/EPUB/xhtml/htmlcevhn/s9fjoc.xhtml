Revelation Chapter 4

1 After this, I looked, and behold, a door stood open in heaven. And the first voice I heard was like a trumpet speaking to me, saying, "Come up here, and I will show you what must take place after these things."

2 At once, I was caught up in the Spirit, and there before me was a throne established in heaven, with someone seated upon it.

3 The one seated on the throne appeared in appearance like a jasper and a carnelian stone. Surrounding the throne was a rainbow, which gleamed with the brilliance of an emerald.

4 Surrounding the throne were twenty-four seats, and on those seats I saw twenty-four elders seated. They were dressed in white garments and wore golden crowns on their heads.

5 From the throne came flashes of lightning, rumblings, and peals of thunder. In front of the throne, seven blazing lamps were burning, which represent the sevenfold Spirit of God.

6 In front of the throne, there was what appeared to be a sea of glass, clear as crystal. Surrounding the throne, and at its center, were four living creatures, covered with eyes both in front and behind.

7 The first living creature resembled a lion, the second looked like a young ox, the third had the face of a human, and the fourth appeared like a soaring eagle.

8 Each of the four living creatures had six wings, and their bodies were covered with eyes all around, even under their wings. Day and night, without ceasing, they proclaim, "Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Almighty, who was, who is, and who is to come."

9 And when those living creatures give praise, honor, and gratitude to the one seated on the throne, who lives eternally and forever,

10 The twenty-four elders fell down before the one seated on the throne and worshiped him who lives forever and ever. They cast their crowns before the throne, saying,

11 You are worthy, O Lord, to receive glory, honor, and power, for you created all things, and by your will they exist and were created.
