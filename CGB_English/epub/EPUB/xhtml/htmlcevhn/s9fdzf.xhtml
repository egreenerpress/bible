Psalms Chapter 13

1 (For the director of music. A psalm of David.) How long, O LORD, will you forget me? Will it be forever? How long will you turn your face away from me?

2 How long must I wrestle with my thoughts, enduring daily sorrow in my heart? How long will my enemy triumph over me?

3 Pay attention to me and listen, O LORD my God. Restore the brightness to my eyes, so that I do not fall into the sleep of death.

4 Do not let my enemy boast, "I have overcome him," nor let those who trouble me celebrate when I falter.

5 I have placed my trust in your steadfast love; my heart will be glad because of your deliverance.

6 I will sing to the LORD, for He has treated me with abundant kindness.
