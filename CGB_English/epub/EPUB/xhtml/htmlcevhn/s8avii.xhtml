Ezekiel Chapter 36

1 Furthermore, son of man, proclaim a message to the mountains of Israel and declare: "Mountains of Israel, listen to the word of the LORD."

2 This is what the Lord GOD says: Because the enemy has boasted against you, saying, "Aha! Even the ancient high places are now ours to possess,"

3 Therefore, prophesy and declare, "This is what the Lord GOD says: Because they have devastated you and surrounded you on every side, intending to make you a possession for the remaining nations, and because you have become the subject of gossip and a byword of disgrace among the people."

4 Therefore, mountains of Israel, listen to the word of the Lord GOD. This is what the Lord GOD says to the mountains, the hills, the rivers, the valleys, the desolate ruins, and the abandoned cities that have become plunder and a source of mockery for the surrounding nations:

5 Therefore, this is what the Lord GOD says: In the heat of my burning anger, I have spoken against the rest of the nations and against all of Edom, who have claimed my land as their own possession with wholehearted delight and malicious intent, seeking to plunder it and drive out its people.

6 Therefore, prophesy concerning the land of Israel and say to the mountains, the hills, the rivers, and the valleys, "This is what the Lord GOD says: Listen, I have spoken in my burning zeal and fierce anger because you have endured the disgrace of the nations."

7 Therefore, this is what the Lord GOD says: I have solemnly sworn that the nations surrounding you will themselves endure their own disgrace.

8 But you, O mountains of Israel, will sprout your branches and bear your fruit for my people Israel, for they are soon to return.

9 Listen carefully, for I am on your side, and I will turn my attention to you. You will be cultivated and planted anew.

10 I will increase the number of people upon you—the entire house of Israel, all of it. The cities will be filled with inhabitants, and the ruined places will be rebuilt.

11 I will cause both people and animals to multiply and flourish among you; they will increase in number and be fruitful. I will restore you to your former prosperity and make your condition even better than it was at the beginning. Then you will know that I am the LORD.

12 Indeed, I will bring people to dwell upon you—my people Israel. They will take possession of you, and you will become their inheritance. Never again will you take away their children or cause them to lose their people.

13 This is what the Lord GOD says: Because people say of you, "You are a land that consumes its inhabitants and causes your nations to lose their children,"

14 Therefore, you will no longer consume people, nor will you cause your nations to suffer loss anymore, declares the Lord GOD.

15 I will no longer allow the disgrace of the nations to be heard against you, nor will you endure the scorn of the peoples any longer. You will no longer cause your own people to stumble, declares the Lord GOD.

16 Furthermore, the message of the LORD came to me, saying,

17 Son of man, when the people of Israel lived in their own land, they polluted it with their conduct and their actions. Their behavior was like the impurity of a woman during her menstrual period, and it was evident before me.

18 Therefore, I unleashed my wrath upon them because of the blood they had shed on the land and because of the idols with which they had defiled it.

19 I dispersed them among the nations, and they were scattered across the lands. I judged them according to their conduct and their actions.

20 When they came to the nations where they went, they dishonored my holy name, for it was said of them, "These are the people of the LORD, yet they have been driven out of his land."

21 But I acted out of concern for my holy name, which the house of Israel had dishonored among the nations wherever they went.

22 Therefore, speak to the house of Israel and say to them, "This is what the Lord GOD declares: I am not doing this for your sake, O house of Israel, but for the sake of my holy name, which you have dishonored among the nations where you have gone."

23 I will make my great name holy, which has been dishonored among the nations—the name you have disgraced in their midst. The nations will then know that I am the LORD, declares the Sovereign LORD, when I demonstrate my holiness through you in their sight.

24 For I will remove you from among the nations, gather you from all the lands, and bring you back to your own land.

25 At that time, I will pour clean water over you, and you will be made clean. I will cleanse you from all your impurities and from all your idols.

26 I will give you a new heart and place a new spirit within you. I will remove your heart of stone from your body and give you a heart of flesh instead.

27 I will place my Spirit within you, enabling you to live according to my decrees. You will carefully observe my laws and faithfully carry them out.

28 You will live in the land that I gave to your ancestors, and you will be my people, and I will be your God.

29 I will also deliver you from all your impurities, and I will summon the grain and cause it to increase abundantly. I will not bring famine upon you.

30 I will cause the fruit of the trees and the produce of the fields to increase abundantly, so that you will no longer suffer the disgrace of famine among the nations.

31 Then you will recall your own wicked ways and your actions that were not good, and you will feel disgust toward yourselves in your own eyes because of your sins and your detestable deeds.

32 The Lord GOD declares: "I am not doing this for your sake, as you should know. Be filled with shame and disgrace because of your own actions, O house of Israel."

33 This is what the Lord GOD says: On the day I cleanse you from all your sins, I will also enable you to live in your cities, and the ruined places will be rebuilt.

34 The barren and abandoned land will be cultivated and worked, even though it had previously lain desolate and empty, visible to all who passed by.

35 And they will declare, "This land that was once barren has now become like the garden of Eden. The cities that were ruined, desolate, and abandoned are now fortified and filled with people."

36 Then the nations that remain around you will know that I, the LORD, rebuild what has been ruined and restore what was desolate. I, the LORD, have declared it, and I will accomplish it.

37 This is what the Lord GOD says: "For this reason, I will still allow the house of Israel to seek me and ask for my intervention, so that I may act on their behalf. I will multiply their people, making them as numerous as a flock."

38 Just as the sacred flock, like the flocks of Jerusalem during its appointed festivals, so shall the desolate cities be filled with multitudes of people. And they will come to understand that I am the LORD.
