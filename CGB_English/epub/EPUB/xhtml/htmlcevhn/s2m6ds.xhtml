Jeremiah Chapter 45

1 This is the message that Jeremiah the prophet delivered to Baruch, the son of Neriah, when Baruch had written down in a scroll the words that Jeremiah dictated to him. This took place in the fourth year of Jehoiakim, the son of Josiah, king of Judah. Jeremiah said to Baruch:

2 This is what the LORD, the God of Israel, says to you, Baruch:

3 You said, "How terrible it is for me now! The LORD has added more grief to my sorrow. I am worn out from my groaning, and I find no relief."
