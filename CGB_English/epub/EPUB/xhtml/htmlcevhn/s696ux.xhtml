Psalms Chapter 143

1 (A Psalm of David.) O LORD, listen to my prayer; pay attention to my pleas. In your unwavering faithfulness, answer me, and in your perfect righteousness, respond.

2 Do not bring your servant into judgment, for no one alive can be considered righteous in your sight.

3 For my enemy has pursued me relentlessly; he has crushed my life to the ground; he has forced me to live in darkness, like those who have long been dead.

4 Therefore, my spirit is utterly overwhelmed within me; my heart within me is filled with despair.

5 I recall the days of long ago; I reflect deeply on all your deeds; I ponder the works of your hands.

6 I reach out my hands to you; my soul longs for you like a parched land thirsts for water. Selah.

7 Hear me quickly, O LORD; my spirit is growing faint. Do not hide your face from me, or I will become like those who descend into the grave.

8 Let me hear of your steadfast love in the morning, for I place my trust in you. Show me the path I should take, for I entrust my life to you.

9 Rescue me, O LORD, from my enemies; I run to you for refuge and protection.

10 Instruct me to carry out your will, for you are my God. Your Spirit is good; guide me in the path of righteousness.

11 Revive me, O LORD, for the sake of your name; in your righteousness, deliver my soul from distress.

12 In your mercy, put an end to my enemies and destroy all who oppress me, for I am your servant.
