Psalms Chapter 110

1 (A Psalm of David.) The LORD declared to my Lord, "Take your seat at my right hand until I make your enemies a footstool beneath your feet."

2 The LORD will extend the scepter of your authority from Zion; you shall reign in the midst of your adversaries.

3 Your people will offer themselves willingly on the day of your power, arrayed in holy splendor, emerging like the dawn from the womb of the morning. You will have the freshness and vitality of youth, like the dew that falls at daybreak.

4 The LORD has made a solemn vow and will not change His mind: "You are a priest forever, in the manner of Melchizedek."

5 The Lord, standing at your right hand, will shatter kings in the day of His fierce anger.

6 He will execute judgment among the nations; he will fill the lands with the slain. He will strike down the rulers of many countries.

7 He will drink from the stream along his path; therefore, he will hold his head high.
