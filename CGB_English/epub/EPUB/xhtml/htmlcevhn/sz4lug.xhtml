Psalms Chapter 21

1 (For the music director. A psalm of David.) The king will delight in your strength, O LORD, and in your saving power he will rejoice with great joy!

2 You have granted him the desire of his heart and have not withheld what he asked for. Selah.

3 For you go before him with abundant blessings of goodness; you place a crown of fine gold upon his head.

4 He requested life from you, and you granted it to himâ€”long life that endures forever and ever.

5 His splendor is magnificent because of your deliverance; you have bestowed upon him honor and majestic dignity.

6 For you have made him supremely blessed forever; you have filled him with immense joy by your presence.

7 The king places his confidence in the LORD, and by the steadfast love of the Most High, he will remain secure and unshaken.

8 Your power will uncover all your enemies; your strength will expose those who hate you.

9 You will make them like a blazing oven in the time of your anger; the LORD will consume them in His wrath, and the fire will utterly destroy them.

10 You will wipe out their offspring from the earth, and their descendants from among humanity.

11 For they planned evil against you; they devised a wicked scheme, but they will not succeed in carrying it out.

12 Therefore, you will cause them to retreat in fear when you prepare your arrows on your bowstring, aiming them directly at their faces.

13 May you, O LORD, be exalted in your mighty strength; then we will sing and give praise to your power.
