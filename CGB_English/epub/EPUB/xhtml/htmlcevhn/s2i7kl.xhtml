Psalms Chapter 86

1 (A Prayer of David.) LORD, incline your ear to me and listen, for I am afflicted and in need.

2 Protect my life, for I am devoted to you; O my God, save your servant who trusts in you.

3 Show me your mercy, O Lord, for I call out to you continually, day after day.

4 Bring joy to the soul of your servant, for to you, O Lord, I lift up my soul.

5 For you, Lord, are good and always willing to forgive; your mercy overflows abundantly to all who call on you.

6 Listen, O LORD, to my prayer; give heed to the sound of my earnest pleas.

7 In the time of my distress, I will call out to you, for you will answer me.

8 Among all the divine beings, there is none like you, O Lord; nor are there any deeds that compare to your mighty works.

9 All the nations you have created will come and bow down before you, O Lord; they will honor and praise your name.

10 For you are great and perform marvelous deeds; you alone are God.

11 Instruct me in your ways, O LORD; I will live according to your truth. Unite my heart completely, so that I may revere your name.

12 I will praise you, O Lord my God, with all my heart, and I will honor your name forever.

13 For your mercy toward me is immense, and you have rescued my life from the depths of the grave.

14 O God, the arrogant have risen up against me, and bands of ruthless men seek my life; they do not acknowledge you or consider your presence.

15 But you, O Lord, are a God of great compassion, kind and patient, overflowing with steadfast love and faithfulness.

16 Turn to me and show me your mercy; grant your strength to your servant, and deliver the child of your maidservant.

17 Grant me a sign of your favor, so that those who oppose me may see it and be put to shame, for you, LORD, have helped me and brought me comfort.
