Psalms Chapter 111

1 Praise the Lord! I will praise the Lord with all my heart, among the gathering of the righteous and in the assembly of the faithful.

2 The works of the LORD are magnificent, carefully examined by all who delight in them.

3 His work is majestic and splendid, and his righteousness remains steadfast forever.

4 He has caused his extraordinary deeds to be remembered; the LORD is kind and overflowing with compassion.

5 He has provided food for those who honor him; he will always remember his covenant.

6 He has demonstrated to His people the might of His deeds, so that He may grant them the inheritance of the nations.

7 The works of His hands are truth and justice; all His commands are steadfast and reliable.

8 They remain steadfast forever and ever, established in truth and integrity.

9 He has brought deliverance to his people; he has established his covenant forever. His name is holy and awe-inspiring.

10 Reverence for the LORD is the foundation of true wisdom; all who follow His commands gain sound understanding. His praise will last forever.
