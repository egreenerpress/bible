Psalms Chapter 46

1 (To the director of music, for the sons of Korah, a song to be sung with high-pitched instruments.) God is our shelter and our might, a constant and ever-ready help in times of distress.

2 Therefore, we will not be afraid, even if the earth is shaken and the mountains are thrown into the depths of the sea.

3 Even if its waters roar and churn, even if the mountains tremble at its surging. Selah.

4 There is a river whose flowing waters bring joy to the city of God, the sacred dwelling place of the Most High.

5 God is present within her; she will remain steadfast and unshaken. God will come to her aid, and His help will arrive at the break of dawn.

6 The nations were in turmoil, the kingdoms were shaken; when God spoke, the earth itself dissolved.

7 The LORD Almighty is with us; the God of Jacob is our stronghold. Pause and reflect on this.

8 Come and see the mighty works of the LORD, the devastation He has brought upon the earth.

9 He brings wars to an end across the entire earth; he shatters the bow, snaps the spear in two, and sets the chariots ablaze with fire.

10 "Cease striving and recognize that I am God; I will be honored among the nations, I will be honored throughout the earth."

11 The LORD Almighty is with us; the God of Jacob is our stronghold. Selah.
