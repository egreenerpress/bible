Psalms Chapter 36

1 (For the music director. A psalm of David, the servant of the LORD.) The sinful actions of the wicked speak loudly within my heart, revealing that they have no reverence for God in their thoughts.

2 For he deceives himself with his own thoughts, until his wrongdoing is exposed and becomes detestable.

3 The words he speaks are filled with wickedness and deception; he has ceased to act wisely and to do what is good.

4 He plans wicked schemes while lying on his bed; he chooses a path that is not upright; he does not reject what is evil.

5 Your steadfast love, O LORD, extends to the heavens; and your faithfulness reaches to the skies.

6 Your righteousness is as enduring as the mighty mountains; your judgments are as vast and profound as the deepest ocean. O LORD, you sustain and protect both humanity and all living creatures.

7 How extraordinary is your steadfast love, O God! Because of this, people find refuge and place their trust in the shelter of your protection.

8 They will be fully satisfied with the abundance of your provision; you will give them drink from the stream of your delights.

9 For with you is the source of life; in your light, we will see true light.

10 Continue to show your steadfast love to those who know you, and your justice to those who are upright in heart.

11 Do not allow the arrogance of the proud to overtake me, nor let the power of the wicked drive me away.

12 Here are the evildoers, brought low and overthrown; they have been cast down and will not be able to rise again.
