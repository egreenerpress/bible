Psalms Chapter 59

1 (For the director of music, to the tune of "Do Not Destroy," a prayer of David, when Saul sent men to watch his house in order to kill him.) Save me from my enemies, O my God; protect me from those who rise up against me.

2 Rescue me from those who commit wickedness, and save me from violent and bloodthirsty individuals.

3 Behold, they lie in ambush for my life; the powerful have banded together against me—not because of any wrongdoing or sin of mine, O LORD.

4 They rush forward and prepare themselves, though I am without fault. Rise up to help me, and take notice.

5 Therefore, O LORD, God of armies, the God of Israel, rise up to bring judgment upon all the nations. Show no mercy to any who are wicked and rebellious. Selah.

6 They come back in the evening, howling like dogs, and prowl around the city.

7 Observe how they spew out words from their mouths; their lips are like sharp swords. They arrogantly declare, “Who is there to hear us?”

8 But you, O LORD, will laugh at them; you will hold all the nations in contempt.

9 Because of His power, I will trust in You, for God is my protection.

10 The God who shows me steadfast love will go before me; God will enable me to see the downfall of my enemies.

11 Do not kill them, or my people may forget; disperse them by your power and bring them low, O Lord, our protector.

12 Because of the sin they commit with their mouths and the words they speak, let them be caught in their own pride. And for the curses and lies they utter, let them face the consequences.

13 Destroy them in your anger, destroy them completely, so that they may cease to exist. Let them come to know that God reigns over Jacob, extending His authority to the farthest reaches of the earth. Selah.

14 In the evening, let them come back; let them howl like dogs and prowl around the city.

15 Let them roam about seeking food, and growl in frustration if they are not filled.

16 But I will sing of your strength; yes, I will joyfully proclaim your steadfast love in the morning, for you have been my stronghold and my safe haven in times of distress.

17 To you, my source of strength, I will sing praises, for God is my protector and the God who shows me steadfast love.
