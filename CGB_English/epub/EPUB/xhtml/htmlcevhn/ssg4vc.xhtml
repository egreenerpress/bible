Psalms Chapter 43

1 O God, judge my case and defend me against a nation that does not honor you. Rescue me from the deceitful and unjust person.

2 You are the God who is my strength: why have you rejected me? Why must I go about in sorrow because of the oppression of the enemy?

3 Send forth your light and your truth; may they guide me and lead me to your sacred mountain, to your dwelling places.

4 Then I will approach the altar of God, to God, who is my greatest joy; indeed, with the harp I will praise you, O God, my God.

5 Why are you so downcast, O my soul? And why are you so troubled within me? Put your hope in God, for I will yet praise Him, who is the source of my strength and my salvation, and my God.
