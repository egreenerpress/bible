Psalms Chapter 12

1 (For the music director, to be played on the Sheminith, a psalm of David.) Lord, come to our aid! The godly are disappearing; the faithful are vanishing from among the people.

2 They speak empty words, each one to their neighbor; with smooth, flattering lips and a deceitful heart, they speak.

3 The LORD will silence all deceitful lips and the tongue that speaks arrogant words.

4 They have declared, "With our words we will triumph; our speech is under our own controlâ€”who can rule over us?"

5 Because of the oppression of the poor and the groaning of the needy, I will now rise up, says the LORD. I will place them in safety, secure from those who mock and threaten them.

6 The words of the LORD are flawless, like silver refined in a furnace of clay, purified sevenfold.

7 You, O LORD, will watch over them; you will protect them from this generation forever.

8 The wicked roam freely on every side, when the most depraved individuals are lifted up in honor.
