Psalms Chapter 123

1 (A song of ascents.) I lift my eyes to you, O you who are enthroned in the heavens.

2 Look, just as the eyes of servants are fixed on the hand of their master, and as the eyes of a servant girl are fixed on the hand of her mistress, so our eyes are fixed on the LORD our God, until He shows us mercy.

3 Show us mercy, O LORD, grant us your mercy, for we are overwhelmed with disgrace and scorn.

4 Our very being is overwhelmed by the mockery of those who live in comfort and by the disdain of the arrogant.
