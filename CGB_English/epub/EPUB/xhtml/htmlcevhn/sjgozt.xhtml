Zechariah Chapter 4

1 Then the angel who was speaking with me returned and roused me, as one might awaken a person from their sleep.

2 He asked me, "What do you see?" I replied, "I see a lampstand made entirely of gold, with a bowl at its top. It has seven lamps on it, along with seven channels leading to each of the lamps at the top."

3 And there are two olive trees beside it, one on the right side of the bowl and the other on its left side.

4 So I responded and spoke to the angel who was speaking with me, saying, "What do these things mean, my lord?"

5 Then the angel who was speaking with me responded and said, "Do you not understand what these things mean?" I replied, "No, my lord."

6 Then he responded and spoke to me, saying, "This is the message of the LORD to Zerubbabel: It will not be accomplished by human strength or by force, but by my Spirit, declares the LORD of hosts."

7 Who are you, O mighty mountain? Before Zerubbabel, you will be leveled to a plain. He will bring out the capstone with shouts of joy, proclaiming, "May grace, grace be upon it!"

8 Furthermore, the message of the LORD came to me, saying,

9 The hands of Zerubbabel have laid the foundation of this house, and his hands will also complete it. Then you will know that the LORD of hosts has sent me to you.

11 Then I responded and asked him, "What do these two olive trees represent, one on the right side of the lampstand and the other on its left?"

13 He responded to me, saying, "Do you not understand what these things mean?" And I replied, "No, my lord."
