I Chronicles Chapter 3

1 Here are the sons of David who were born to him in Hebron: the firstborn was Amnon, whose mother was Ahinoam from Jezreel; the second was Daniel, whose mother was Abigail from Carmel.

2 The third son was Absalom, whose mother was Maacah, the daughter of Talmai, king of Geshur. The fourth son was Adonijah, whose mother was Haggith.

3 The fifth son was Shephatiah, born to Abital; the sixth was Ithream, born to Eglah, his wife.

4 These six sons were born to him in Hebron, where he ruled for seven years and six months. Afterward, he reigned in Jerusalem for thirty-three years.

5 These were the sons born to him in Jerusalem: Shimea, Shobab, Nathan, and Solomon—four in total—by Bath-shua, the daughter of Ammiel.

6 Ibhar, Elishama, and Eliphelet were also among them.

7 And Nogah, Nepheg, and Japhia.

8 And Elishama, Eliada, and Eliphelet—nine in total.

9 These were all the sons of David, in addition to the sons born to his concubines, and Tamar, their sister.

10 Solomon's son was Rehoboam; his son was Abijah, then Asa, and after him, Jehoshaphat.

11 Joram, his son; Ahaziah, his son; Joash, his son.

12 Amaziah his son, Azariah his son, Jotham his son.

13 Ahaz's son, Hezekiah, and Hezekiah's son, Manasseh.

14 Amon his son, Josiah his son.

15 Josiah's sons were as follows: the firstborn was Johanan, the second was Jehoiakim, the third was Zedekiah, and the fourth was Shallum.

16 The sons of Jehoiakim were Jeconiah, his son, and Zedekiah, his son.

17 The sons of Jeconiah were Assir and Salathiel, his son.

18 Malchiram, Pedaiah, Shenazar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and Nedabiah.

19 The descendants of Pedaiah were Zerubbabel and Shimei. Zerubbabel’s children were Meshullam, Hananiah, and their sister Shelomith.

20 And Hashubah, Ohel, Berechiah, Hasadiah, and Jushab-hesed—five in total.

21 The descendants of Hananiah were Pelatiah and Jesaiah. The descendants of Rephaiah, the descendants of Arnan, the descendants of Obadiah, and the descendants of Shecaniah followed in their lineage.

22 The descendants of Shecaniah included Shemaiah. Shemaiah’s sons were Hattush, Igeal, Bariah, Neariah, and Shaphat—six in total.

23 The sons of Neariah were Elioenai, Hezekiah, and Azrikam—three in total.

24 The sons of Elioenai were Hodaiah, Eliashib, Pelaiah, Akkub, Johanan, Dalaiah, and Anani—seven in total.
