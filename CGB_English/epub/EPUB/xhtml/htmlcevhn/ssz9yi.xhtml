Psalms Chapter 41

1 (For the music director, a psalm of David.) Blessed is the one who shows concern for the needy; the LORD will rescue him in times of distress.

2 The LORD will protect him and sustain his life; he will be blessed on the earth, and you will not hand him over to the desires of his enemies.

3 The LORD will sustain him on his sickbed; you will restore him fully during his illness.

4 I prayed, "LORD, show me mercy; heal my soul, for I have sinned against you."

5 My adversaries speak maliciously about me, asking, "When will he die, and his name be forgotten?"

6 If he comes to visit me, he speaks empty words; his heart gathers up wickedness within itself. When he goes out, he spreads it abroad.

7 All those who hate me conspire together against me; they plot to bring harm upon me.

8 They say, "A terrible affliction clings to him, and now that he has fallen, he will never rise again."

9 Indeed, my close friend, whom I trusted, who shared my bread, has turned against me and betrayed me.

10 But you, O LORD, show me mercy and lift me up, so that I may repay them.

11 By this, I know that you are pleased with me, because my enemy has not prevailed over me.

12 As for me, you sustain me in my integrity and establish me in your presence forever.

13 Praise be to the LORD, the God of Israel, from eternity past to eternity future. So be it, and so be it.
