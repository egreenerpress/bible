I Thessalonians Chapter 1

1 Paul, Silas, and Timothy, to the church of the Thessalonians, who belong to God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ: May grace and peace be yours, from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

2 We continually give thanks to God for all of you, remembering you in our prayers.

3 We continually remember before our God and Father your faithful deeds, your loving efforts, and your steadfast hope in our Lord Jesus Christ.

4 Beloved brothers and sisters, we are fully aware that you have been chosen by God.

5 The message we brought to you was not conveyed merely through words, but also with power, through the Holy Spirit, and with deep conviction. You know very well the kind of people we were among you, and how we lived for your benefit.

6 You became imitators of us and of the Lord, for you welcomed the message despite great suffering, accompanied by the joy given by the Holy Spirit.

7 As a result, you became examples to all the believers in Macedonia and Achaia.

8 The message of the Lord has gone forth from you, not only in Macedonia and Achaia, but in every place where your faith in God has become known. As a result, we have no need to say anything further.

9 For they themselves speak about us, telling of the kind of reception we had among you, and how you turned from idols to serve the living and true God.
