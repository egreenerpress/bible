Psalms Chapter 18

1 (For the music director. A psalm of David, the servant of the LORD, who spoke to the LORD the words of this song on the day the LORD rescued him from the power of all his enemies and from the hand of Saul. He said:) I will love you, O LORD, my strength.

2 The LORD is my solid foundation, my secure stronghold, and my rescuer; my God, my source of strength, in whom I place my complete trust; my shield, the power of my deliverance, and my lofty refuge.

3 I will call upon the LORD, who is deserving of all praise, and in doing so, I will be delivered from my enemies.

4 The anguish of death surrounded me, and the overwhelming torrents of wicked people filled me with terror.

5 The agonies of the grave surrounded me; the traps of death confronted me.

6 In my time of trouble, I called out to the LORD and pleaded with my God. He heard my voice from his holy temple, and my cry reached him, entering into his very presence.

7 Then the earth quaked and trembled; the very foundations of the mountains shifted and shook, for He was filled with anger.

8 Smoke rose from his nostrils, and flames burst from his mouth, consuming all before them; burning coals were ignited by its heat.

9 He bent the heavens and descended; thick darkness was beneath His feet.

10 He mounted a cherub and soared; indeed, he flew swiftly on the wings of the wind.

11 He made darkness His hiding place; His surrounding shelter was the deep waters and dense clouds of the heavens.

12 At the radiance that preceded Him, His dense clouds swept by, bringing hailstones and fiery coals.

13 The LORD thundered from the heavens, and the Most High spoke with His voice; hailstones and fiery coals rained down.

14 Indeed, He unleashed His arrows and dispersed them; He hurled lightning bolts and threw them into confusion.

15 Then the channels of the waters became visible, and the foundations of the earth were laid bare at Your rebuke, O LORD, at the blast of the breath of Your nostrils.

16 He reached down from on high, He took hold of me, He pulled me out of deep and turbulent waters.

17 He rescued me from my powerful adversary and from those who hated me, for they were too mighty for me to overcome.

18 In the day of my distress, they confronted me, but the LORD was my support.

19 He led me out into a spacious place; he rescued me because he took pleasure in me.

20 The LORD has given me what I deserved based on my uprightness; according to the purity of my actions, He has repaid me.

21 For I have faithfully followed the ways of the LORD and have not turned away from my God in wickedness.

22 For all His decrees were ever before me, and I did not turn away from His commands.

23 I also walked in integrity before Him, and I guarded myself against my own wrongdoing.

24 Therefore, the LORD has rewarded me according to my uprightness, according to the purity of my actions in His sight.

25 To the merciful, you will show yourself merciful; to the blameless, you will show yourself blameless.

26 With the pure, you will show yourself pure; but with the deceitful, you will show yourself shrewd.

27 You will rescue the oppressed and humble the proud.

28 For you will light my lamp; the LORD my God will dispel my darkness.

29 With your help, I have charged through an army; by the power of my God, I have scaled a wall.

30 As for God, His way is flawless; the word of the LORD is proven true. He is a shield for all who take refuge in Him.

31 Who is God except the LORD? And who is a rock except our God?

32 It is God who equips me with strength and makes my path complete.

33 He makes my feet as swift as those of a deer, and he places me securely on the heights.

34 He trains my hands for battle, so that my arms can bend a bow of bronze.

35 You have also given me the shield of your deliverance, and your right hand has upheld me. Your kindness has made me great.

36 You have made my path wide and secure beneath me, so that my steps have not faltered.

37 I have pursued my enemies and caught up to them; I did not turn back until they were completely defeated.

38 I have struck them down so severely that they cannot rise again; they have fallen beneath my feet.

39 For you have equipped me with strength for the battle; you have brought down beneath me those who stood against me.

40 You have also granted me victory over my enemies, enabling me to defeat those who oppose me.

41 They called out for help, but no one came to rescue them; they even cried out to the LORD, but he did not respond to them.

42 Then I crushed them into fine powder, scattering them like dust before the wind; I swept them away like mud in the streets.

43 You have rescued me from the conflicts of the people; you have established me as a leader over the nations. A people I did not know will now serve me.

44 As soon as they hear of me, they will obey; foreigners will yield and submit to me.

45 Foreigners will wither away and tremble with fear in their strongholds.

46 The LORD lives; blessed be my rock; and may the God of my salvation be exalted.

47 It is God who takes vengeance for me and brings nations under my authority.

48 He rescues me from my enemies; indeed, you raise me up above those who oppose me. You have saved me from the violent one.

49 Therefore, I will give thanks to you, O LORD, among the nations, and I will sing praises to your name.

50 He grants great victory to his king; he shows steadfast love to his anointed one, to David, and to his descendants forever.
