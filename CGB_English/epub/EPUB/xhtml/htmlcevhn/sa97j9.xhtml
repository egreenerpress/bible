Psalms Chapter 145

1 (Davidâ€™s Psalm of praise.) I will exalt you, my God and King; I will praise your name forever and always.

2 Every day I will honor you, and I will praise your name without end, for all eternity.

3 The LORD is exceedingly great and deserves the highest praise; His greatness is beyond comprehension and cannot be fully explored.

4 One generation will proclaim your works to the next, and they will recount your mighty deeds.

5 I will proclaim the magnificent splendor of your majesty and recount the extraordinary deeds you have done.

6 People will speak of the power displayed in your awe-inspiring deeds, and I will proclaim your greatness.

7 They will continually proclaim the remembrance of your abundant kindness and will joyfully sing of your justice and moral perfection.

8 The LORD is kind and deeply compassionate, patient in anger, and overflowing with steadfast love.

9 The LORD is good to everyone, and His compassionate care extends over all His creation.

10 All that you have created will give you praise, O LORD; and your faithful ones will honor you.

11 They will proclaim the splendor of your kingdom and declare the greatness of your might.

12 To declare to all humanity His powerful deeds and the splendid majesty of His reign.

13 Your kingdom is an eternal kingdom, and your rule endures through all generations.

14 The LORD supports all who stumble and lifts up all who are weighed down.

15 All eyes look to you in hope, and you provide them with their food at the proper time.

16 You open your hand and fulfill the needs of every living creature.

17 The LORD is just in all His actions and blameless in all His deeds.

18 The LORD is close to all who call on him, to all who call on him sincerely and with genuine faith.

19 He will satisfy the longing of those who revere him; he will also listen to their plea and deliver them.

20 The LORD protects all who love him, but he will bring ruin upon all the wicked.

21 I will declare the praise of the LORD, and may all people honor and bless His holy name forever and always.
