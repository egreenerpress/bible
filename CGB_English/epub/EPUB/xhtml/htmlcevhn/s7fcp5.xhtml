Psalms Chapter 48

1 (A song and psalm composed by the descendants of Korah.) The LORD is truly great and deserves the highest praise in the city of our God, on His holy mountain.

2 Majestic in its setting, the delight of all the earth, is Mount Zion, on the northern heights, the city of the great King.

3 God is recognized within her strongholds as a place of safety and protection.

4 Behold, the kings gathered together; they advanced as one.

5 They observed it and were filled with astonishment; they were deeply disturbed and quickly retreated.

6 Terror seized them in that place, and anguish, like that of a woman in labor.

7 You shatter the ships of Tarshish with the force of an east wind.

8 Just as we have heard, so we have witnessed in the city of the LORD of hosts, in the city of our God: God will firmly establish it forever. Selah.

9 We have reflected on your steadfast love, O God, within the sanctuary of your temple.

10 As your name is known, O God, so is your renown proclaimed to the farthest reaches of the earth. Your mighty hand is filled with justice and integrity.

11 Let Mount Zion be filled with joy, and let the daughters of Judah celebrate with gladness, because of your righteous judgments.

12 Walk around Zion, and circle her; observe her towers closely.

13 Take careful note of her fortifications, examine her grand buildings closely, so that you may recount these things to the generations to come.

14 For this God is our God forever and eternally; He will be our guide even until the moment of death.
