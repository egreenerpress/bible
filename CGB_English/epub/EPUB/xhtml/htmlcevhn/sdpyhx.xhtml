Psalms Chapter 130

1 (A song of ascents.) From the deepest despair, I have called out to you, O LORD.

2 Lord, listen to my voice; may your ears be attentive to the sound of my earnest prayers.

3 If you, LORD, were to keep a record of sins, O Lord, who could possibly stand before you?

4 However, with you there is forgiveness, so that you may be revered.

5 I wait for the LORD; my soul waits, and in his word I place my hope.

6 My soul waits for the Lord more eagerly than those who watch for the dawnâ€”yes, more than those who long for the morning to break.

7 Let Israel place their hope in the LORD, for with the LORD there is steadfast love, and with Him is abundant redemption.

8 And he will deliver Israel from all their sins.
