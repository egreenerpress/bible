Psalms Chapter 10

1 Why do you remain distant, O LORD? Why do you conceal yourself in times of distress?

2 The wicked, in their arrogance, oppress the poor; may they be caught in the very schemes they have devised.

3 The wicked proudly proclaim their selfish desires and praise those who are greedy, even though the LORD detests such people.

4 The wicked, driven by their arrogance, refuse to seek God; God has no place in their thoughts.

5 His actions are always troublesome; your judgments are beyond his understanding. As for all his enemies, he scoffs at them.

6 He has said to himself, "I will not be shaken; I will never face misfortune."

7 His speech is filled with curses, lies, and deceit; beneath his tongue lie trouble and wickedness.

8 He waits in ambush near the villages; from hidden places he kills the innocent. His eyes are secretly fixed on the helpless.

9 He lies in ambush, hidden like a lion in its den; he waits to seize the poor. He captures the poor when he lures them into his trap.

10 He stoops low and humbles himself, so that the vulnerable may fall prey to his powerful schemes.

11 He has said to himself, "God has forgotten; He hides His face; He will never see it."

12 Rise up, O LORD; O God, raise your hand in power. Do not forget the lowly and afflicted.

13 Why do the wicked despise God? They say in their hearts, "He will not hold us accountable."

14 You have witnessed it, for You observe trouble and malice, to repay it with Your own hand. The helpless entrust themselves to You; You are the protector of the fatherless.

15 Shatter the power of the wicked and the evildoer; thoroughly examine their wrongdoing until no trace of it remains.

16 The LORD reigns as King forever and ever; the nations have been removed from His land.

17 LORD, you have listened to the longing of the lowly; you will strengthen their hearts, and you will incline your ear to hear them.

18 To administer justice for the fatherless and the oppressed, so that those who wield power on earth may no longer inflict suffering.
