Psalms Chapter 96

1 Sing to the LORD a fresh song; let all the earth raise its voice in praise to the LORD.

2 Sing to the LORD, praise his name; proclaim his deliverance every day.

3 Proclaim His splendor among the nations, His marvelous works among all peoples.

4 The LORD is truly great and deserves the highest praise; He is to be revered above all other gods.

5 The gods of all the nations are mere idols, but it is the LORD who created the heavens.

6 Honor and splendor surround Him; power and magnificence fill His holy place.

7 Give to the LORD, all you families of the nations, give to the LORD the honor and power that He deserves.

8 Give to the LORD the honor that is rightfully His; present an offering and enter His presence with reverence.

9 Worship the LORD in the splendor of His holiness; let all the earth stand in awe of Him.

10 Proclaim among the nations that the LORD reigns! The world is firmly established and will not be shaken; He will judge the peoples with fairness and justice.

11 Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be filled with joy; let the sea resound with its mighty roar, and all that fills it.

12 Let the fields be filled with joy, and everything within them; then all the trees of the forest will sing with gladness.
