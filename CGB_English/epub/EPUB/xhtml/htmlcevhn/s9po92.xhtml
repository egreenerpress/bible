Psalms Chapter 33

1 Be glad in the LORD, you who are righteous, for praise is fitting and beautiful for those who live with integrity.

2 Praise the LORD with the harp; sing to Him with the lyre and the ten-stringed instrument.

3 Sing to Him a new song; play skillfully with a joyful and resounding melody.

4 The word of the LORD is trustworthy, and all His actions are carried out with integrity.

5 He loves righteousness and justice; the earth is filled with the steadfast love of the LORD.

6 By the command of the LORD, the heavens were created, and all their vast array by the power of His breath.

7 He gathers the waters of the sea into a vast mass; he stores the depths in reservoirs.

8 Let all the earth revere the LORD; let every person in the world hold Him in profound awe.

9 For he spoke, and it came to be; he gave the command, and it was firmly established.

10 The LORD frustrates the plans of the nations; He nullifies the schemes of the peoples.

11 The plans of the LORD endure forever; the intentions of His heart remain steadfast through all generations.

12 Fortunate is the nation whose God is the LORD, and the people He has chosen as His own treasured possession.

13 The LORD gazes down from heaven; He observes all the children of humanity.

14 From the place where He dwells, He observes all who live on the earth.

15 He shapes the hearts of all people in the same way; He observes and understands everything they do.

16 No king is saved by the size of his army; no warrior is rescued by his great strength.

17 A horse is a futile source of security; despite its great strength, it cannot save anyone.

18 Look, the LORD watches over those who honor Him, over those who trust in His steadfast love.

19 To rescue their lives from death and to sustain them during times of famine.

20 Our whole being waits for the LORD; He is our help and our protection.

21 Our hearts will be filled with joy because of Him, for we have placed our trust in His sacred name.

22 May your steadfast love, O LORD, rest upon us, in accordance with the trust we place in you.
