I Chronicles Chapter 24

1 Here are the divisions of Aaron’s descendants: Aaron’s sons were Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar.

2 However, Nadab and Abihu died during their father's lifetime, and they had no children. As a result, Eleazar and Ithamar carried out the duties of the priesthood.

3 David assigned them their duties, including Zadok from the descendants of Eleazar and Ahimelech from the descendants of Ithamar, according to their roles and responsibilities in their service.

4 Among the descendants of Eleazar, more leaders were identified than among the descendants of Ithamar, and so they were divided accordingly. From the sons of Eleazar, there were sixteen leaders representing their ancestral families, while from the sons of Ithamar, there were eight leaders representing their ancestral families.

5 They were assigned their duties by drawing lots, each group receiving its portion equally. For the leaders of the sanctuary and the leaders of the house of God were chosen from the descendants of Eleazar and the descendants of Ithamar.

6 Shemaiah, the son of Nethaneel, who was a scribe and one of the Levites, recorded the names in the presence of the king, the leaders, Zadok the priest, Ahimelech the son of Abiathar, and the heads of the ancestral families of the priests and Levites. One leading family was chosen for Eleazar, and another for Ithamar.

7 The first lot was drawn for Jehoiarib, and the second for Jedaiah.

8 The third lot fell to Harim, and the fourth to Seorim.

9 The fifth lot fell to Malchijah, and the sixth to Mijamin.

10 The seventh lot fell to Hakkoz, and the eighth to Abijah.

11 The ninth lot fell to Jeshua, and the tenth to Shecaniah.

12 The eleventh lot fell to Eliashib, and the twelfth to Jakim.

13 The thirteenth lot fell to Huppah, and the fourteenth to Jeshebeab.

14 The fifteenth lot fell to Bilgah, and the sixteenth to Immer.

15 The seventeenth lot fell to Hezir, and the eighteenth to Aphses.

16 The nineteenth lot fell to Pethahiah, and the twentieth to Jehezekel.

17 The twenty-first lot fell to Jachin, and the twenty-second to Gamul.

18 On the twenty-third day, the duty fell to Delaiah; on the twenty-fourth day, it was Maaziah's turn.

19 This was the arrangement of their duties for serving in the house of the LORD, according to their assigned roles, under the authority of Aaron their father, just as the LORD, the God of Israel, had commanded him.

20 The remaining descendants of Levi were as follows: From the line of Amram came Shubael, and from the line of Shubael came Jehdeiah.

21 Regarding Rehabiah: among his descendants, Isshiah was the foremost.

22 From the descendants of Izhar: Shelomoth. From the sons of Shelomoth: Jahath.

23 The sons of Hebron were Jeriah the first, Amariah the second, Jahaziel the third, and Jekameam the fourth.

24 From the descendants of Uzziel: Michah. And from the descendants of Michah: Shamir.

25 Michah's brother was Isshiah, and among Isshiah's sons was Zechariah.

26 The descendants of Merari were Mahli and Mushi. The sons of Jaaziah were Beno.

27 The descendants of Merari through Jaaziah were Beno, Shoham, Zaccur, and Ibri.

28 From the line of Mahli came Eleazar, who had no sons.

29 Regarding Kish: Jerahmeel was the son of Kish.

30 The descendants of Mushi were Mahli, Eder, and Jerimoth. These were the sons of the Levites, listed according to their ancestral families.

31 These individuals also cast lots alongside their relatives, the descendants of Aaron, in the presence of King David, Zadok, Ahimelech, and the leaders of the priestly and Levitical families. The senior leaders participated in this process alongside their younger relatives.
