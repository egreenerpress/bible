Psalms Chapter 144

1 (A Psalm of David.) Praise be to the LORD, my source of strength, who trains my hands for battle and my fingers for combat.

2 You are my source of goodness and my stronghold; my secure refuge and my rescuer; my protector and the one in whom I place my trust. He brings my people under my authority.

3 LORD, what is humanity, that You take notice of them? What is a mere mortal, that You give them any thought?

4 Human beings are like a fleeting breath; their days are as transient as a passing shadow.

5 O LORD, bend the heavens and descend; touch the mountains, and let them rise in smoke.

6 Send forth your lightning and disperse them; unleash your arrows and bring them to ruin.

7 Reach down your hand from on high; rescue me and set me free from the deep waters, from the grasp of foreign oppressors.

8 Their words are empty and deceitful, and their actions are filled with dishonesty.

9 I will sing a new song to you, O God; with a harp and a ten-stringed instrument, I will offer praises to you.

10 It is He who grants victory to kings; He rescues David, His servant, from the deadly sword.

11 Rescue me and save me from the power of foreign oppressors, whose words are empty and deceitful, and whose actions are marked by dishonesty and treachery.

12 May our sons flourish like well-nurtured plants in their youth, and may our daughters be like finely crafted cornerstones, shaped and adorned as though for a majestic palace.

13 May our storehouses be filled with every kind of provision, abundantly supplied. May our flocks multiply by the thousands, even tens of thousands, filling our pastures.

14 May our oxen be strong and capable for their work; may there be no breaches in our defenses or invasions from outside; and may there be no outcry or distress in our streets.

15 Blessed are the people who find themselves in such a condition; indeed, blessed are the people whose God is the LORD.
