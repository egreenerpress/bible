Psalms Chapter 11

1 (For the music director, a Psalm of David.) I place my trust in the LORD. Why then do you say to me, "Flee like a bird to the mountains"?

2 Behold, the wicked prepare their bows and set their arrows on the string, ready to secretly shoot at those who are upright in heart.

3 If the very foundations are shattered, what can the righteous accomplish?

4 The LORD is present in His holy temple; His throne is established in heaven. His eyes observe, and His gaze examines, the actions of all humanity.

5 The LORD examines the righteous, but the wicked and those who love violence, He detests with all His being.

6 Upon the wicked, He will pour down traps, fire and sulfur, and a terrifying storm: this will be their allotted portion.

7 For the Lord, who is just, loves justice; His gaze rests upon those who live with integrity.
