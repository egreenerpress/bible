Malachi Chapter 4

1 Listen carefully, for the day is coming that will burn like a blazing furnace. All who are arrogant and all who do evil will be like dry stubble. That day will consume them completely, declares the LORD of hosts, leaving them with neither root nor branch.

2 But for you who revere my name, the Sun of Righteousness will rise with healing in its rays. You will go out and leap with joy, like calves released from the stall.

3 You will trample down the wicked, for they will be like ashes beneath the soles of your feet on the day when I act, declares the LORD of hosts.

4 Remember the law of Moses, my servant, which I commanded him at Horeb for all Israel, including the statutes and ordinances.

5 Listen carefully, I will send Elijah the prophet to you before the arrival of the great and awe-inspiring day of the LORD.

6 He will restore the hearts of the fathers to their children and the hearts of the children to their fathers, so that I do not come and strike the land with complete destruction.
