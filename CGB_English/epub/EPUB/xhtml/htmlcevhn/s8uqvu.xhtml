Psalms Chapter 38

1 (A Psalm of David, for remembrance.) O LORD, do not correct me in your anger, nor discipline me in your fierce wrath.

2 Your arrows have pierced me deeply, and your hand presses heavily upon me.

3 My body is in complete distress because of your anger; there is no health in me. My bones ache incessantly because of my sin.

4 My sins have overwhelmed me; they are like a heavy burden, too much for me to bear.

5 My wounds are foul and festering because of my own foolishness.

6 I am deeply distressed; I am utterly weighed down; I spend the entire day in sorrow and mourning.

7 For my body is afflicted with a grievous sickness, and there is no health in my flesh.

8 I am weak and utterly crushed; I cry out in anguish because of the turmoil in my heart.

9 Lord, all my longings are laid open before you; my sighs and sorrows are not concealed from your sight.

10 My heart races within me, my strength is failing, and even the brightness of my vision has faded away.

11 My loved ones and close friends keep their distance from my suffering, and even my relatives stand far away.

12 Those who seek to take my life set traps for me; those who wish me harm speak maliciously and spend their days plotting deceit.

13 But I, like a deaf person, did not hear; and I was like a mute who does not open his mouth.

14 I became like someone who cannot hear, and in whose mouth there are no words of defense or rebuke.

15 For in you, O LORD, I place my trust; you will listen, O Lord my God.

16 For I thought, "Please listen to me, so that they do not gloat over me; when my foot slips, they boast and exalt themselves against me."

17 For I am on the verge of stumbling, and my grief is always before me.

18 I will openly confess my wrongdoing; I will deeply grieve over my sin.

19 However, my enemies are vigorous and powerful, and those who unjustly hate me have greatly increased in number.

20 Those who repay evil for good oppose me, because I pursue what is good and right.

21 Do not abandon me, O LORD; my God, do not distance yourself from me.

22 Hurry to assist me, O Lord, my deliverer.
