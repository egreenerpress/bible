Psalms Chapter 112

1 Praise the LORD! Blessed is the one who reveres the LORD, who takes great joy in following His commands.

2 His descendants will be powerful and influential on the earth; the generation of the righteous will be abundantly blessed.

3 Wealth and abundance will fill his household, and his righteous deeds will stand firm forever.

4 For those who live with integrity, light shines even in the darkest times. They are kind, filled with compassion, and act with justice.

5 A good person acts with kindness and generosity, lending to those in need. They manage their affairs with wisdom and careful judgment.

6 Certainly, he will never be shaken; the righteous will be remembered forever.

7 He will not fear bad news; his heart remains steadfast, secure in his trust in the LORD.

8 His heart is steadfast; he will not be afraid, until he sees his triumph over his enemies.

9 He has generously given and distributed to the poor; his acts of justice and righteousness will last forever; his strength and dignity will be lifted high with honor.

10 The wicked will see it and be filled with anguish; they will gnash their teeth in frustration and despair, and their strength will fade away. The hopes and desires of the wicked will come to nothing.
