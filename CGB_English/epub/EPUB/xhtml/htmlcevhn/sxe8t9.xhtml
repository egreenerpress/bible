Psalms Chapter 127

1 (A song of ascents, dedicated to Solomon.) Unless the Lord builds the house, those who labor to construct it work for nothing. Unless the Lord watches over the city, the guard who stays awake does so in vain.

2 It is pointless for you to rise early, stay up late, and toil anxiously for your food, for God provides restful sleep to those he loves.

3 Behold, children are a gift from the LORD; the offspring of the womb are His blessing.

4 Like arrows in the hand of a warrior, so are the children born in one's youth.

5 Blessed is the man whose quiver is full of them; he will not be disgraced but will stand confidently before his adversaries at the city gate.
