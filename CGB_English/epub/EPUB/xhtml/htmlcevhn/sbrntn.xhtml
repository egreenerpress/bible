Psalms Chapter 122

1 (A song of ascents, by David.) I rejoiced when they said to me, "Let us go to the house of the LORD."

2 Our feet will stand within your gates, O Jerusalem.

3 Jerusalem is constructed as a city, united and closely knit together.

4 Where the tribes ascend, the tribes of the LORD, to the covenant testimony of Israel, to give thanks to the name of the LORD.

5 For the thrones of justice are established there, the thrones belonging to the house of David.

6 Pray for the well-being of Jerusalem; may those who love you thrive and prosper.

7 May peace dwell within your walls, and may prosperity fill your palaces.

8 For the sake of my brothers and companions, I will now declare, "May peace dwell within you."

9 For the sake of the house of the LORD our God, I will seek your well-being.
