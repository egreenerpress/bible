Jeremiah Chapter 1

1 The words of Jeremiah, the son of Hilkiah, one of the priests from Anathoth in the territory of Benjamin.

2 The message of the LORD came to him during the reign of Josiah, son of Amon, king of Judah, in the thirteenth year of his rule.

3 This event also occurred during the reign of Jehoiakim, the son of Josiah, king of Judah, and continued until the eleventh year of Zedekiah, the son of Josiah, king of Judah, up to the time when Jerusalem was taken into exile in the fifth month.

4 Then the word of the LORD came to me, saying,

5 Before I shaped you in the womb, I knew you intimately; before you were born, I set you apart and appointed you as a prophet to the nations.

6 Then I said, "Ah, Lord God! Look, I cannot speak, for I am only a youth."

7 But the LORD said to me, "Do not say, 'I am only a child,' for you must go to everyone I send you to, and you must speak whatever I command you to say."

8 Do not be intimidated by their expressions or their presence, for I am with you to rescue and protect you, declares the LORD.

9 Then the LORD reached out His hand and touched my mouth. The LORD said to me, "Look, I have placed My words in your mouth."

10 Behold, today I have appointed you over the nations and over the kingdoms, to uproot and to tear down, to demolish and to overthrow, to build up and to plant.

11 Furthermore, the word of the LORD came to me, saying, "Jeremiah, what do you see?" I replied, "I see a branch from an almond tree."

12 Then the LORD said to me, "You have seen correctly, for I am watching over my word to fulfill it."

13 The word of the LORD came to me a second time, saying, "What do you see?" I replied, "I see a boiling pot, and its opening is tilted toward the north."

14 Then the LORD said to me, "From the north, disaster will be unleashed upon all who live in the land."

15 Listen carefully, for I, the LORD, declare this: I am summoning all the families of the northern kingdoms. They will come and set up their thrones at the entrance gates of Jerusalem, surrounding its walls on every side, and they will position themselves against all the cities of Judah.

16 I will declare my judgments against them concerning all their wickedness—how they have abandoned me, burned incense to other gods, and bowed down to worship the works of their own hands.

17 Therefore, prepare yourself for action, stand up, and speak to them everything I command you. Do not be intimidated by their presence, or I will cause you to falter before them.

18 Listen carefully, for today I have established you as a fortified city, an unshakable iron pillar, and walls of bronze against the entire land—against the kings of Judah, its officials, its priests, and all the people of the land.

19 They will rise up and wage war against you, but they will not overcome you, for I am with you, declares the LORD, to rescue and protect you.
