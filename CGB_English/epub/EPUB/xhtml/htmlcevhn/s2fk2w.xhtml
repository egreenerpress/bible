Psalms Chapter 71

1 In you, O LORD, I place my trust; may I never be brought to shame.

2 Rescue me through your righteousness, and bring me to safety; turn your ear to me and deliver me.

3 Be my secure refuge, a place I can always turn to for safety. You have commanded that I be saved, for you are my rock and my fortress.

4 Rescue me, O my God, from the grasp of the wicked, from the power of the unjust and ruthless.

5 For you are my hope, O Lord God; you have been my confidence since my youth.

6 From the moment I was born, you have supported and sustained me; you brought me safely from my mother’s womb. My praise for you will never cease.

7 I have become a source of astonishment to many, but you are my mighty shelter.

8 May my mouth continually overflow with praise for you and proclaim your glory throughout the entire day.

9 Do not reject me in my old age; do not abandon me when my strength is gone.

10 My enemies speak against me, and those who plot to harm me conspire together.

11 They say, "God has abandoned him; pursue him and seize him, for there is no one to rescue him."

12 O God, do not stay distant from me; O my God, hurry to help me.

13 May those who oppose me be put to shame and utterly defeated; may those who seek to harm me be overwhelmed with disgrace and dishonor.

14 Yet I will maintain my hope without ceasing, and I will continue to praise you ever more abundantly.

15 My mouth will proclaim your righteousness and your saving acts throughout the day, for I cannot fully grasp their vastness.

16 I will move forward in the power of the Lord GOD; I will proclaim your righteousness—yours alone.

17 O God, you have instructed me since my youth, and to this day I have proclaimed your marvelous deeds.

18 Now, even as I grow old and my hair turns gray, O God, do not abandon me. Let me continue to proclaim your mighty deeds to this generation and declare your power to all who are yet to come.

19 Your righteousness, O God, reaches to the heavens; you have accomplished mighty deeds. O God, who can compare to you?

20 You, who have shown me great and severe troubles, will revive me once more and raise me up again from the depths of the earth.

21 You will increase my honor and surround me with your comfort on all sides.

22 I will praise you with the psaltery, O my God, for your faithfulness; I will sing to you with the harp, O Holy One of Israel.

23 My lips will overflow with joy as I sing praises to you, and my soul, which you have rescued and redeemed, will rejoice.

24 My tongue will continually speak of your righteousness throughout the day, for those who seek to harm me are disgraced and put to shame.
