Exodus Chapter 24

1 The Lord said to Moses, "Come up to me, you along with Aaron, Nadab, Abihu, and seventy of the elders of Israel. You are to worship from a distance."

2 Only Moses is to approach the LORD; the others must not come near, nor are the people to go up with him.

3 Moses went and communicated to the people all the words of the LORD and all the ordinances. Then all the people responded together, saying, "We will do everything the LORD has spoken."

4 Moses recorded all the words of the LORD. Early the next morning, he built an altar at the base of the mountain and set up twelve stone pillars, corresponding to the twelve tribes of Israel.

5 He sent young men from among the Israelites, who presented burnt offerings and sacrificed peace offerings of oxen to the LORD.

6 Moses took half of the blood and placed it in bowls, and the other half he sprinkled on the altar.

7 Moses took the book of the covenant and read it aloud in the hearing of the people. They responded, "We will do everything the LORD has commanded and will obey."

8 Moses took the blood and sprinkled it on the people, saying, "This is the blood of the covenant that the LORD has established with you, based on all these words."

9 Then Moses, along with Aaron, Nadab, Abihu, and seventy of the elders of Israel, went up.

10 They beheld the God of Israel, and beneath His feet was something like a pavement of sapphire stone, as clear and radiant as the expanse of the heavens in its purity.

11 And He did not lay His hand on the leaders of the Israelites; they beheld God, and they ate and drank.

12 The LORD said to Moses, "Come up to me on the mountain and stay there. I will give you stone tablets, along with the law and commandments I have written, so that you may instruct the people."

13 Moses stood up, accompanied by his assistant Joshua, and Moses ascended the mountain of God.

14 He said to the elders, "Stay here and wait for us until we return to you. Aaron and Hur are here with you. If anyone has any issues or concerns to address, let them bring them to Aaron and Hur."

15 Moses ascended the mountain, and a cloud enveloped it.

16 The majestic presence of the LORD rested upon Mount Sinai, and the cloud enveloped it for six days. On the seventh day, the LORD called to Moses from within the cloud.
