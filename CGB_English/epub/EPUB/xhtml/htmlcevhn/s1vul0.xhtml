Psalms Chapter 149

1 Praise the LORD! Sing to the LORD a new song, and proclaim His praise in the assembly of His faithful people.

2 Let Israel celebrate the One who created them; let the people of Zion delight in their King.

3 Let them honor his name with dancing; let them sing praises to him with tambourine and harp.

4 The LORD delights in His people; He will adorn the humble with deliverance.

5 Let the faithful ones rejoice in their glorious state; let them sing with joy from their resting places.

6 Let the highest praises of God be on their lips, and a sharp, double-edged sword in their hand.

7 To carry out judgment against the nations and bring retribution upon the peoples.

8 To restrain their kings with chains and their nobles with iron shackles;
