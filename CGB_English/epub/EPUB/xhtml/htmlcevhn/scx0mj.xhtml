I Chronicles Chapter 25

1 Furthermore, David and the commanders of the army appointed certain individuals from the sons of Asaph, Heman, and Jeduthun to serve in worship. These men were designated to prophesy, accompanied by harps, lyres, and cymbals. The number of these skilled workers, assigned according to their duties, was as follows:

2 From the descendants of Asaph: Zaccur, Joseph, Nethaniah, and Asarelah. These were the sons of Asaph, who served under their father Asaph’s direction. They prophesied in accordance with the king’s command.

3 Concerning Jeduthun: the sons of Jeduthun were Gedaliah, Zeri, Jeshaiah, Hashabiah, and Mattithiah—six in total. They served under the direction of their father Jeduthun, who prophesied with the accompaniment of a harp, offering thanks and praise to the LORD.

4 Concerning Heman: the sons of Heman were Bukkiah, Mattaniah, Uzziel, Shebuel, Jerimoth, Hananiah, Hanani, Eliathah, Giddalti, Romamti-ezer, Joshbekashah, Mallothi, Hothir, and Mahazioth.

5 All these were the sons of Heman, the king’s seer, who spoke the words of God to exalt His power. And God granted Heman fourteen sons and three daughters.

6 All these individuals were under the direction of their father for the musical worship in the house of the LORD. They played cymbals, lyres, and harps as part of their service in the house of God, carrying out the duties assigned by the king to Asaph, Jeduthun, and Heman.

7 So the total number of them, along with their fellow Levites who were trained in singing the songs of the LORD—all those skilled in music—was two hundred and eighty-eight.

8 They drew lots to determine their duties, group by group, with no distinction between the young and the old, the teacher and the student. Each was assigned their responsibility fairly, regardless of their status or role.

9 The first lot was drawn for Asaph, belonging to Joseph; the second lot went to Gedaliah, along with his brothers and sons, making a total of twelve.

10 The third lot fell to Zaccur, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve in number.

11 The fourth lot fell to Izri, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve in all.

12 The fifth lot fell to Nethaniah, along with his sons, his brothers, and their families, totaling twelve.

13 The sixth lot fell to Bukkiah; he, along with his sons and his relatives, numbered twelve in total.

14 The seventh lot fell to Jesharelah, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve in all.

15 The eighth lot fell to Jeshaiah, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve in number.

16 The ninth lot fell to Mattaniah; he, along with his sons and his relatives, numbered twelve in total.

17 The tenth lot fell to Shimei, along with his sons, his brothers, and their families, totaling twelve in all.

18 The eleventh lot fell to Azareel, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve in number.

19 The twelfth lot fell to Hashabiah, along with his sons, his brothers, and their families, totaling twelve in all.

20 The thirteenth lot fell to Shubael, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve in all.

21 The fourteenth lot fell to Mattithiah, along with his sons and his relatives, making a total of twelve.

22 The fifteenth lot fell to Jeremoth, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve in all.

23 The sixteenth lot fell to Hananiah, along with his sons, his brothers, and their families, totaling twelve.

24 The seventeenth lot fell to Joshbekashah, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve.

25 The eighteenth lot fell to Hanani, along with his sons, his brothers, and their families, totaling twelve.

26 The nineteenth lot fell to Mallothi, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve.

27 The twentieth lot fell to Eliathah, along with his sons and his relatives, making a total of twelve.

28 The twenty-first lot fell to Hothir, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve.

29 The twenty-second lot fell to Giddalti, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve in all.

30 The twenty-third lot fell to Mahazioth, along with his sons and his relatives, making a total of twelve.

31 The twenty-fourth lot fell to Romamti-ezer, along with his sons and his relatives, totaling twelve.
