Job Chapter 26

1 But Job replied and said,

2 How have you assisted the powerless? How do you rescue the arm that lacks strength?

3 How have you advised someone who lacks understanding? And how have you so thoroughly explained the matter as it truly is?

4 To whom have you spoken these words? And whose spirit has come forth from you?

5 Lifeless forms emerge from beneath the waters, along with those who dwell in them.

6 The realm of the dead lies exposed before Him, and the abyss of destruction is utterly uncovered.

7 He spreads out the northern skies over the void and suspends the earth in empty space.

8 He gathers the waters within His dense clouds, yet the clouds do not burst beneath their weight.

9 He veils the presence of His throne and spreads His cloud over it.

10 He has established boundaries for the waters, setting limits that they cannot cross, until the cycle of day and night comes to an end.

11 The very foundations of the heavens quake and are filled with awe at His rebuke.

12 With His mighty power, He parts the sea, and by His wisdom, He shatters the defiant.

13 By His Spirit, He has adorned the heavens; His hand has crafted the coiling serpent.
