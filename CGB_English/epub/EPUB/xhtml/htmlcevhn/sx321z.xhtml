Psalms Chapter 97

1 The LORD reigns; let the earth be filled with joy; let the many islands rejoice because of it.

2 Clouds and deep darkness surround Him; righteousness and justice form the foundation of His throne.

3 A fire goes before Him, consuming His enemies on every side.

4 His lightning flashes illuminated the world; the earth saw it and shook with fear.

5 The mountains flowed like molten wax before the presence of the LORD, before the presence of the Sovereign over all the earth.

6 The skies proclaim his justice, and all humanity beholds his majesty.

7 Let all who serve carved images be put to shame, all who take pride in idols. Bow down before Him, all you divine beings.

8 Zion heard and was filled with joy; the people of Judah rejoiced because of your righteous decrees, O LORD.

9 For you, LORD, are supreme over all the earth; you are lifted up far above every other power and authority.

10 You who love the LORD, reject evil: He protects the lives of His faithful ones; He rescues them from the power of the wicked.

11 Light is destined for the righteous, and joy for those whose hearts are pure.

12 Be glad in the LORD, you who are upright; and offer gratitude when you reflect on his perfect purity.
