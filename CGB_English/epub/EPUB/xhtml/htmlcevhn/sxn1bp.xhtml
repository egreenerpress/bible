Psalms Chapter 34

1 (A Psalm of David, written when he pretended to be insane before Abimelech, who then drove him away, and he left.) I will praise the LORD at all times; His praise will always be on my lips.

2 I will wholeheartedly praise the LORD; those who are humble will hear of it and rejoice.

3 Come, join me in honoring the LORD, and let us together lift high his name in praise.

4 I sought the LORD, and He answered me, rescuing me from all my fears.

5 They turned their gaze to Him and were filled with radiance; their faces were not covered with shame.

6 This humble man called out, and the LORD listened to him, delivering him from all his distress.

7 The angel of the LORD sets up camp around those who honor Him, and He rescues them.

8 Experience and recognize that the LORD is good; truly blessed is the one who places their trust in Him.

9 Revere the LORD, you who are his holy ones, for those who honor him will lack nothing.

10 Even the young lions may grow weak and go hungry, but those who seek the LORD will lack no good thing.

11 Come, children, listen to me; I will instruct you in what it means to revere the LORD.

12 Who is the person who desires a fulfilling life and longs to enjoy many good days?

13 Refrain from speaking evil, and guard your lips from deceitful words.

14 Turn away from wrongdoing and embrace what is good; actively seek peace and strive to make it a reality.

15 The LORD watches over the righteous, and He listens attentively to their pleas.

16 The Lord's presence is set against those who commit evil, with the intent to erase their memory from the earth.

17 When the righteous cry out, the LORD hears them and rescues them from all their distress.

18 The LORD is close to those whose hearts are broken; He rescues those who have a humble and repentant spirit.

19 The righteous face many troubles, but the LORD rescues them from every one.

20 He protects all his bones; not a single one is broken.

21 The wicked will be destroyed by their own evil deeds, and those who despise the righteous will be left in ruin.

22 The LORD rescues the lives of His servants; no one who takes refuge in Him will be left forsaken.
