Proverbs Chapter 9

1 Wisdom has established her house; she has carved out its seven pillars.

2 She has prepared her animals for the feast; she has mixed her wine with spices; she has also set her table with care.

3 She has sent out her servants; she calls out from the highest points of the city.

4 "Whoever is inexperienced, let them come here; and to those who lack understanding, she says,"

5 Come, partake of the food I have prepared, and drink the wine I have carefully mixed.

6 Leave behind foolish ways, and you will truly live; walk in the path of wisdom and understanding.

7 Whoever corrects a mocker brings shame upon themselves, and whoever rebukes a wicked person earns only disgrace.

8 Do not correct a mocker, or he will resent you; but correct a wise person, and he will appreciate you.

9 Instruct a wise person, and they will grow even wiser; teach a righteous person, and they will gain deeper understanding.

10 Reverence for the LORD is the foundation of true wisdom, and insight into the Holy One brings understanding.

11 For through me, your days will be made many, and the years of your life will be increased.

12 If you are wise, your wisdom will benefit you; but if you mock or reject wisdom, you alone will suffer the consequences.

13 A foolish woman is loud and unruly; she is naive and lacks understanding.

14 For she sits at the entrance of her house, on a seat in the prominent places of the city,

15 To summon travelers who continue steadfastly on their paths.

16 "Whoever is inexperienced, let them come here; and to those who lack understanding, she says,"

17 Illicit pleasures may seem delightful, and forbidden things may appear satisfying.

18 But he is unaware that the dead are present there, and that those who enter her house are descending to the depths of the grave.
