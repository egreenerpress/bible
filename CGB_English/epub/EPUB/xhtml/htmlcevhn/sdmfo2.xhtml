Revelation Chapter 8

1 When the seventh seal was opened, there was silence in heaven for about half an hour.

2 I saw seven angels standing in the presence of God, and they were given seven trumpets.

3 Then another angel came and stood at the altar, holding a golden censer. He was given a large amount of incense to offer, along with the prayers of all God’s people, on the golden altar that stands before the throne.

4 And the smoke of the incense, mingled with the prayers of God’s holy people, rose up before God from the hand of the angel.

5 The angel took the incense burner, filled it with fire from the altar, and hurled it down to the earth. At this, there were loud rumblings, peals of thunder, flashes of lightning, and a violent earthquake.

6 And the seven angels who held the seven trumpets made ready to sound them.

7 The first angel blew his trumpet, and there came hail and fire mixed with blood, which were hurled down upon the earth. As a result, a third of all the trees were burned up, and every patch of green grass was consumed by the flames.

8 Then the second angel blew his trumpet, and what appeared to be a massive mountain, ablaze with fire, was hurled into the sea. A third of the sea turned to blood.

9 And a third of the living creatures in the sea perished, and a third of the ships were ruined.

10 Then the third angel blew his trumpet, and a great star, blazing like a torch, fell from heaven. It landed on a third of the rivers and on the springs of water.

11 The name of the star is called Wormwood, and a third of the waters turned bitter like wormwood. Many people died from the waters because they had become poisonous.

12 Then the fourth angel sounded his trumpet, and a third of the sun was struck, along with a third of the moon and a third of the stars. As a result, a third of their light was extinguished, leaving a third of the day without sunlight and a third of the night without the glow of the moon and stars.

13 Then I looked, and I heard an angel flying high in the sky, proclaiming with a loud voice, "Disaster, disaster, disaster to those who dwell on the earth because of the remaining trumpet blasts from the three angels who are about to sound their trumpets!"
