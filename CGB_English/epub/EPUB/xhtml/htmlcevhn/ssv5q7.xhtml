Psalms Chapter 50

1 (A Psalm of Asaph.) The Almighty God, the LORD Himself, has spoken and summoned the earth, from the place where the sun rises to where it sets.

2 From Zion, the pinnacle of beauty, God has radiated His glory.

3 Our God will come and will not remain silent; a consuming fire will blaze before Him, and a mighty storm will rage all around Him.

4 He will summon the heavens above and the earth below to stand as witnesses, so that he may bring judgment upon his people.

5 Assemble my faithful ones before meâ€”those who have entered into a covenant with me through sacrifice.

6 The heavens will proclaim his justice, for God himself is the judge. Selah.

7 Listen, my people, and I will speak; O Israel, and I will bring charges against you: I am God, your God.

8 I will not rebuke you concerning your sacrifices or your burnt offerings, which have been continually presented before me.

9 I will not accept a bull from your household, nor goats from your pens.

10 Every creature of the forest belongs to me, and the livestock that graze on a thousand hills are mine as well.

11 I am fully aware of all the birds that dwell in the mountains, and the wild animals of the open fields belong to me.

12 If I were in need of food, I would not inform you, for the entire world and everything in it belongs to me.

13 Do I consume the flesh of bulls or drink the blood of goats?

14 Present your gratitude to God as an offering, and fulfill the promises you have made to the Most High.

15 Call out to me in times of distress; I will rescue you, and you will honor me.

16 But God says to the wicked, "What right do you have to recite my laws or to speak of my covenant with your mouth?"

17 Because you despise correction and reject my teachings, casting them aside.

18 When you saw a thief, you joined with him and became a partner with adulterers.

19 You use your mouth to speak evil, and your tongue crafts deceit.

20 You sit and speak against your brother; you slander the son of your own mother.

21 You have done these things, and I remained silent; you assumed that I was just like you. But I will confront you and lay out the charges clearly before your eyes.

22 Consider this carefully, you who have forgotten God, or I will tear you apart, and there will be no one to rescue you.

23 Whoever offers praise honors me, and to the one who lives with integrity, I will reveal the salvation of God.
