Psalms Chapter 30

1 (A Psalm and Song for the dedication of Davidâ€™s house.) I will exalt you, O LORD, for you have raised me up and have not allowed my enemies to gloat over me.

2 O LORD, my God, I called out to you, and you restored me to health.

3 O LORD, you have rescued my life from the grave; you have preserved me, so that I would not descend into the pit.

4 Sing to the LORD, all you who are faithful to him, and offer thanks as you reflect on his perfect holiness.

5 For his anger lasts only a moment, but his favor brings life; though sorrow may linger through the night, joy arrives with the dawn.

6 In my time of prosperity, I declared, "I will never be shaken."

7 LORD, by your gracious favor, you have established my mountain to stand firm and secure; but when you hid your face, I was filled with distress.

8 I called out to you, O LORD; and to the LORD I earnestly pleaded for help.

9 What benefit is there in my death, when I descend to the grave? Can the dust praise you? Can it proclaim your faithfulness?

10 Listen, O LORD, and show me mercy; LORD, be my helper.

11 You have transformed my mourning into joyful dancing; you have removed my sackcloth of grief and clothed me with gladness.

12 So that my innermost being may sing your praises and never fall silent. O LORD, my God, I will give you thanks forever.
