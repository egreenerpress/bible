Psalms Chapter 113

1 Praise the LORD! Praise Him, all you who serve the LORD; praise the name of the LORD.

2 Praise be to the name of the LORD, both now and forevermore.

3 From the moment the sun rises until it sets, the name of the LORD is worthy of praise.

4 The LORD is exalted above all nations, and His majesty surpasses the heavens.

5 Who is like the LORD our God, who reigns in majesty on high?

6 Who stoops down to observe the things in heaven and on earth!

7 He lifts the poor from the dust and raises the needy from the ash heap.

8 So that he may place him among the leaders, even among the prominent figures of his people.

9 He transforms the childless woman into a homemaker, making her a joyful mother of children. Give praise to the LORD.
