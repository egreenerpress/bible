Genesis Chapter 36

1 These are the descendants of Esau, who is also known as Edom.

2 Esau married women from among the daughters of Canaan: Adah, the daughter of Elon the Hittite, and Oholibamah, the daughter of Anah and granddaughter of Zibeon the Hivite.

3 And Basemath, the daughter of Ishmael and sister of Nebaioth.

4 Adah gave birth to Eliphaz for Esau, and Bashemath gave birth to Reuel.

5 Aholibamah gave birth to Jeush, Jaalam, and Korah. These were the sons of Esau, born to him in the land of Canaan.

6 Esau took his wives, his sons, his daughters, all the members of his household, his livestock, all his animals, and all the possessions he had acquired in the land of Canaan, and he moved to a different region to distance himself from his brother Jacob.

7 Their possessions were so abundant that they could no longer live together in the same place, and the land where they were staying as foreigners could not support them because of the large number of livestock they owned.

8 So Esau settled in the hill country of Seir; Esau is also known as Edom.

9 This is the account of Esau, the father of the Edomites, in the region of Mount Seir.

10 Here are the names of Esau’s sons: Eliphaz, the son of Adah, Esau’s wife, and Reuel, the son of Basemath, Esau’s wife.

11 The sons of Eliphaz were Teman, Omar, Zepho, Gatam, and Kenaz.

12 Timna was the concubine of Eliphaz, the son of Esau, and she bore Amalek to Eliphaz. These were the descendants of Adah, Esau’s wife.

13 Here are the sons of Reuel: Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah. These were the sons of Basemath, Esau’s wife.

14 These were the sons of Aholibamah, the daughter of Anah and granddaughter of Zibeon, who was Esau’s wife. She bore Esau three sons: Jeush, Jaalam, and Korah.

15 These were the tribal leaders among the descendants of Esau: the sons of Eliphaz, Esau's firstborn. Their leaders were Teman, Omar, Zepho, and Kenaz.

16 Chief Korah, chief Gatam, and chief Amalek: these were the chiefs descended from Eliphaz in the land of Edom; they were the sons of Adah.

17 These are the sons of Reuel, Esau’s son: chief Nahath, chief Zerah, chief Shammah, and chief Mizzah. These are the chiefs descended from Reuel in the land of Edom. They are the sons of Basemath, Esau’s wife.

18 These are the sons of Aholibamah, Esau’s wife: Chief Jeush, Chief Jaalam, and Chief Korah. These were the chiefs descended from Aholibamah, the daughter of Anah, who was Esau’s wife.

19 These are the descendants of Esau, also known as Edom, and these are their tribal leaders.

20 These are the descendants of Seir the Horite, who lived in the land: Lotan, Shobal, Zibeon, and Anah.

21 Dishon, Ezer, and Dishan: these were the chiefs of the Horites, the descendants of Seir in the land of Edom.

22 The descendants of Lotan were Hori and Hemam, and Lotan’s sister was named Timna.

23 The descendants of Shobal were as follows: Alvan, Manahath, Ebal, Shepho, and Onam.

24 These are the descendants of Zibeon: both Ajah and Anah. This is the same Anah who discovered the hot springs in the wilderness while he was tending the donkeys of his father Zibeon.

25 The descendants of Anah were as follows: Dishon and Aholibamah, who was Anah’s daughter.

26 These are the descendants of Dishon: Hemdan, Eshban, Ithran, and Cheran.

27 The descendants of Ezer are as follows: Bilhan, Zaavan, and Akan.

28 The descendants of Dishan are as follows: Uz and Aran.

29 These are the chiefs who descended from the Horites: Chief Lotan, Chief Shobal, Chief Zibeon, and Chief Anah.

30 Chief Dishon, chief Ezer, and chief Dishan: these were the leaders descended from Hori, among their chiefs in the land of Seir.

31 These are the kings who ruled in the land of Edom before any king reigned over the Israelites.

32 Bela, the son of Beor, became king in Edom, and the name of his city was Dinhabah.

33 Bela died, and Jobab, the son of Zerah from Bozrah, succeeded him as king.

34 Jobab passed away, and Husham from the land of Teman succeeded him as ruler.

35 Husham died, and Hadad, the son of Bedad, succeeded him as king. Hadad defeated Midian in the territory of Moab, and the name of his city was Avith.

36 And Hadad died, and Samlah from Masrekah became king in his place.

37 Samlah died, and Saul from Rehoboth by the river succeeded him as king.

38 And Saul passed away, and Baal-hanan, the son of Achbor, succeeded him as king.

39 Baal-hanan, the son of Achbor, died, and Hadar succeeded him as king. The name of his city was Pau, and his wife’s name was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, who was the daughter of Mezahab.

40 Here are the names of the tribal chiefs descended from Esau, listed according to their clans and regions, each identified by name: Chief Timnah, Chief Alvah, Chief Jetheth.

41 Chief Aholibamah, chief Elah, chief Pinon.

42 Chief Kenaz, chief Teman, chief Mibzar.

43 Chief Magdiel, chief Iram: these were the leaders of Edom, according to their settlements in the land they possessed. This was Esau, the father of the Edomites.
