I John Chapter 1

1 What existed from the very beginning, what we have heard, what we have seen with our own eyes, what we have closely observed, and what our hands have touchedâ€”this is the Word of life.

2 The life was revealed, and we have seen it. We testify to it and proclaim to you the eternal life that was with the Father and has been made known to us.

3 We are declaring to you what we have seen and heard, so that you may also share in the fellowship we have. And indeed, our fellowship is with the Father and with His Son, Jesus Christ.

4 We are writing these things to you so that your joy may be complete.

5 This is the message we have heard from Him and now declare to you: God is light, and in Him there is no darkness whatsoever.

6 If we claim to have a close relationship with him but continue to live in darkness, we are lying and not living according to the truth.

7 However, if we live in the light, just as he exists in the light, we share a close relationship with one another, and the blood of Jesus Christ, his Son, purifies us from every sin.

8 If we claim to be without sin, we are only fooling ourselves, and the truth has no place in us.

9 If we acknowledge and admit our sins, God is trustworthy and righteous to forgive us for our wrongdoings and to purify us from all ungodliness.

10 If we claim that we have not sinned, we are calling God a liar, and his truth does not dwell within us.
