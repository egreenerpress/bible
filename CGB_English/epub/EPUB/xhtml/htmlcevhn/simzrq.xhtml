Amos Chapter 9

1 I saw the Lord standing by the altar, and He said, "Strike the top of the doorway so that the thresholds tremble. Shatter them all, breaking them at the head. I will kill the last of them with the sword; not one who flees will escape, and not one who tries to get away will be saved."

2 Even if they dig down to the realm of the dead, my hand will reach them and pull them out; even if they ascend to the heavens, I will bring them down from there.

3 Even if they try to hide on the summit of Mount Carmel, I will search for them and bring them out from there. And if they conceal themselves from my sight in the depths of the sea, I will command the serpent to strike them there.

4 Even if they are taken captive by their enemies, I will command the sword to pursue them, and it will strike them down. I will fix my gaze upon them, not for their benefit, but to bring them harm.

5 The Lord GOD Almighty is the one who touches the land, and it will melt away. All who live there will mourn, and the land will rise up like a surging flood, only to be overwhelmed and swept away, as by the floodwaters of Egypt.

6 It is He who builds His lofty chambers in the heavens and establishes His vaulted dome over the earth; He who summons the waters of the sea and pours them out upon the surface of the earth—the LORD is His name.

7 Are you not like the descendants of the Cushites to me, O people of Israel? declares the LORD. Did I not bring Israel up from the land of Egypt, and the Philistines from Caphtor, and the Arameans from Kir?

8 Look, the eyes of the Lord GOD are fixed upon the sinful kingdom, and I will annihilate it from the face of the earth. Yet, I will not completely destroy the house of Jacob, declares the LORD.

9 Listen carefully, for I am about to issue a command: I will sift the house of Israel among all the nations, just as grain is sifted in a sieve. Yet not even the smallest grain will fall to the ground and be lost.

10 All those among my people who persist in sin will perish by the sword—those who arrogantly declare, "Disaster will not overtake us or confront us."

11 On that day, I will restore the fallen dwelling of David and repair its broken walls. I will rebuild its ruins and restore it to its former glory, as it was in days gone by.

12 So that they may take possession of what remains of Edom and all the nations that bear my name, declares the LORD, who will accomplish this.

13 Listen, the time is coming, declares the LORD, when the plowman will work right behind the reaper, and the grape-treader will follow closely after the one sowing seed. The mountains will flow with sweet wine, and every hill will dissolve with abundance.

14 I will restore the fortunes of my people Israel, and they will rebuild the ruined cities and settle in them. They will plant vineyards and drink their wine; they will cultivate gardens and enjoy their fruit.

15 I will establish them firmly in their own land, and they will never again be uprooted from the land I have given them, declares the LORD your God.
