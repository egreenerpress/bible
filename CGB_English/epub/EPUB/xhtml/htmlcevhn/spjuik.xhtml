Psalms Chapter 140

1 (For the music director. A psalm of David.) O LORD, rescue me from the wicked; protect me from the violent.

2 They devise wicked schemes in their hearts; they are constantly assembling together for conflict.

3 They have made their tongues as sharp as a serpent's; the venom of vipers lies beneath their lips. Pause and reflect on this.

4 Protect me, O LORD, from the grasp of the wicked; guard me from the violent, who scheme to trip me up and bring me down.

5 The arrogant have secretly set traps for me; they have laid out cords and spread a net along the path; they have prepared snares to catch me. Selah.

6 I declared to the LORD, "You are my God. Listen, O LORD, to the sound of my earnest prayers."

7 O LORD, my God, the source of my deliverance and strength, you have protected me in the midst of conflict and shielded me in the time of war.

8 Do not grant, O LORD, the desires of the wicked; do not let their evil plans succeed, or they will become proud and boastful. Selah.

9 As for the leader of those who surround me, may the harm caused by their own words overwhelm them.

10 May burning coals rain down upon them; may they be thrown into the fire, into deep pits, so that they cannot rise again.

11 Do not allow a deceitful or wicked person to gain a foothold in the land; for violence will pursue the ruthless, seeking to bring about their downfall.

12 I am certain that the LORD will uphold the cause of those who are oppressed and defend the rights of the needy.

13 Certainly, those who are righteous will offer praise to your name; the upright will live in your presence.
