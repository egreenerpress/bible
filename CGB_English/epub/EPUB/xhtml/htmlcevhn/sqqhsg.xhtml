Psalms Chapter 115

1 Not to us, O LORD, not to us, but to your name give glory, because of your steadfast love and your faithfulness.

2 Why should the nations say, "Where is their God now?"

3 But our God is in the heavens; He does all that He desires.

4 Their idols are made of silver and gold, crafted by human hands.

5 They have mouths, but they cannot speak; they have eyes, but they cannot see.

6 They have ears, but they cannot hear; they have noses, but they cannot smell.

7 They have hands, but they cannot grasp; they have feet, but they cannot walk; nor can they make a sound with their throats.

8 Those who create idols become like them; the same is true for all who place their trust in them.

9 O Israel, place your trust in the LORD; He is your help and your protection.

10 House of Aaron, place your trust in the LORD; He is your help and your protection.

11 You who revere the LORD, place your trust in Him; He is your protector and your defense.

12 The LORD has remembered us and will show us favor; He will bless us, He will bless the house of Israel, and He will bless the house of Aaron.

13 He will bless those who revere the LORD, both the humble and the mighty.

14 The LORD will cause you to flourish abundantly, both you and your descendants.

15 You are blessed by the LORD, the Creator of heaven and earth.

16 The heavens, even the highest heavens, belong to the LORD, but the earth he has entrusted to humanity.

17 The dead do not praise the LORD, nor do those who descend into the silence of the grave.

18 From this moment onward and forevermore, we will praise the LORD. Let all honor and glory be given to the LORD.
