Psalms Chapter 147

1 Praise the LORD! It is good to sing praises to our God; it is a joyful and fitting act, and praise is beautiful.

2 The LORD is the one who rebuilds Jerusalem; he gathers the scattered exiles of Israel.

3 He heals those whose hearts are shattered and mends their deep wounds.

4 He determines the number of the stars; he gives each one its name.

5 Our Lord is great and mighty in power; his wisdom is boundless and without limit.

6 The LORD raises up those who are humble; He brings down the wicked to the ground.

7 Sing to the LORD with gratitude; offer praise to our God with the harp.

8 He who covers the sky with clouds, who provides rain for the earth, and who causes grass to grow on the mountains.

9 He provides food for the animals and for the young ravens when they call out.

10 The Lord does not find joy in the power of horses, nor does He take pleasure in the strength of human legs.

11 The LORD delights in those who revere Him, in those who place their trust in His steadfast love.

12 Praise the LORD, O Jerusalem; give honor to your God, O Zion.

13 For He has made the bars of your gates strong; He has blessed your children who dwell within you.

14 He establishes peace within your boundaries and provides you with the finest wheat, satisfying you abundantly.

15 He issues His decree across the earth; His word speeds swiftly to its fulfillment.

16 He sends the snow like a blanket of wool; He spreads the frost like a scattering of ashes.

17 He hurls down his ice like crumbs of bread; who can endure the biting cold he sends?

18 He sends forth his command, and they melt away; he makes his wind blow, and the waters begin to flow.

19 He reveals his word to Jacob, his laws and his decrees to Israel.

20 He has not treated any other nation in this way; as for His laws and decrees, they have not experienced them. Praise the LORD.
