Isaiah Chapter 46

1 Bel bows down, Nebo stoops low; their idols are loaded onto animals, carried on the backs of cattle. These burdens are heavy, a weight too great for the weary animals to bear.

2 They bend low, they crouch down together; they are unable to save the burden they carry, and they themselves are taken away into captivity.

3 Listen to me, O descendants of Jacob, and all the remaining people of Israel, whom I have upheld since your birth, whom I have carried since you were in the womb.

4 Even when you grow old, I will remain the same; and even when your hair turns gray, I will sustain you. I created you, and I will continue to support you; I will carry you and rescue you.

5 To whom will you compare me, or consider me equal? Who can you liken me to, so that we might be seen as similar?

6 They pour out gold from their bags and carefully measure silver on the scales. They hire a craftsman to fashion it into a god, and then they bow down and worship it.

7 They carry him on their shoulders, they lift him up and place him in his position, and there he stands. He cannot move from his place. Even if someone cries out to him, he cannot answer or rescue them from their distress.

8 Consider this carefully and take courage; reflect on it deeply, you who have turned away from what is right.

9 Call to mind the ancient events of the past, for I am God, and there is no other; I am God, and no one is like me.

10 From the very start, I reveal what will happen in the end, and from ancient times, I declare what has not yet come to pass. I say, "My purpose will remain firm, and I will accomplish all that I desire."

11 I am summoning a swift and voracious bird from the east, a man from a distant land who will carry out my purpose. Indeed, I have declared it, and I will surely bring it to pass; I have planned it, and I will certainly accomplish it.

12 Listen to me, you who are stubborn-hearted, who are distant from living righteously:

13 I am bringing my righteousness near; it will not be distant, and my salvation will not be delayed. I will establish salvation in Zion, and I will bestow my glory upon Israel.
