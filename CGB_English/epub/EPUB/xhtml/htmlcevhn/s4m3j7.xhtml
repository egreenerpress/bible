Psalms Chapter 100

1 (A Psalm of praise.) Shout joyfully to the LORD, all the earth!

2 Worship the LORD with joy; enter into his presence with songs of praise.

3 Understand this: the LORD is God. He is the one who created us; we did not create ourselves. We are his people, the flock under his care.

4 Approach His gates with gratitude, and enter His courts with songs of praise. Give thanks to Him and honor His name.

5 For the LORD is good; his steadfast love endures forever, and his faithfulness continues through all generations.
