Psalms Chapter 54

1 (For the director of music, to be played on stringed instruments. A contemplative psalm of David, written when the Ziphites came and said to Saul, "Is David not hiding among us?") O God, deliver me by the power of your name, and vindicate me by your might.

2 Listen to my prayer, O God; pay attention to the words I speak.

3 Foreign adversaries have risen against me, and violent oppressors seek to take my life; they do not acknowledge or consider God in their actions. Pause and reflect on this.

4 See, God is my helper; the Lord is with those who sustain my life.

5 He will repay my enemies with harm; destroy them according to your faithfulness.

6 I will offer you a willing sacrifice; I will give thanks to your name, O LORD, for it is good.

7 For He has rescued me from all my troubles, and my eyes have witnessed His judgment upon my enemies.
