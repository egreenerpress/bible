Numbers Chapter 34

1 The LORD spoke to Moses, saying,

2 Instruct the people of Israel, saying to them: When you enter the land of Canaan—this is the land that will be allotted to you as an inheritance, the land of Canaan with its boundaries—

3 Your southern boundary will begin at the wilderness of Zin, running alongside the territory of Edom. The southern border will extend to the farthest edge of the Dead Sea, toward the east.

4 Your boundary will turn from the south toward the ascent of Akrabbim and continue to Zin. Its southern limit will extend from there to Kadesh-barnea, then proceed to Hazar-addar, and continue on to Azmon.

5 The boundary will curve from Azmon to the Brook of Egypt, and its endpoint will be at the Mediterranean Sea.

6 As for the western boundary, the Great Sea will serve as your border; this will be your western boundary.

7 And this will be your northern boundary: from the Mediterranean Sea, you shall mark out a line to Mount Hor.

8 From Mount Hor, you shall mark out your boundary as far as the entrance of Hamath, and the boundary will extend to Zedad.

9 The boundary will continue to Ziphron, and its endpoint will be at Hazar-enan. This will mark your northern border.

10 You shall mark out your eastern boundary from Hazar-enan to Shepham.

11 The boundary will extend from Shepham to Riblah, on the eastern side of Ain. From there, the border will descend and reach the edge of the Sea of Chinnereth to the east.

12 The boundary will extend down to the Jordan River, and its endpoint will be at the Salt Sea. This will be your land, with its surrounding borders on every side.

13 Moses instructed the Israelites, saying, "This is the land that you are to receive as your inheritance by casting lots, as the LORD has commanded to be given to the nine tribes and the half-tribe."

14 For the tribe of Reuben, grouped by their ancestral families, and the tribe of Gad, also grouped by their ancestral families, have already received their allotted inheritance. Additionally, half of the tribe of Manasseh has received its inheritance.

15 The two tribes and the half-tribe have received their allotted territory on this side of the Jordan, near Jericho, to the east, toward the direction of the sunrise.

16 The LORD spoke to Moses, saying,

17 These are the names of the men who will assign the land to you: Eleazar the priest and Joshua, the son of Nun.

18 You shall select one leader from each tribe to assist in apportioning the land as an inheritance.

19 The names of the men are as follows: From the tribe of Judah, Caleb son of Jephunneh.

20 From the tribe of Simeon, Shemuel son of Ammihud.

21 From the tribe of Benjamin, Elidad, the son of Chislon.

22 And the leader of the tribe of the descendants of Dan was Bukki, the son of Jogli.

23 The leader representing the descendants of Joseph, from the tribe of Manasseh, was Hanniel, the son of Ephod.

24 And the leader of the tribe of the descendants of Ephraim was Kemuel, the son of Shiphtan.

25 And the leader of the tribe of Zebulun was Elizaphan, the son of Parnach.

26 And the leader of the tribe of Issachar was Paltiel, the son of Azzan.

27 And the leader of the tribe of the descendants of Asher was Ahihud, the son of Shelomi.

28 And the leader of the tribe of Naphtali was Pedahel, the son of Ammihud.

29 These are the ones whom the LORD instructed to distribute the inheritance among the Israelites in the land of Canaan.
