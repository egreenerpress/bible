Psalms Chapter 129

1 (A song of ascents.) From my earliest days, they have repeatedly oppressed me—let Israel now declare this:

2 Many times they have oppressed me since my youth, yet they have not overcome me.

3 The plowmen plowed across my back; they made their furrows long.

4 The LORD is just; He has severed the bonds of the wicked.

5 May all who despise Zion be utterly disgraced and driven away.

6 Let them be like the grass that grows on rooftops, which withers before it can fully sprout.

7 The mower does not fill his hand with it, nor does the one who gathers the sheaves fill his arms.

8 Those who pass by do not say, "May the LORD’s blessing be upon you." Nor do they declare, "We bless you in the name of the LORD."
