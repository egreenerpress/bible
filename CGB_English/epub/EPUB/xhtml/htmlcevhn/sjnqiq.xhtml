Psalms Chapter 35

1 (A Psalm of David.) Lord, take up my case against those who oppose me; contend with those who contend against me.

2 Take up your shield and armor, and rise up to defend and assist me.

3 Extend your spear and block the path of those who pursue me. Declare to my soul, "I am your deliverance."

4 May those who seek to take my life be disgraced and put to shame; may those who plot my harm be turned back and utterly confounded.

5 Let them be like chaff blown away by the wind, and may the angel of the LORD pursue them.

6 May their path be shrouded in darkness and treacherous to tread, and may the angel of the LORD pursue them relentlessly.

7 They have set a hidden trap for me without any just reason; they have dug a pit to ensnare me, though I have done nothing to deserve it.

8 May sudden ruin overtake him without warning; may the trap he has set for others ensnare him instead. Let him fall headlong into the very destruction he intended for others.

9 And my soul will find joy in the LORD; it will delight in His deliverance.

10 Every part of my being will declare, "LORD, who is like you? You rescue the oppressed from those who overpower them, and you save the poor and needy from those who exploit them."

11 False witnesses came forward against me; they accused me of things I did not know or commit.

12 They repaid me with evil in return for the good I had done, bringing ruin to my very being.

13 As for me, when they were ill, I wore sackcloth as a sign of mourning; I humbled myself through fasting, and my heartfelt prayer turned back upon me.

14 I acted as if he were my close friend or brother; I deeply mourned and bowed down in sorrow, as one grieving for his mother.

15 But when I was in distress, they took joy and gathered together against me. Even the lowly and despised assembled against me, though I was unaware of it. They tore at me without ceasing.

16 At their festive gatherings, those deceitful mockers turned against me, grinding their teeth in hostility.

17 Lord, how long will you remain silent? Save my life from their destruction, my precious one from the power of the lions.

18 I will give you thanks in the midst of the great assembly; I will praise you before a multitude of people.

19 Do not let my enemies, who act unjustly, take joy in my suffering; nor let those who hate me without reason gloat or mock me with their eyes.

20 For they do not speak of peace; instead, they plot deceitful schemes against those who live peacefully in the land.

21 Indeed, they spoke against me with open hostility, saying, "Aha! Aha! We have seen it with our own eyes!"

22 You have witnessed this, O LORD; do not remain silent. O Lord, do not stay distant from me.

23 Rise up and take action to defend me; bring justice to my cause, my God and my Lord.

24 "Lord my God, judge me according to your perfect justice, and do not allow them to gloat over me."

25 Do not let them think in their hearts, "Ah, this is exactly what we wanted!" Nor let them say, "We have utterly destroyed him."

26 May those who take pleasure in my suffering be disgraced and utterly confounded; may they be covered with shame and dishonor who exalt themselves against me.

27 Let those who support my righteous cause rejoice and be glad; may they continually proclaim, "May the LORD be exalted, for He delights in the well-being of His servant."

28 And my tongue will continually proclaim your justice and sing your praises throughout the day.
