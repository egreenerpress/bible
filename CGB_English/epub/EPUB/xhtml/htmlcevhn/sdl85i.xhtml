Genesis Chapter 10

1 This is the account of Noah’s descendants—Shem, Ham, and Japheth. After the flood, sons were born to them.

2 The descendants of Japheth were Gomer, Magog, Madai, Javan, Tubal, Meshech, and Tiras.

3 The sons of Gomer were Ashkenaz, Riphath, and Togarmah.

4 The descendants of Javan were Elishah, Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.

5 From these, the coastlands of the nations were spread out into their territories, each according to their languages, their clans, and their nations.

6 The descendants of Ham were Cush, Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.

7 The descendants of Cush were Seba, Havilah, Sabtah, Raamah, and Sabtecha. The descendants of Raamah were Sheba and Dedan.

8 Cush fathered Nimrod, who became a powerful and influential figure in the land.

9 He was a powerful hunter in the presence of the LORD; therefore, it is said, "Like Nimrod, the mighty hunter in the presence of the LORD."

10 The foundation of his kingdom began with Babel, Erech, Accad, and Calneh, all located in the land of Shinar.

11 From that land, Asshur went out and built Nineveh, along with the city of Rehoboth and Calah.

12 And Resen lies between Nineveh and Calah; it is a significant city.

13 Mizraim became the father of Ludim, Anamim, Lehabim, and Naphtuhim.

14 And Pathrusim, and Casluhim (from whom the Philistines descended), and Caphtorim.

15 Canaan became the father of Sidon, his firstborn, and Heth.

16 The Jebusites, the Amorites, and the Girgashites...

17 And the Hivites, the Arkites, and the Sinites.

18 The Arvadites, the Zemarites, and the Hamathites—these were the descendants of Canaan. Later, the clans of the Canaanites spread out and settled in various regions.

19 The territory of the Canaanites extended from Sidon, as you travel toward Gerar, all the way to Gaza, and then continued toward Sodom, Gomorrah, Admah, and Zeboim, reaching as far as Lasha.

20 These are the descendants of Ham, grouped according to their families, languages, territories, and nations.

21 To Shem as well, who was the father of all the descendants of Eber and the brother of Japheth, the older one, children were born.

22 The descendants of Shem were Elam, Asshur, Arphaxad, Lud, and Aram.

23 The descendants of Aram were Uz, Hul, Gether, and Mash.

24 And Arphaxad became the father of Salah, and Salah became the father of Eber.

25 To Eber were born two sons: the name of one was Peleg, because in his time the earth was divided; and his brother’s name was Joktan.

26 Joktan became the father of Almodad, Sheleph, Hazarmaveth, and Jerah.

27 And Hadoram, Uzal, and Diklah.

28 And Obal, Abimael, and Sheba.

29 And Ophir, Havilah, and Jobab—all these were the descendants of Joktan.

30 Their territory extended from Mesha all the way to Sephar, the eastern mountain range.

31 These are the descendants of Shem, grouped according to their families, languages, territories, and nations.

32 These are the family lines of Noah's sons, according to their generations and their nations. From them, the nations spread across the earth after the flood.
