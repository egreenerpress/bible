Psalms Chapter 15

1 (A Psalm of David.) LORD, who is worthy to live in your sacred dwelling? Who may reside on your holy mountain?

2 The one who lives with integrity, acts justly, and speaks truth sincerely from the heart.

3 The one who does not slander with their tongue, nor harm their neighbor, nor bring disgrace upon their neighbor.

4 In the eyes of this person, a wicked individual is despised, but those who honor and revere the LORD are held in high regard. Such a person keeps their word, even when it brings personal harm, and does not waver or change their commitment.

5 The one who does not lend money at excessive interest, nor accepts bribes to harm the innocent. Whoever lives in this way will remain secure and unshaken.
