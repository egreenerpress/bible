John Chapter 14

1 Do not let your heart be distressed. You trust in God; trust also in me.

2 In my Father’s house, there are many dwelling places. If this were not true, I would have told you. I am going to prepare a place for you.

3 And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will return and bring you to be with me, so that where I am, you may also be.

4 "And where I am going, you know, and the way to it you also know."

5 Thomas said to him, "Lord, we do not know where you are going. How can we know the way?"

6 Jesus said to him, "I am the path, the truth, and the source of life. No one can come to the Father except through me."

7 If you had truly known me, you would have also known my Father. But from now on, you do know Him, and you have seen Him.

8 Philip said to him, "Lord, show us the Father, and that will be enough for us."

9 Jesus said to him, "Have I been with you for such a long time, and still you do not know me, Philip? Whoever has seen me has seen the Father. How can you say, ‘Show us the Father’?"

10 Do you not believe that I am in the Father and the Father is in me? The words I speak to you are not spoken on my own authority, but the Father, who dwells within me, is the one doing the works.

11 Trust that I am united with the Father, and the Father is united with me. If you cannot believe this based on my words alone, then believe because of the works I have done.

12 Truly, truly, I tell you, whoever believes in me will also do the works that I am doing. In fact, they will do even greater works than these, because I am going to the Father.

13 And whatever you ask in my name, I will do it, so that the Father may be honored through the Son.

14 If you ask for anything in my name, I will do it.

15 If you truly love me, you will follow and obey my instructions.

16 I will ask the Father, and He will give you another Helper, who will remain with you forever.

17 This is the Spirit of truth, whom the world cannot accept, because it does not see Him or recognize Him. But you know Him, because He remains with you and will be within you.

18 I will not abandon you as orphans; I will come to you.

19 In a short time, the world will no longer see me, but you will see me. Because I live, you also will live.

20 On that day, you will understand that I am united with the Father, and you are united with me, and I am united with you.

21 The one who has my commandments and keeps them is the one who truly loves me. And the one who loves me will be loved by my Father, and I will love them as well, and I will reveal myself to them.

22 Judas (not Iscariot) said to Him, “Lord, how is it that You will reveal Yourself to us, but not to the world?”

23 Jesus replied to him, "If anyone loves me, they will obey my teachings. My Father will love them, and we will come to them and make our home with them."

24 Whoever does not love me does not follow my teachings. And the message you hear is not from me, but from the Father who sent me.

25 "I have told you these things while I am still here with you."

26 However, the Helper, who is the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in my name, will teach you everything and remind you of all that I have said to you.

27 I leave you with peace; my own peace I give to you. I do not give it as the world gives. Do not let your hearts be troubled, and do not be afraid.

28 You have heard me say to you, "I am going away, and I will return to you." If you truly loved me, you would rejoice because I am going to the Father, for the Father is greater than I.

29 I have told you about these things beforehand, so that when they happen, you may believe.

30 From now on, I will not speak much with you, for the ruler of this world is coming. He has no power over me, for there is nothing in me that belongs to him.

31 But this is so that the world may understand that I love the Father and that I am doing exactly as the Father has commanded me. Come, let us leave this place and go forward.
