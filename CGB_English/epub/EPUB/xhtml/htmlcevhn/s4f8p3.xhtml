Exodus Chapter 25

1 The LORD spoke to Moses, saying,

2 Tell the Israelites to bring me an offering. From everyone who is willing to give freely from their heart, you are to receive the offering for me.

3 This is the contribution you are to receive from them: gold, silver, and bronze.

4 The materials included blue, purple, and scarlet cloth, fine linen, and goats' hair.

5 And rams' skins dyed red, and fine leather, and acacia wood.

6 Oil for the lamps, spices for the sacred anointing oil, and for the fragrant incense.

7 Onyx stones, as well as gemstones to be mounted in the ephod and the breastplate.

8 "Have them construct a sacred dwelling place for me, so that I may reside among them."

9 According to everything I have shown you—the design of the tabernacle and the pattern of all its furnishings—you are to construct it in exactly this manner.

10 They are to construct an ark out of acacia wood. It shall be two and a half cubits in length, one and a half cubits in width, and one and a half cubits in height.

11 You shall cover it entirely with pure gold, both inside and out, and you shall craft a gold molding around its edges.

12 You shall make four gold rings for it and attach them to its four corners. Two rings shall be on one side of it, and two rings on the other side.

13 You shall make poles from acacia wood and cover them with gold.

14 You shall insert the poles into the rings on the sides of the ark, so that the ark can be carried with them.

15 The poles shall remain in the rings of the ark; they must not be removed from it.

16 You are to place inside the ark the covenant law that I will give to you.

17 You shall construct a mercy seat of pure gold: its length shall be two and a half cubits, and its width one and a half cubits.

18 You shall craft two cherubim out of gold, hammered from a single piece, and place them at the two ends of the mercy seat.

19 And you shall make one cherub at one end and the other cherub at the opposite end. The cherubim shall be made as part of the mercy seat, one at each of its two ends.

20 The cherubim shall stretch out their wings upward, covering the mercy seat with their wings, and their faces shall be turned toward one another. The faces of the cherubim shall be directed toward the mercy seat.

21 You shall place the mercy seat on top of the ark, and inside the ark you shall put the testimony that I will give to you.

22 There I will meet with you, and I will speak with you from above the mercy seat, from between the two cherubim that are on the ark of the covenant, concerning all that I will command you to instruct the people of Israel.

23 You shall also construct a table of acacia wood: its length shall be three feet, its width one and a half feet, and its height two and a quarter feet.

24 You shall cover it entirely with pure gold and craft a gold molding around its edges.

25 You shall create a rim around it, about a hand's width wide, and you shall fashion a golden molding to go around the rim.

26 You shall make four gold rings for it and attach the rings to the four corners at its four legs.

27 The rings shall be positioned opposite the frame, serving as holders for the poles used to carry the table.

28 You shall make the poles out of acacia wood and cover them with gold, so that the table can be carried with them.

29 You shall make its dishes, its spoons, its covers, and its bowls for pouring offerings—all of them you are to make out of pure gold.

30 You shall place the bread of the Presence on the table before me at all times.

31 You shall make a lampstand of pure gold. The lampstand shall be crafted by hammering it into shape. Its base, its branches, its cups, its ornamental knobs, and its blossoms shall all be made from the same piece of gold.

32 The lampstand shall have six branches extending from its sides—three branches on one side and three branches on the other side.

33 Three cups shaped like almond blossoms, each with a bud and a flower, were on one branch; and three cups shaped like almond blossoms, each with a bud and a flower, were on the other branch. This was the design for the six branches extending from the lampstand.

34 The lampstand shall have four cups shaped like almond blossoms, each with its ornamental knob and flower.

35 And there shall be a decorative bud beneath each pair of branches, a bud beneath the first pair, a bud beneath the second pair, and a bud beneath the third pair, corresponding to the six branches that extend from the lampstand.

36 The decorative buds and branches shall be made as one piece with it; the entire lampstand shall be crafted from a single piece of pure gold, hammered into shape.

37 You shall make seven lamps for it, and you shall set up the lamps so that they shine their light toward the front of the lampstand.

38 The tongs and the ash trays for it shall be made of pure gold.

39 He shall make it from a talent of pure gold, along with all its vessels.

40 Be sure to construct them exactly according to the design that was shown to you on the mountain.
