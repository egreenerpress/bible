Psalms Chapter 47

1 (To the director of music. A psalm for the descendants of Korah.) Clap your hands, all you nations; shout to God with cries of joy!

2 For the LORD, the Most High, is awe-inspiring; He is the great King over all the earth.

3 He will bring the peoples under our authority and make the nations submit to our rule.

4 He will select our inheritance for us, the pride and glory of Jacob, whom He has loved. Pause and reflect on this.

5 God has ascended with a triumphant shout; the LORD has gone up with the blast of the trumpet.

6 Offer songs of praise to God, sing out in worship; lift up your voices in honor of our King, sing praises to Him.

7 For God reigns as the supreme King over all the earth; offer your praises with thoughtful reverence and insight.

8 God rules over the nations; God is seated on his holy throne.

9 The leaders of the nations have assembled, along with the people of the God of Abraham. For the rulers and protectors of the earth belong to God; He is supremely exalted.
