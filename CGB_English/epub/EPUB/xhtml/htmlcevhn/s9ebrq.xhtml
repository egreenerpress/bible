Psalms Chapter 3

1 (A Psalm of David, written when he fled from his son Absalom.) O LORD, how many are my adversaries! So many rise up against me.

2 Many people say concerning me, "There is no deliverance for him from God." Selah.

3 But you, O LORD, are my protector; you are my honor and the one who lifts my head high.

4 I called out to the LORD with my voice, and he answered me from his holy mountain. Selah.

5 I lay down and slept; I awoke, for the LORD upheld me.

6 I will not fear even if tens of thousands of people rise up against me and surround me on every side.

7 Rise up, O LORD; rescue me, my God! For you have struck all my enemies on the jaw; you have shattered the teeth of the wicked.

8 Salvation comes from the LORD alone; your blessing rests upon your people. Selah.
