Revelation Chapter 15

1 Then I saw another awe-inspiring sign in heaven: seven angels holding the seven final plagues, for with these plagues the full measure of God’s wrath is completed.

2 I saw what appeared to be a sea of glass, glowing with fire. Standing upon this sea of glass were those who had triumphed over the beast, his image, his mark, and the number of his name. They held harps given by God.

3 They sang the song of Moses, the servant of God, and the song of the Lamb, proclaiming: "Great and marvelous are Your works, Lord God Almighty! Righteous and true are Your ways, O King of the nations!"

4 Who will not fear you, O Lord, and honor your name? For you alone are holy. All nations will come and bow down in worship before you, for your righteous acts have been revealed.

5 After this, I looked again, and suddenly, the heavenly temple—the tabernacle containing the testimony—was opened before me.

6 Then the seven angels emerged from the temple, carrying the seven plagues. They were dressed in pure, bright linen and wore golden sashes around their chests.

7 Then one of the four living creatures handed to the seven angels seven golden bowls filled with the anger of God, who lives forever and ever.

8 The temple became filled with smoke because of the glory of God and His power, and no one was able to enter the temple until the seven plagues brought by the seven angels were completed.
