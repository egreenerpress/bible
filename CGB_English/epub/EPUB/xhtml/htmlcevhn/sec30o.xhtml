Psalms Chapter 125

1 (A song of ascents.) Those who place their trust in the LORD will be like Mount Zionâ€”unshakable, enduring forever.

2 Just as the mountains surround Jerusalem, so the LORD surrounds his people, both now and forevermore.

3 For the power of the wicked will not remain over the portion of the righteous, so that the righteous will not be driven to act unjustly.

4 Do good, O LORD, to those who are good, and to those who are upright in heart.

5 Concerning those who turn away and follow their own twisted paths, the LORD will lead them away with those who practice wickedness. But peace will rest upon Israel.
