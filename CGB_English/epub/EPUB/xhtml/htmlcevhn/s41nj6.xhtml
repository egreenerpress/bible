Psalms Chapter 24

1 (A Psalm of David.) The earth belongs to the LORD, and everything in it; the world and all who live in it.

2 For He has established it upon the seas and firmly set it upon the waters.

3 Who is worthy to ascend the mountain of the LORD? Who may stand in His sacred presence?

4 The one who has clean hands and a pure heart, who has not devoted themselves to worthless things nor sworn deceitfully.

5 He will receive the LORD's blessing and experience the righteousness that comes from the God who saves him.

6 This is the generation of those who seek Him, who earnestly seek Your presence, O God of Jacob. Selah.

7 Raise up your heads, you gates; be lifted up, you ancient doors, and the King of glory will enter.

8 Who is this King of glory? The LORD, strong and powerful, the LORD, mighty in warfare.

9 Raise up your heads, you gates; be lifted up, you ancient doors, so that the King of glory may enter.
