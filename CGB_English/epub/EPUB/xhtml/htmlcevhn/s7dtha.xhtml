Psalms Chapter 107

1 Oh, give thanks to the LORD, for He is good; His steadfast love endures forever.

2 Let those whom the LORD has redeemed declare it—those he has rescued from the power of the enemy.

3 He brought them together from all the lands—from the east and the west, from the north and the south.

4 They roamed through the wilderness, isolated and without direction; they could find no settled place to call home.

5 Hungry and thirsty, their strength failed within them.

6 Then they called out to the LORD in their time of trouble, and He rescued them from their hardships.

7 He guided them along the right path, so that they could reach a city where they could settle and dwell.

8 If only people would praise the LORD for His steadfast love and for the extraordinary deeds He has done for humanity!

9 For He satisfies the soul that yearns deeply, and He fills the hungry soul with His abundant goodness.

10 Those who dwell in darkness and in the shadow of death, trapped in misery and bound by chains of iron;

11 Because they defied the words of God and rejected the guidance of the Most High.

12 Therefore, He caused their hearts to grow weary through hardship; they stumbled and fell, with no one to come to their aid.

13 Then they called out to the LORD in their time of trouble, and He delivered them from their hardships.

14 He led them out of darkness and the gloom of death, and shattered the chains that bound them.

15 If only people would praise the LORD for His unfailing kindness and for the extraordinary deeds He has done for humanity!

16 For He has shattered the gates of bronze and broken the bars of iron into pieces.

17 Fools suffer affliction because of their rebellious actions and their sins.

18 Their very being detests all kinds of food; they are on the brink of death.

19 Then they call out to the LORD in their time of trouble, and He delivers them from their afflictions.

20 He sent forth his command, and they were healed; he rescued them from the brink of destruction.

21 If only people would praise the LORD for His steadfast love and for the extraordinary deeds He has done for humanity!

22 Let them offer sacrifices of gratitude and proclaim his deeds with joyful celebration.

23 Those who venture out to sea in ships, conducting their work upon the vast and mighty waters;

24 These people witness the mighty acts of the LORD and the extraordinary wonders He performs in the vast depths of the sea.

25 For He gives the command and stirs up the tempestuous wind, causing its waves to rise high.

26 They rise up to the heavens, then descend again to the depths; their spirit is overwhelmed because of their distress.

27 They stagger back and forth, swaying like a drunkard, and are utterly bewildered, with no idea what to do.

28 Then they call out to the LORD in their time of trouble, and He delivers them from their hardships.

29 He quiets the storm, causing its waves to grow calm.

30 Then they rejoice because the storm has calmed; thus, He guides them safely to the harbor they long for.

31 If only people would praise the LORD for His unfailing kindness and for the extraordinary deeds He has done for humanity!

32 Let them also honor him in the gathering of the people, and give him praise in the meeting of the elders.

33 He transforms rivers into barren wastelands, and springs of water into parched, lifeless land.

34 A fertile land turned into a desolate wasteland, because of the evil deeds of those who live in it.

35 He transforms the barren wilderness into pools of water, and the parched land into flowing springs.

36 And there He causes the hungry to settle, so that they may establish a city to live in.

37 Cultivate the fields and plant vineyards, so that they may produce abundant fruit.

38 He blesses them as well, causing them to increase greatly in number, and He does not allow their livestock to diminish.

39 Once more, they are reduced and humbled by oppression, suffering, and grief.

40 He brings disgrace upon rulers and makes them lose their way in a desolate wasteland, where there is no path to follow.

41 Yet He lifts the needy out of their suffering and establishes their households, making them flourish like a flock.

42 The righteous will witness it and be filled with joy, while all wickedness will be silenced, unable to speak.

43 Whoever is wise and takes the time to consider these things will come to understand the steadfast love and faithfulness of the LORD.
