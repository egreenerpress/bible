Psalms Chapter 131

1 (A song of ascents, by David.) Lord, my heart is not proud, nor are my eyes arrogant. I do not concern myself with matters too great or with things too lofty for me to understand.

2 Certainly, I have composed and calmed myself, like a child who has been weaned from its mother; my soul is as content as a weaned child.

3 Let Israel place their trust in the LORD from this time forward and forevermore.
