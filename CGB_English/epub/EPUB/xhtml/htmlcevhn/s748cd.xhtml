Psalms Chapter 114

1 When Israel departed from Egypt, the house of Jacob from a people who spoke a foreign tongue;

2 Judah became His holy dwelling place, and Israel was the realm of His sovereign rule.

3 The sea saw it and retreated; the Jordan River turned back.

4 The mountains leaped like rams, and the small hills bounded like young lambs.

5 What troubled you, O sea, that you fled? O Jordan, that you turned back?

6 You mountains, why did you leap like rams? And you small hills, why did you bound like lambs?

7 Shake with awe, O earth, in the presence of the Lord, in the presence of the God of Jacob.

8 He transformed the rock into a pool of water, the hard stone into a flowing spring.
