Psalms Chapter 87

1 (A psalm or song for the descendants of Korah.) His foundation is established on the sacred mountains.

2 The LORD holds the gates of Zion in greater affection than all the dwellings of Jacob.

3 Magnificent things are proclaimed about you, O city of God. Pause and reflect.

4 I will speak of Rahab and Babylon to those who know me; look, there is Philistia, Tyre, and Cushâ€”this one was born there.

5 And concerning Zion, it will be said, "This one and that one were born in her," for the Most High himself will establish her securely.

6 The LORD will record, when He registers the people, that this man was born there. Selah.

7 Both the singers and the musicians will be present; all my sources of life and joy are found in you.
