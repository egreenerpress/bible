Psalms Chapter 98

1 (A Psalm.) Sing to the LORD a new song, for He has accomplished extraordinary deeds; His right hand and His holy arm have secured victory for Him.

2 The LORD has revealed His deliverance; He has made His righteousness clearly known in the presence of the nations.

3 He has called to mind His steadfast love and faithfulness toward the house of Israel; all the farthest reaches of the earth have witnessed the saving power of our God.

4 Shout joyfully to the LORD, all the earth! Raise your voices, celebrate with gladness, and sing praises to Him.

5 Sing to the LORD with the accompaniment of the harp; with the harp and the sound of a song.

6 With trumpets and the blast of the horn, raise a joyful shout before the LORD, the King.

7 Let the sea thunder and all that fills it; let the earth and all who live upon it resound.

8 Let the rivers resound with applause; let the mountains rejoice together in celebration.

9 In the presence of the LORD, for He is coming to judge the earth. He will judge the world with righteousness and the nations with fairness.
