Psalms Chapter 61

1 (For the music director, to be accompanied by stringed instruments. A psalm of David.) O God, listen to my plea; give your attention to my prayer.

2 From the farthest reaches of the earth, I will call out to you when my heart is overcome with despair. Guide me to the rock that is higher and more secure than I am.

3 For you have been my refuge, a secure fortress against my foes.

4 I will dwell in your sanctuary forever; I will take refuge in the shelter of your wings. Selah.

5 For you, O God, have listened to my vows; you have granted me the inheritance of those who revere your name.
