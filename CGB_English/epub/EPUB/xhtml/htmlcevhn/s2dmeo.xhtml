Job Chapter 25

1 Then Bildad the Shuhite replied and said,

2 Authority and awe belong to Him; He establishes harmony in His heavenly realms.

3 Can anyone count the size of His armies? And is there anyone upon whom His light does not shine?

4 How then can a person be declared righteous before God? Or how can someone born of a woman be considered pure?

5 Look, even the moon lacks brightness, and the stars are not pure in His sight.

6 How much less is a mere human, who is like a worm? And the offspring of humanity, who is no more than a worm?
