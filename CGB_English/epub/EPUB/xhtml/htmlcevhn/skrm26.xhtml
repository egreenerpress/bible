Psalms Chapter 91

1 Whoever lives in the shelter of the Most High will remain secure under the protection of the Almighty.

2 I will declare of the LORD, "He is my shelter and my stronghold, my God; in Him I place my trust."

3 Certainly, He will rescue you from the hunter's trap and from the deadly plague.

4 He will shelter you with his wings, and beneath his protection you will find safety. His faithfulness will be your shield and your defense.

5 You shall not fear the terror that comes by night, nor the arrow that flies by day.

6 Nor for the plague that moves in the shadows, nor for the devastation that strikes at midday.

7 A thousand may fall at your side, and ten thousand at your right hand, but it will not come near you.

8 You will observe with your own eyes and witness the consequences that come upon the wicked.

9 Because you have made the LORD, who is my refuge, even the Most High, your dwelling place;

10 No harm will come to you, and no disaster will approach your home.

11 For He will command His angels concerning you, to guard you in all your paths.

12 They will lift you up in their hands, so that you will not strike your foot against a stone.

13 You will walk upon the lion and the cobra; you will trample the young lion and the serpent underfoot.

14 Because he has devoted his love to me, I will rescue him; I will place him in a position of safety, for he has come to know and trust in my name.

15 When he calls out to me, I will answer him; I will stand by him in times of distress. I will rescue him and bring him honor.

16 I will grant him a long and fulfilling life, and I will reveal to him my deliverance and saving power.
