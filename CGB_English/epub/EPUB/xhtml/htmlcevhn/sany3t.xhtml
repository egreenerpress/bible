Hosea Chapter 4

1 Listen to the word of the LORD, you people of Israel: for the LORD has a case against those who live in the land, because there is no faithfulness, no steadfast love, and no knowledge of God in the land.

2 By making false oaths, lying, murdering, stealing, and committing adultery, they act without restraint, and one act of violence follows another.

3 Therefore, the land will grieve, and all who live in it will waste away—along with the wild animals, the birds of the sky, and even the fish of the sea—they will all perish.

4 Do not let anyone contend or rebuke another, for your people are like those who argue with the priest.

5 Therefore, you will fall during the day, and the prophet will also fall with you during the night. I will bring destruction upon your mother.

6 My people are being destroyed because they lack understanding. Since you have rejected knowledge, I will also reject you from serving as my priest. Because you have forgotten the law of your God, I will also forget your children.

7 As they grew in number, they also increased their sin against me; therefore, I will turn their honor into disgrace.

8 They consume the offerings meant to atone for the sins of my people, and they eagerly desire their wrongdoing.

9 The people and the priests will be alike, for I will hold them both accountable for their actions and repay them according to their deeds.

10 They will eat, but not be satisfied; they will engage in promiscuity, yet not prosper—because they have ceased to pay attention to the LORD.

11 Prostitution, wine, and strong drink corrupt the mind and lead one astray.

12 My people seek guidance from their wooden idols, and their divining rods give them answers. For a spirit of unfaithfulness has led them astray, and they have turned away from their God to pursue falsehood.

13 They offer sacrifices on the mountaintops and burn incense on the hills, beneath oak, poplar, and terebinth trees, because their shade is pleasant. As a result, your daughters will engage in promiscuity, and your brides will commit adultery.

14 I will not punish your daughters when they engage in prostitution, nor your wives when they commit adultery, for the men themselves consort with prostitutes and offer sacrifices with shrine prostitutes. As a result, the people who lack understanding will stumble and fall.

15 Even though you, Israel, act unfaithfully, do not let Judah fall into sin. Do not go to Gilgal or journey to Beth-aven, and do not swear, "As surely as the LORD lives."

16 Israel has turned away like a stubborn heifer; now the Lord will let them wander like a lamb in an open field.

17 Ephraim is deeply attached to idols; leave him to his ways.

18 Their wine has turned sour; they have persistently engaged in immoral acts. Her leaders are deeply devoted to shameful practices—they eagerly embrace disgrace. Give them what they deserve.

19 The wind has carried her away in its force, and they will be disgraced because of their offerings.
