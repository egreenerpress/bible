Leviticus Chapter 18

1 The LORD spoke to Moses, saying,

2 Say to the people of Israel and tell them: I am the LORD your God.

3 Do not follow the practices of the land of Egypt, where you once lived, nor imitate the ways of the land of Canaan, where I am bringing you. You must not conform to their customs or live by their rules.

4 You must follow my decrees and keep my laws, living in accordance with them. I am the LORD your God.

5 You must therefore observe my statutes and my ordinances; if a person follows them, they will live by them. I am the LORD.

6 Do not approach any close relative to engage in sexual relations and expose their nakedness. I am the LORD.

7 You must not expose the nakedness of your father or your mother; she is your mother—you must not uncover her nakedness.

8 You must not expose the nakedness of your father’s wife; it is your father’s own nakedness.

9 You must not expose the private parts of your sister, whether she is the daughter of your father or your mother, whether she was born in your household or elsewhere. You are forbidden to uncover her nakedness.

10 You must not expose the nakedness of your son’s daughter or your daughter’s daughter, for their nakedness is your own.

11 You must not have sexual relations with your father’s wife’s daughter, who is your sister, whether she is the daughter of your father or your mother. She is your sister, and you must not expose her nakedness.

12 You must not have sexual relations with your father’s sister, for she is a close relative of your father.

13 You must not have sexual relations with your mother’s sister, for she is a close relative of your mother.

14 You must not expose the nakedness of your father’s brother; you must not have sexual relations with his wife, for she is your aunt.

15 You must not have sexual relations with your daughter-in-law; she is your son’s wife. You are forbidden to expose her nakedness.

16 You must not have sexual relations with your brother’s wife, for it would dishonor your brother.

17 You must not expose the nakedness of a woman and her daughter, nor shall you take her son’s daughter or her daughter’s daughter to expose their nakedness. They are her close relatives; such an act is depraved.

18 You must not marry a woman and then also take her sister as a wife, causing rivalry between them by exposing the nakedness of one while the other is still alive.

19 You must not have sexual relations with a woman during her menstrual period, when she is ritually unclean.

20 Furthermore, you must not engage in sexual relations with your neighbor's wife, as doing so would defile yourself with her.

21 You must not allow any of your children to be sacrificed by passing them through the fire to Molech, nor shall you dishonor the name of your God. I am the LORD.

22 You must not engage in sexual relations with a man as you would with a woman; such an act is detestable.

23 You must not engage in sexual relations with any animal, as doing so defiles yourself. Likewise, no woman is to present herself to an animal for sexual purposes. Such acts are a perversion and a violation of the natural order.

24 Do not defile yourselves by engaging in any of these practices, for it is through such acts that the nations I am driving out before you have become defiled.

25 The land has become defiled; therefore, I will punish it for its iniquity, and the land itself will expel its inhabitants.

26 You must therefore observe my statutes and my ordinances, and you must not commit any of these detestable acts—neither any of your own people, nor any foreigner living among you.

27 For all these detestable acts have been committed by the people of the land who lived there before you, and the land has become polluted.

28 So that the land does not expel you as well when you defile it, just as it expelled the nations that were there before you.

29 Whoever commits any of these detestable acts—those who do them will be completely cut off from their community.

30 Therefore, you must observe my statutes, so that you do not engage in any of these detestable practices that were carried out before you, and so that you do not defile yourselves by them. I am the LORD your God.
