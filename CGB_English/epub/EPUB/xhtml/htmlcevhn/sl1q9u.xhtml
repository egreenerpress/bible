Ezekiel Chapter 37

1 The power of the LORD came upon me, and by His Spirit, He led me out and brought me to the middle of a valley that was filled with bones.

2 He led me all around them, and I saw that there were very many in the open valley, and indeed, they were completely dry.

3 And He said to me, "Mortal, can these bones live?" I replied, "O Lord God, only You know."

4 Once more, He said to me, "Speak a prophetic message over these bones and declare to them: 'O dry bones, listen to the word of the LORD.'"

5 This is what the Lord GOD says to these bones: "Listen carefully, I will bring breath into you, and you will come to life."

6 I will attach tendons to you, cause flesh to grow upon you, cover you with skin, and put breath within you, so that you will come to life. Then you will know that I am the LORD.

7 So I prophesied as I had been commanded. And as I prophesied, there was a sound, and suddenly a rattling, and the bones came together, each bone joining to its proper place.

8 As I looked, I saw that tendons and flesh began to grow on them, and skin covered them completely. However, there was no breath of life in them.

9 Then He said to me, "Prophesy to the wind, prophesy, son of man, and say to the wind, 'This is what the Lord GOD says: Come from the four winds, O breath, and breathe upon these slain, so that they may live.'"

10 So I prophesied as he instructed me, and the breath entered them, and they came to life, rising to their feetâ€”a vast and mighty army.

11 Then he said to me, "Mortal, these bones represent the entire house of Israel. Listen to what they are saying: 'Our bones are dried up, our hope is gone, and we are completely cut off.'"

12 Therefore, prophesy and say to them, "This is what the Lord GOD declares: Listen, my people, I will open your graves and raise you from them, and I will bring you back to the land of Israel."

13 And you will come to understand that I am the LORD when I open your graves, my people, and raise you up from them.

14 I will put my Spirit within you, and you will come to life. Then I will settle you in your own land, and you will know that I, the LORD, have spoken and have done this, declares the LORD.

15 The message of the LORD came to me once more, saying,

16 Furthermore, son of man, take a single stick and write on it, "For Judah and for the Israelites associated with him." Then take another stick and write on it, "For Joseph, the stick of Ephraim, and for all the house of Israel associated with him."

17 Take the sticks and join them together so that they become one in your hand. They will be united as a single piece, symbolizing their unity.

18 And when the people of your nation come to you and ask, "Will you not explain to us what these things mean?"

19 Tell them this: This is what the Lord GOD says: Listen carefully, I will take the tribe of Joseph, represented by Ephraim, along with the other tribes of Israel associated with him, and I will join them together with the tribe of Judah. I will unite them into one, and they will become a single entity in my hand.

20 The wooden tablets on which you write will be in your hand, visible to them as a sign.

21 And say to them, "This is what the Lord GOD declares: Behold, I will take the people of Israel from among the nations where they have been scattered, and I will gather them from every direction and bring them back to their own land."

22 I will unite them into a single nation in the land, on the mountains of Israel, and one king will rule over all of them. They will no longer be two separate nations, nor will they ever again be divided into two kingdoms.

23 They will no longer defile themselves with their idols, nor with their abominable practices, nor with any of their sins. Instead, I will rescue them from all the places where they have lived and sinned, and I will purify them. Then they will truly be my people, and I will be their God.

24 David, my chosen servant, will reign as their king, and they will all have one shepherd. They will live according to my laws, faithfully obey my decrees, and carry them out.

25 They will live in the land that I have given to Jacob, my servant, where your ancestors lived. They will settle thereâ€”they, their children, and their descendants forever. And my servant David will be their leader forever.

26 Furthermore, I will establish a covenant of peace with them; it will be an everlasting covenant. I will settle them, increase their numbers, and set my sanctuary among them forever.

27 My dwelling place will also be among them; indeed, I will be their God, and they will be my people.

28 Then the nations will know that I, the LORD, set apart Israel as holy, when my sanctuary is established among them forever.
