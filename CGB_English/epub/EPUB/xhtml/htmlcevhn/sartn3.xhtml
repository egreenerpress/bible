Psalms Chapter 85

1 (For the music director, a psalm of the sons of Korah.) Lord, you have shown kindness to your land; you have restored the fortunes of Jacob.

2 You have pardoned the wrongdoing of your people; you have concealed all their sins. Selah.

3 You have removed all your wrath; you have turned away from the intensity of your anger.

4 Turn us back to you, O God who saves us, and put an end to your anger against us.

5 Will you remain angry with us forever? Will your wrath extend to all generations?

6 Will you not restore us once more, so that your people may find joy in you?

7 Show us your mercy, O LORD, and grant us your salvation.

8 I will listen to what God, the LORD, will say, for He will speak peace to His people and to His faithful ones. But they must not return to their foolish ways.

9 Certainly, His deliverance is close to those who honor Him, so that His glorious presence may reside in our land.

10 Compassion and faithfulness come together; justice and peace embrace one another.

11 Truth will rise up from the ground, and righteousness will gaze down from the heavens.

12 Indeed, the LORD will provide what is good, and our land will produce its abundant harvest.

13 Righteousness will lead the way before Him, guiding us to walk in the path of His footsteps.
