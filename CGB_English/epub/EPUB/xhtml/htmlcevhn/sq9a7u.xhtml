Psalms Chapter 99

1 The LORD reigns; let the nations tremble. He is enthroned above the cherubim; let the earth quake.

2 The LORD is greatly exalted in Zion; he is supreme over all the nations.

3 Let them praise your great and awe-inspiring name, for it is holy.

5 Praise the LORD our God, and bow down in reverence before his throne; for he is holy.

6 Moses and Aaron were among His priests, and Samuel was among those who called on His name. They cried out to the LORD, and He answered them.

7 He spoke to them from the pillar of cloud; they obeyed his instructions and the decrees he gave them.

8 You answered them, O LORD our God; you were a God who forgave them, yet you held them accountable for their sinful actions.

9 Praise the LORD our God, and bow down in worship at his sacred mountain; for the LORD our God is holy.
