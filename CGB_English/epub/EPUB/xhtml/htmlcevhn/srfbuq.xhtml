Esther Chapter 10

1 King Ahasuerus imposed a tax on the land and on the coastal regions.

2 All the deeds of his power and his might, along with the account of the greatness of Mordecai, to which the king had elevated him, are they not recorded in the book of the annals of the kings of Media and Persia?

3 Mordecai the Jew held a position of high honor next to King Ahasuerus. He was greatly esteemed among the Jewish people and well-regarded by the multitude of his fellow Jews. He continually sought the welfare and prosperity of his people and spoke words of peace and well-being to all his descendants.
