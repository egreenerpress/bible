Isaiah Chapter 12

1 On that day you will say, "O LORD, I will praise you. Though you were angry with me, your anger has turned away, and you have comforted me."

2 Look, God is my deliverance; I will trust and not be afraid, for the LORD, the Eternal One, is my strength and my song; He has indeed become my salvation.

3 Therefore, you will draw water with gladness from the springs of deliverance.

4 On that day, you will say: "Give thanks to the LORD; call on his name. Proclaim his deeds among the nations; declare that his name is lifted high."

5 Sing to the LORD, for He has accomplished marvelous deeds; this is recognized throughout the entire world.

6 Shout aloud and rejoice, you who dwell in Zion, for the Holy One of Israel is mighty and dwells among you!
