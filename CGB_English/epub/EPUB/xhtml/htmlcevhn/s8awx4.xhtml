Psalms Chapter 88

1 (A song or psalm by the sons of Korah, for the music director, to be sung according to the tune "Mahalath Leannoth." A contemplative song by Heman the Ezrahite.) O LORD, God of my salvation, I have cried out to you day and night.

2 Allow my prayer to reach you; turn your ear and listen to my plea.

3 For my soul is overwhelmed with troubles, and my life is nearing the grave.

4 I am numbered among those who descend to the grave; I am like a man who has no strength left.

5 "Like the slain who lie in the grave, I am set free among the deadâ€”those whom you no longer remember, for they are cut off from your care and protection."

6 You have placed me in the deepest pit, in utter darkness, in the depths of despair.

7 Your anger weighs heavily upon me, and You have overwhelmed me with all Your waves. Selah.

8 You have removed my friends far from me; you have made me repulsive to them. I am confined and unable to break free.

9 My eyes are filled with sorrow because of my suffering. Lord, I have called out to you every day; I have lifted my hands to you in prayer.

10 Will you perform miracles for the dead? Can those who have died rise up and give you praise? Pause and reflect on this.

11 Will your steadfast love be proclaimed in the realm of the dead? Will your faithfulness be declared in the place of destruction?

12 Will your wonders be made known in the realm of darkness? Will your righteousness be revealed in the land of oblivion?

13 But to you, O LORD, I have cried out; and in the morning my prayer will come before you.

14 LORD, why have you abandoned me? Why do you turn your face away and refuse to see me?

15 I have been afflicted and near death since my youth; I endure your terrors and am overwhelmed with distress.

16 Your intense anger overwhelms me; your terrifying judgments have completely overwhelmed and isolated me.

17 They surrounded me every day like a flood; they closed in on me from every side.

18 You have removed my loved ones and friends far from me; those I once knew are now hidden in darkness.
