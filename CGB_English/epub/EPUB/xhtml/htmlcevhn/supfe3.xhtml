Psalms Chapter 93

1 The LORD reigns; He is robed in majesty. The LORD is clothed with power, which He has wrapped around Himself. The world is firmly established; it cannot be shaken.

2 Your throne has been established since ancient times; you are from eternity.

3 The waters have risen, O LORD; the waters have raised their voice; the waters surge with their mighty waves.

4 The LORD, who reigns on high, is more powerful than the roaring of many waters, even mightier than the surging waves of the sea.

5 Your decrees are completely trustworthy; holiness is the fitting adornment for Your house, O LORD, for all time.
