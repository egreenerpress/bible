About This Edition: Covenant of Grace Bible (CGB)

This Ministry Edition of the Covenant of Grace Bible: A Contemporary KJV Translation (CGB) is provided free of charge by eGreener Press to support ministry outreach and to make the Word of God accessible to a wider audience. See the copyright page for important usage details regarding reproduction and distribution.

The CGB Bible, published by eGreener Press (egrpress.com), offers a fresh and readable presentation of the King James Version, aiming for clarity for contemporary readers while honoring the KJV's rich heritage. At eGreener Press (www.egrpress.com), we are committed to publishing resources that serve the Christian community.

We hope this edition of the CGB Bible will be a valuable tool in your ministry and personal study.
